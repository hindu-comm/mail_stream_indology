avattam in the sense you mean is found as avattankaL viLaiyum in Tivviya -
Tiruv y- 10, 3, 9.
It could be a derivative from Skt. a-baddha and in that case mean 1.
Nonsense, falsehood. 2. That which is useless, vain.
A.Veluppillai
