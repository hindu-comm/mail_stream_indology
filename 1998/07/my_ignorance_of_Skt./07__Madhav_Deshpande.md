Gaur bAhIkaH is taken as a derogatory comment meaning:  "The bAhIka (or
bAhlIka, a resident of the northwestern region of this name) is [stupid
like] a bull."  This is often cited as an example of gauNI lakSaNA.

                        Madhav Deshpande
