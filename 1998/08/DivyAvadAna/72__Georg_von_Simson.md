>Is the Potalaka mountain mentioned in the Divya avadAnam?

Edgerton mentions in his Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit Dictionary s.v. potalaka
(2) (n. of a mountain) in addition to GandhavyUha 208.8 only MahAvyutpatti
4154, and not the DivyAvadAna. And s.v. Pautalaka = Po. his only reference
is MahAmAyUrI (ed. Oldenbourg 1899) 253.27 (prose).
