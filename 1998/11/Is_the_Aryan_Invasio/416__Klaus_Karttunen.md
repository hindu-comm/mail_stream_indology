> Can someone please tell me the etymology of "Sutlej"?
> ...

Epic Zatadru, Vedic ZutudrI, Greek Zaradros (in Ptolemy), Latin
Sydrus (Pliny). Note that in Indic z stays for palatal sibilant, in
Greek for the letter zeta. See my India and the Hellenistic World.
Helsinki 1997. p. 116.
