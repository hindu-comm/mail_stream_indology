 The nakSatras common in Tamil literature are:

  --------------------------------------------------
  Name        nakSatra no.           Patron deity
  --------------------------------------------------
  vicAkam         16                 Skanda-Murukan
  pUram           11                 Parvati-imaiyavaL
  paraNi           2                 Durga-koRRavai
  Atirai           6                 'Siva
  ONam            22                 Vishnu/TirumAl
  uttaram         12                 Surya
