> Does anyone know a web site location to download the norman and normyn

As far as I know, the original Norman and Normyn fonts are no longer
available anywhere. However, fully-compatible fonts called Times Norman
are available (and are anyway of higher quality than the originals): go to
http://bombay.oriental.cam.ac.uk and follow the "fonts" link.
