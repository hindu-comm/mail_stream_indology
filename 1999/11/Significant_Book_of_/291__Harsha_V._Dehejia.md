Indologists:

The only book that comes to mind that would qualify as a significant contribution to Indian thought in the 20th century is The Life Divine and Savitri by Sri Aurobindo.

A close second would be The Religion of Man and Gitanjali by Rabindranath Tagore.

Harsha V. Dehejia
