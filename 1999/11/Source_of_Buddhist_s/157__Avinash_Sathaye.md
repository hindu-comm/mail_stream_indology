In my experience, the common term used to denote a "Foundation" is
"pratiShThAna".
The word "SaMsthAna" was used for the various "kingdoms" under the
British Domain - somewhat like a State or a Province. It is also used to
denote an institution in Hindi.

Allen W Thrasher wrote:

> SaMsthAna is pretty common in modern Sanskrit for "foundation;" many
> institutions publishing in Sanskrit include it in their name on the
> Sanskrit title pages.
