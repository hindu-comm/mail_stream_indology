The poet UdhayaNan from Helsinki who has rendered the Finnish
epic Kalevala into Tamil has sung a nice & short poem for today.

  Happy New Millennium
  --------------------

  puttAyiram ANTukaL varuka!
  muttAn2a vAzttukkaL peRuka!
  cittikaL cErntelAm perukap
  pattAyiram varattait taruka!

Happy New Century
-----------------

putiya nURRANTE varuka!
putiya vAzttukkaL taruka!
putiya pAtaikaL teriyap
putiya varaGkaL taruka!

   Happy New Year
   --------------

  IrAyiram ANTE varuka!
  OrAyiram vAzttup peRuka!
  cIrAyiram ciRappup peruka,
  nURAyiram varaGkaL taruka!

Any Sanskrit blessing-cATu on the New Year?
