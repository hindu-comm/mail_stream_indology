>this does

>Purusha. By denying the Samyoga between Prakriti and Purusha, the Samkhya

>merely dispelling the **apparent** admixture of Prakriti and Purusha.

???? In Samkhya, samyoga of purusha and prakRti is not admixture, but
proximity. Samkhya does not deny this samyoga, but invokes it to explain the
manifestation of the unmanifest material nature. This manifestation is held
to be real, so that the samyoga which brings it about also has to be real.
