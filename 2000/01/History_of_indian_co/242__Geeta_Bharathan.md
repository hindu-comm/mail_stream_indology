> When was cumin seed was introduced in India?

According to Achaya (1998), it is mentioned "after 300 BC (Charaka,
Sushrutha, Kautilya) as ajaji, karavi and kuchika. Late Sanskrit has
jeeraka ...from the Persian zira."
