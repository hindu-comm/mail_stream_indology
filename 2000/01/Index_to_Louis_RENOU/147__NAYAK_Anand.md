Dear Indologists,

You know how much the following work is of use for indologists :

Louis RENOU et Jean FILLIOZAT, L'Inde classique. Manuel des études
indiennes, Vols.I et II, Adrien Maisonneuve/Ecole française
d'extrême-orient, Paris, 1985 (réimprssion).

Its only defect is that it does not contain a suitable index and therefore
it takes time to get at the matter. I have tried to prepare an index which
can be of use to those who consult this work. It's a small New Year's gift
to indologists. Download it from :
http://www.unifr.ch/imr/EDFpage_publications.htm
<http://www.unifr.ch/imr/EDFpage_publications.htm> 

All the best. 
Anand Nayak
