The young Vaasudeva was named Puur.napraj;na and later
became Aanandatiirtha (there you have another such name ending:
-tiirtha); he finally gave himself the name 'Madhva' (hence his
followers are known as 'Maadhvas'). He is quite other than
Maadhava.
