"bhAv" is used in Hindi for both "price" and "feeling/emotion".

According to the Oxford Hindi-English dictionary, "bhAv" in
Avadhi and Brijbhasa also has the meaning of "bhA" ( to be liked
or appreciated).

-arun gupta
