The copy in the university library of Tübingen is listed as:

Karmasiddhiprakarana : the treatise on action by Vasubandhu / by Étienne
Lamotte. Engl. transl. by Leo M. Pruden. - Berkeley, Calif. : Asian
Humanities Pr., 1988. - 131 S.; (engl.) 
ISBN 0-89581-907-4 
Original: Karmasiddhiprakarana <engl.> 
Universitätsbibliothek Tübingen 
Signatur: 29 A 19655
