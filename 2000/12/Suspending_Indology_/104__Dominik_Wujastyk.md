To switch off INDOLOGY during the holiday period, go to the listserv
website, either via

        http://www.indology.org.uk (INDOLOGY discussion archive/Join or
        leave the list (or change settings))

or directly at Liverpool

        http://listserv.liv.ac.uk/archives/indology.html (Join or leave
        the list (or change settings))

Once there, you can login and set your subscription to "nomail".  You will
continue to be a member, but you will not receive any messages until you
change the setting again.

To unsubscribe completely from the list, send an email saying

        unsub indology

to the address

        indology-unsubscribe-request at liverpool.ac.uk
