Am Mit, 07 Feb 2001 schrieb N. Ganesan:

> What does Basava mean in KannaDa language?
> Anything to do with light, brightness, etc.?

A tadbhava of Skt. v.r.sabha, "bull". (The religious thinker and
saint Basava / Basava.n.na (12th cent.), who organised the Virasaiva
community, is also considered an incarnation of Nandin.)

Robert Zydenbos
Institut für Indologie und Iranistik
Universität München
