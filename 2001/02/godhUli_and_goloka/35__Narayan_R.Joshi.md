In Maharashtra, the evening hour is called godhUli or goraja hour because
cows return home at that time. For wedding ceremonies, godhUli or goraja
muhUrta is considered auspicious. Anyway thanks to Dr.Ganesan for sharing
with us a nice Tamil verse.Regards N.R.Joshi.
