>Please can someone identify the source of
>
>         satyam. brahmaa / jaganmithya / jivo brahmaiva na para
>
>Many thanks.

The pada "brahma satya.m jagan mithyaa" is the first one in
verse 20 of vivekacuu.daama.ni, attributed to Zankara. The
vedaanta.di.n.dima (author?) has the verse -

brahma satya.m jagan mithyaa jiivo brahmaiva naapara.h |
jiivanmuktastu tadvidvaan iti vedaanta.di.n.dima.h ||

Vidyasankar
