The (rather) comprehensive bibliography of Przyluski's works put
together by Alexander W. MacDonald and Marcelle Lalou mentions only two
articles that deal inter alia with the Bhagavadgita (see A.W.
MacDonald et Marcelle Lalou: L'oeuvre de Jean Przyluski. Paris
1970, No. 40 and No. 114).

Dominik Wujastyk possibly means the monograph of Étienne Lamotte?
That is:
	Lamotte, Étienne: Notes sur la Bhagavadgita. Paris:
	Geuthner, 1929 (Société Belge d'études orientales)
