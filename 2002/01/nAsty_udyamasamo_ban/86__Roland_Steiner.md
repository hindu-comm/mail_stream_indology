The source _is_ Bhart.rhari's "Satakatraya, in Kosambi's edition (Bombay 1948) stanza no. 216 (p. 86). The complete stanza runs as follows:
aalasya.m hi manu.syaa.naa.m "sariirastho mahaaripu.h |
naasty udyamasamo bandhu.h k.rtvaa ya.m naavasiidati ||

See also Ludwik Sternbach's Mahaa-subhaa.sita-sa.mgraha, Vol. III (Hoshiarpur 1977), no. 5297, for further sources and readings.
