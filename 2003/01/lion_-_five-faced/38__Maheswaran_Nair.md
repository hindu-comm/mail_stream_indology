Sabdakalpadruma says-pancam vistrtam ànanam yasya simhah.In the figure of a lion what is comparatively the most prominent and broad is its face.And the least in size is its kati or loin.That is why the loin of beautiful woman is compared with that of lion-mrgaraajakatii.
