An annotated bibliography of Indian medicine

Jan Meulenbeld has just published An annotated bibliography of Indian
medicine on the internet. The address is

        http://www.ub.rug.nl/indianmedicine.

An earlier version of this bibliography was published in his book A
history of Indian medical literature.  At present the bibliography
contains some 10.000 items, which can be accessed by searching or browsing
alphabetically.

Visitors are invited to contribute to this bibliography by adding
information about existing titles and informing the author about recent
appearances.
