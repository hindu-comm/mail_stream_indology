Jonathan:

The closest we get is the sixth kind of son called "apaviddha" --
that is a child who has been abandoned by the parents and then
adopted by someone else. See Manu 9.171. It is not clear from the
texts whether this child is a baby, a toddler, or an older child. The
age is left undefined.
