Jain Studies MA Scholarship 2006

This award is sponsored by the Jain Spirit Magazine. The total value of 
this scholarship is £5000, and it is awarded to students registered at 
SOAS for an MA degree with a major in Jaina Studies. Applications by 
letter, accompanied by a short CV, should be submitted by e-mail to the 
Centre of Jaina Studies at the Department of the Study of Religions at 
SOAS, University of London.
