At the risk of wee pennant waving ... this information can often be
found for more recent publications by following the LoC ToC URL at the
bottom of a Catalogus return. See, for e.g., the Colombia return at:

 http://tinyurl.com/mwolw

The ToC field is found in the USMARC records of many of the
Institutions on the States page at:

 http://indica-et-buddhica.org/catalogus/us.shtml
