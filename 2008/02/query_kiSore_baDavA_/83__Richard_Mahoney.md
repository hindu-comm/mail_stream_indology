For what its worth a quick search of the IeB Philologica version
(http://philologica.indica-et-buddhica.org/) of the Mahābhārata and
Rāmayāṇa turned up the following:

Mahābhārata:

 04,003.003d*0072_001 naduṣṭāś ca bhaviṣyanti kiśorā vaḍavā api

 04,003.003d*0074_001 na māṃ paribhaviṣyanti kiśorā vaḍavās tathā

Rāmayāṇa:

 2,017.009c abhicakrāma saṃhṛṣṭā kiśoraṃ vaḍavā yathā
