SOAS JAINA STUDIES MA SCHOLARSHIP 2009

This award is sponsored by Jain Spirit. The total value of this scholarship
is £5000, and it is awarded to students registered at SOAS for an MA degree
with a major in Jaina Studies. Applications by letter, accompanied by a
short CV, should be submitted by e-mail to the Centre of Jaina Studies at
the Department of the Study of Religions at SOAS, University of London
before September 2009.
