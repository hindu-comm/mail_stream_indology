Author: Suhnu Ram Sharma
Title: Byangsi Grammar and Vocabulary.
Pune: Deccan College Postgraduate Research Institute, 2007, 151 p.,

[linguistic description based on fieldwork]

"Byangsi ... is one of seven Tibeto-Burman languages that are reported by Grierson (1909) as being spoken in the Himalayas and sub-Himalayas between the Gangetic Plain and the Tibetan Plateau, in the areas traditionally known as Kumaa~u and Ga.rhwaal [now part of] Uttaraanchal ... west of Nepaal and to the east of the state of Himaachal Pradesh."
