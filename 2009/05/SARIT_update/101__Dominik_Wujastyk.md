Dear colleagues,

Richard Mahoney and I are pleased to be able to report that the SARIT 
transcript of the Aṣṭāṅgahṛdayasaṁhitā by Vāgbhaṭa has just been released. 
It can be searched alongside Manu and Kauṭalya through the SARIT web 
interface:

  Table of Contents :: Vāgbhaṭa [1997], A machine-readable
  transcription of the Aṣṭāṅgahṛdaya by Vāgbhaṭa :: (INDOLOGY: Resources
  for Indological Scholarship, London, 1997)
  http://sarit.indology.info/newphilo/navigate.pl?indologica.3

or downloaded for off-line use:

  SARIT: Text Archive Downloads
  http://sarit.indology.info/downloads.shtml


We would like to express our gratitude to Prof. R. P. Das and the late 
Prof. Dr Emmerick for inputting this text and making it generally 
available.
