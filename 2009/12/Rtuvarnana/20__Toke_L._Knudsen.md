Dear Venetia,

Inspired by Kālidāsa's Ṛtusaṃhāra, the Indian astronomers also  
wrote poems describing the seasons.  See Bhāskara II's Ṛtuvarṇana  
(part of the Siddhāntaśiromaṇi).  Jñānarāja gives a more  
elaborate poem on the seasons in the Siddhāntasundara.

All  best wishes,
Toke
