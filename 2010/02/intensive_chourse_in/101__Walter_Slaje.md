Dear Colleagues, 

I am pleased to announce that an Intensive Course in Pāli Grammar and Syntax will be held at the Indology Department of Martin Luther University Halle (Saale) from September 6th to 17th, 2010:

http://www.indologie.uni-halle.de/aktuelles/poster_pali.pdf

This course, comprising an introduction into Pāli grammar and syntax as well as the reading of the Pātimokkhasutta and the Kaṅkhāvitaraṇī, will be taught by Dr. Petra Kieffer-Pülz (Weimar).

The medium of teaching will be German.

All those interested in the course are most welcome and kindly asked to register by July 1, 2010.

The timing allows to attend the immediately following German Oriental Studies Congress (DOT) in Marburg (20-24 September).

More details are available on our website: http://www.indologie.uni-halle.de/aktuelles/index.htm

Please do pass on this message to anyone possibly interested.
