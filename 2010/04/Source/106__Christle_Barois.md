Dear Indologists,

Could any one let me know the source of this verse,  found in
Yogaraaja's commentary ad Abhinavagupta's Paramaarthasaara, v.  58?

ekako'ham iti sams.rtau janas traasasaahasarasena khidyate
ekako'ham iti ko'paro'sti me ittham asmi gatabhiir vyavasthita.h

Yograraaja attributes it to the granthakaara, that is, to Abhinavagupta.
