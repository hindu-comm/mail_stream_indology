*Karmendu Shishir Shodhagar (KSS)* is the first international archival
collection of Hindi literary little magazines (*Laghupatrikās*). The project
aims to digitize, catalogue and document the vibrant literary circles of
North India. Unique to the *Shodhagar *is the attempt to retrieve
now-almost-extinct little Hindi literary magazines and journals (*
Laghupatrikās*), currently about 4500 issues, covering the period from 1883
to 1947 and from 1947 to 2009.
