Dear Colleagues,

A DFG funded project in Hamburg, Sonderforschungsbereich 950,
Manuskriptkulturen in Asien, Afrika und Europa (Manuscript cultures in Asia, Africa and Europe), has two position openings that may be of interest for the members of this list.

See

http://www.manuscript-cultures.uni-hamburg.de/sfb/Jobs.html

or

http://www.manuscript-cultures.uni-hamburg.de/sfb/Jobs_e.html

for details. The two positions are:

B 04, "Dividing Texts: Conventions of visual text-organization in North Indian and Nepalese manuscripts up to ca. CE 1300"

and 

C 01, "A Twelfth-Century East Indian Monastic Library and its Fate"

B 04 is a 2/3-time position (intended primarily for Ph.D. candidates) and C 01 is a full-time (Post-doc) position.
