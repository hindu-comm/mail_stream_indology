Dear Dominic,

> How is pra"saantaka rendered in Tibetan translation when it occurs  
> in the Hevajratantra ?

praśāntaka in Hevajratanta I.11.3d has been translated in its literal  
meaning as zhi ba (Snellgrove 1959 II: 41); zhi ba "to become quiet"  
(= Skt. śānta).
