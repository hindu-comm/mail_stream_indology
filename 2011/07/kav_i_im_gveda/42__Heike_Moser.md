Publication announcement:

Köhler, Frank: Kaví im Ṛgveda. Dichtung, Ritual und Schöpfung im
frühvedischen Denken
Aachen: Shaker 2011
ISBN: 978-3-8440-0121-1
Language: German
388 pages

Kaví- ranks among the central concepts in the world view of the early
Vedic poets. This study offers an analysis of this term in the Ṛgveda.
All references of kaví- have been translated and interpreted in order
to obtain a consistent understanding of its meaning.
