Dear James, 

In the epics, the phrase "carācara" (mobile/immobile) is quite common--see for example Bhagavad-gītā 10.39:
yac cāpi sarvabhūtānāṃ bījaṃ tad aham arjuna |
na tad asti vinā yat syān mayā bhūtaṃ carācaram ||


All best wishes,

Adheesh
