The universal Sanskrit dichotomy seems to be moving/nonmoving (carācara, 
sthāvara-jaṅgama, etc). In astrological texts one sometimes sees a 
division into mineral, vegetable and animal (dhātu, mūla, jīva); but I’m 
not sure if that division is indigenous.

Martin Gansten
