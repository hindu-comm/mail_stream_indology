> *Vedic asū'rta-, sū'rta-, asūryá-, asūrá-*
>
> by
>
> *Marcos Albino*
>
> *ABSTRACT*
>
> *In this paper I try to show that Vedic **asū'**rta is an extended
> exocentric compound based on svàr/sū'**r ‘sun’, that it is not a negative
> formation with the “possessive” suffix –ta, and that sū'**rta is a
> decompositional formation. *
>
> *I also propose that Ved. as**ūryá, too, is an extended exocentric
> compound. Further, I propose that Ved. asūré is an “absence” formation of
> the type arajjáu ‘without rope.’*
