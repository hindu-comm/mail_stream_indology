Dear Colleagues,


 I am pleased to announce that an Intensive Course in Pāli (reading matter
for beginners: Sutta texts (Satipaṭṭhāna) and Jātakas) will be held at the
Indology Department of Martin Luther University Halle (Saale) this fall,
from September 2 through September 13, 2013.

The course will be taught by Dr Petra Kieffer-Pülz (Weimar).

The medium of teaching is German.

All those interested in the course are most welcome and are requested to
register by 1 August.

Details are available on our website:
http://www.indologie.uni-halle.de/aktuelles/.

Kindly pass on this message to all potentially interested.

Thanking you,

Walter Slaje
