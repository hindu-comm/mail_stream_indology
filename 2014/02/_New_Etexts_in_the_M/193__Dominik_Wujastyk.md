Dear list members,

The Muktabodha Indological Research Institute has recently added the
following 31 searchable etexts ( 19 of which are unedited manuscripts) to
our digital library of Tantric/Agamic literature at:
http://muktalib5.org/digital_library.htm

We now have over 300 searchable etexts in our library. In the past we have
not announced new additions to the library but we will do so in the future.
