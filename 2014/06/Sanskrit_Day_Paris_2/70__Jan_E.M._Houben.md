Announcement / Reminder



SANSKRIT DAY in Paris on 28 June 2014



Cité Universitaire, 7 (R) Boulevard Jourdan 75014, 15h – 19h



PROGRAMME



15h *Ga**ṇ**e**ś**asarasvat**ī**pr**ā**rthan**ā* Prayer to Gaṇeśa and
Sarasvatī

Welcome by Vasundhara Kavali-Filliozat and Dr. Sanyal



15h15 : *D**ī**podd**ī**panam, **Śāntipāṭhaḥ* Dīpoddīpanam & Śāntipāṭha,
vedic chant by Shravan

*Stotr**āṇ**i* Chant of devotional hymnes by Shreyas & Shravyā



15h30 : *Nr**tyam – Da**śā**vat**ā**r**āḥ* « The ten avatāras of Viṣṇu » by
Tarikavalli (Bharatanāṭyam) and Mahina Khanum (Oḍissi) :

*Vy**ā**khy**ā**nam, nr**tyam – dev**ī**m**ā**h**ā**tmyam* Commentary,
dance to the glory of Devī by Iris Iran Farkhondeh



16h : *Vy**ā**khy**ā**nam – sa**ṁ**skr**tak**ā**vyam* « Sanskrit poetry »
by Sylvain Brocquet



16h30 : *Vr**k**ṣā**ropa**ṇ**ak**ā**ra**ḥ* « The man who planted trees »,
discussion by Gopabandhu Mishra and Pierre-Sylvain Filliozat



16h45 : *Rādhākrṣṇasudhīḥ sadā vijayate* « Glory to Radhakrishnan » poem of
Pandit Kavali, read by Vasundhara & Bhāmatī Filliozat



17h PAUSE



17h15 *Pyāris-nagarī vijayate* « To the glory of Paris » poem by Gopabandhu
Mishra



17h30 *Bharatan**ā**ṭyam – G**ī**tagovinde candanacarcita-* Gītagovinda
« Perfumed by

sandal ... », Bharatanāṭya by Tarikavalli



17h45 *Vy**ā**khy**ā**nam – sa**ṁ**skr**tan**ā**ṭakalak**ṣ**a**ṇ**am*
« Sanskrit theatre » by Sylvain Brocquet



18h00 *Bh**ā**samah**ā**kaviviracita**ṁ** pratim**ā**n**ā**ṭakam* « The
Statues » of Bhāsa, presentation by Charles Malamoud ; read by Jacques
Roussy & Patrick Alguératéguy



*Vandan**ā**rpa**ṇ**a**ṁ** de**ś**asa**ṁ**g**ī**ta**ṁ** ca* Greetings and
chant to the glory of the nation, by Bittoo Banger
