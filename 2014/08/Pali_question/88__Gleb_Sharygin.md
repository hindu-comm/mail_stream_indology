Dear Dr. Walser,

"-tvā" is perfectly grammatical and standard, while "-tva" isn't (must be a
typo. Most PTS editions were made on a basis of only a few MSS.) See
Geiger's "A Pāli grammar", §§ 209-210.

With best regards,
Gleb Sharygin
