awards by Government of India. Thanks to Vidwan Nityanand Misra for
improving the collection of names.

2. Śivakumāra Svāmi: Padma Bhūṣaṇa
3. David Frawley (Vāmadeva Śāstrī): Padma Bhūṣaṇa
4. Svāmī Satyamitrānanda Giri: Padma Bhūṣaṇa
5. Bettina Śāradā Bäumer: Padma Śrī
6. Brahmadeva Śarmā: Padma Śrī
7. Annette Schmiedchen: Padma Śrī
8. Huang Baosheng: Padma Śrī
9. George L. Hart: Padma Śrī
