Probably “vacana” here does not stand for an exact quotation but for an abbreviated statement of Īśopaniṣad 1-2, which contain “kurvann eveha karmāṇi” and “tena tyaktena bhuñjīthāḥ”. Note "ca”.

Best wishes.

ashok
