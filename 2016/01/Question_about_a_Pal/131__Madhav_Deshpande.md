Thanks to several colleagues who responded to my question.  In spite of
irregular phonetic connection between Sanskrit Maitrāyaṇī and Pali Mantānī,
it is now clear that Sanskrit Buddhist texts use the expression
Maitrāyaṇīputra matching the Pali Mantānīputta.

Madhav Deshpande
