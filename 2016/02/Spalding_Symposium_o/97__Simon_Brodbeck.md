Dear colleagues,

Registration is now open for the 41st Spalding Symposium on Indian Religions, to be held in Cardiff, 15th - 17th April 2016.

The theme this year is "narrative".

The programme and registration form are available here, on the Spalding Symposium's website: http://spaldingsymposium.org/

The symposium will be held at St Michael's College in Llandaff (a northern suburb of Cardiff). The St Michael's College website is here (see at bottom right for directions): http://stmichaels.ac.uk/

With all best wishes, from Simon Brodbeck and James Hegarty.
