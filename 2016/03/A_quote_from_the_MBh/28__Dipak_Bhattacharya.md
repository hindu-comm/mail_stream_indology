One may compare

*na puragrāmadurgāṇi na cāyudhdharā narāḥ/ kṣayātiśayaduḥkhaṃ ca
nārthaśāstrasya cādaraḥ// dharmaikavāsanā lokāḥ pṛthau rājyaṁ praśāsati*/
Matsya Pur. 10 31-32

This occurs in the story of Pṛthu and Veṇa
