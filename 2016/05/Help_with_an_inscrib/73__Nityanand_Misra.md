I concur with Prof. Olivelle, it seems to be of Jain origins. While not all
characters can be made out, but one can note something like
श्रीश्रीश्रीरत्नज्ञार[?]वरसूरि in the third and second lines from
bottom. श्रीश्रीश्री and सूरि would indicate reference to a Jain saint.
