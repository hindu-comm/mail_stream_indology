Dear list,

Can somebody help trace the citation for ‘kusūla’ with the dental consonant
s (sakāra)?

vācaspatya says
kusūla
kusūla¦ pu0 kusa bā0 kūlacu . kuśūlaśabdārthe bharataḥ .


śabdakalpadruma says
kusūlaḥ
kusūlaḥ, puṃ, (kus + kūlac . yadvā kuśūlaḥ nipātanāt satvam .) kuśūlaḥ .
iti kaṭaśabdārthe bharataḥ

Which is this work referred to as bharataḥ? Does anybody have a digital
copy or a print copy to help with the precise quotation?

Thanks, Nityanand
