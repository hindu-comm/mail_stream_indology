For almost a year the current PDF file listing more than 650 journals
containing Buddhist Studies material has been available here:

  Journals with Buddhist Studies material (PDF)
  http://indica-et-buddhica.org/indica-et-buddhica-tabulae-current.pdf

>From now on this material will also be available in file formats
suitable for e-book readers, Adobe Digital Editions, Kindle, Calibre
&c.:

  Journals with Buddhist Studies material (EPUB)
  http://indica-et-buddhica.org/indica-et-buddhica-tabulae-current.epub

  Journals with Buddhist Studies material (MOBI)
  http://indica-et-buddhica.org/indica-et-buddhica-tabulae-current.mobi


Each of the titles in these files link directly to bibliographic
details on the Indica et Buddhica site, e.g.:

  Journal of the American Oriental Society
  http://indica-et-buddhica.org/tabulae/j/journal-of-the-american-oriental-society


Comments, corrections and additions are most welcome.
