Many thanks to all who responded so quickly to my query concerning the
online version of Monier-Williams’ Sanskrit-English dictionary. Apparently
the link from the index site of dictionaries at
http://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/ leads to version of the
dictionary that does not work. The version available at
http://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/scans/MW72Scan/2014/web/webtc/indexcaller.php,
to which François Patte kindly drew my attention, works perfectly well.



Best wishes,
