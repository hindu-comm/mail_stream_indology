reference to hand -- Brahmasūtrabhāṣya perhaps?): 
nityānitya-vastu-viveka, ihāmutra-phala-bhoga-vairāgya, ṣaṭ-sampatti, 
and mumukṣutva. The six treasures are śama, dama, uparati, titkṣā, 
śraddhā, samādhāna.
