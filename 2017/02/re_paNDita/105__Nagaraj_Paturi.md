Bhasa's Pratimaa naaTakam  in Sixth Anka has the following line where
paanDityam is used not in the sense of scholarship or quality of being
learned:

अस्ति सम्यग् पांडित्यं विचारयितुम् । अथ विदितमेतद् गुरुजनस्य ।
