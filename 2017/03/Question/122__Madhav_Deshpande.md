On another discussion group, Shri Ujjwal Rajput cited the following verse:

सिध्यन्ति कर्मसु महत्स्वपि यन्नियोज्याः संभावनागुणमवेहि तमीश्वराणाम्।
किं वा *भवेत्स्म* तमसामरुणो विभेत्ता तं चेत्सहस्रकिरणो धुरि न *स्म कुर्यात्*
॥

This combination of "sma" with optative forms is very rare.  Are there
other examples of this usage, and any discussion available about it?

Madhav Deshpande
Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
