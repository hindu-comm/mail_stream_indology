To the best of my knowledge, krūra[graha] is never used as a proper name 
or exclusive epithet of one planet/graha, but only as a synonym of 
pāpa/aśubha/asaumya/etc., a category that always includes Mars and 
Saturn, and often the sun, Rāhu and Ketu.
