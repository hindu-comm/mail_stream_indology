Dear list,


We are pleased to announce that Amrita Darshanam — International Centre for
Spiritual Studies — is organizing a one-day workshop on ‘Philosophy of
Language: Reading of the Brahmakāṇḍa of Bhartṛhari’s Vākyapadīya’ on 16th
November 2017 in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru.


Prof. Ashok Akjulkar <http://asia.ubc.ca/persons/ashok-aklujkar/> (
Professor *Emeritus*, University of British Columbia) will be conducting
the sessions.


The workshop is open to all interested. Kindly refer to the attached
brochure for further information.
