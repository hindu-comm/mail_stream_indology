in the Malayalam intensive course for beginners at Tübingen still seats are available:

Malayāḷam – Course for Beginners
12.-16.2. and 19.-23.2.2018

Prof. Dr. Heike Oberlin
Venue: Institute of Asian and Oriental Studies, Dept. of Indology Keplerstr. 2, 72074 Tuebingen, room 002
Time: Both weeks: Monday – Friday, daily 10:00-12:30 and 14:30-17:00 h
Fees:
For students of the University of Tuebingen: both weeks 15 € (copying costs for teaching materials)
Others: per week 60 € (including copying costs for teaching materials)
Registration: sarah.merkle-schneider at uni-tuebingen.de <mailto:sarah.merkle-schneider at uni-tuebingen.de>
Credit points / ECTS: Each of the two weeks is equivalent to a lecture with 2 hours per week. For students of other departments or universities: The count of credit points must be fixed individually with the respective department or home university.
