We are extremely pleased to announce that Amrita Darshanam (ICSS), Amrita
Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru, will organize a two-day workshop titled,
"Glimpses of Indian Mathematics".

Prof. K. Ramasubramanian (Professor, Cell for Indian Science and Technology
in Sanskrit, IIT Bombay) will be conducting the sessions. Details are:

*Date*: 3-4 March, 2018
*Venue*: Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore.

Kindly refer to the brochure attached for further information or visit:
https://www.amrita.edu/event/workshop-glimpses-indian-mathematics/register
