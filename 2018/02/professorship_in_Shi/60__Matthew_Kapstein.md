Please note that the Ecole Pratique des Hautes Etudes has recently opened a search for a

Directeur d'études - equivalent to a tenured professorship - in Indian Religions with a specialty in Shivaism:


EPHE, Section des Sciences religieuses :

https://www.ephe.fr/actualites/recrutement_des_enseignants-chercheurs_2018/sr-profils-postes-dossier-candidature-2018.pdf

DE n° 5155 - Religions de l'Inde : études śivaïtes / Religions of India: Shaivite Studies

The application may be prepared in English, though the language of instruction is French.


Matthew Kapstein
Directeur d'études,
Ecole Pratique des Hautes Etudes

Numata Visiting Professor of Buddhist Studies,
The University of Chicago
