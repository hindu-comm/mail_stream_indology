One more verse for Mādhava this evening:

विपिने मृगीं भ्रमन्तीमवलोक्य कुतोऽनुधावसि मुकुन्द ।
हरिणाक्षी तव निकटे, माधव मा धाव मा धाव ।।

Having seen a female deer wandering in the forest, O Mukunda, why are
following her. The Deer-Eyed [Rādhā] is right next to you. O Mādhava, stop
running [mā dhāva], stop running [mā dhāva].

Madhav Deshpande
Campbell, California
