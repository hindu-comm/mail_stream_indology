Dear Colleagues,

I would like to point out two new publications in the Indica et  
Tibetica-Verlag.


1.
Āyurveda und Poesie
Lolimbarājas Lehrgedicht »Leben des Arztes« (Vaidyajīvana)

Herausgegeben und übersetzt von Jürgen Hanneder und Thomas Schäfer.
Marburg: Indica et Tibetica Verlag 2018. 113 pp. (Broschiert)
ISBN 978-3-923776-57-3
Price:  18,00 €

A collection of Āyurvedic recepies, but written as a Kāvya and  
directed to the wife of the author. Re-edited Sanskrit text with  
German translation, Index of substances etc.

2.
Subhūticandra's Kavikāmadhenu on Amarakośa 1.4.8cd–2.2.5ab
Together with Si tu Paṇ chen's Tibetan translation
(Indica et Tibetica 56)

Edited and introduced by Lata Mahesh Deokar.
Marburg: Indica et Tibetica Verlag 2018. xiii, 740 pp. (Hardcover)
ISBN 978-3-923776-58-0
Price:  48,00 €

First Critical Edition of the Text and Tibetan Translation of this  
elaborate commentary on the Amarakośa.

The first volume was IeT 55 (2014).

For details and previews see  http://www.iet-verlag.de

Best regards,

Jürgen Hanneder
