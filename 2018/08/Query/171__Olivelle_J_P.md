In one of his Japanese papers, A. Kanazawa, the compiler of Word-Index to Kumārila's Ślokavārttika, collects the quotations of this verse, most probably a fragment of the Bṛhaṭṭīkā, from the following texts (according to the order of their appearance in the paper):

TnR    Tantrarahasya
PrPn    Prakaraṇapañcikā
NMn    Nyāyamañjarī
NVTT    Nyāyavārttikatātparyaṭīkā
JSAS    Jaiminīyasūtrārthasaṅgraha
RS    Ratnasārīṇī
Pra    Prabhāvalī

I am attaching a pdf of this paper,
Kanazawa, Atsushi. “Kumārila no mono to kangae rareru ichi shisetsu ni tsuite” (A note on a quoted verse attributed to Kumārila). Tōhōgaku (Eastern Studies) no. 81, 1991, pp. 129–143.
