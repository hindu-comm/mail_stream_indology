Thanks, Prasad Ji,

     I had that verse on मत्कुणशङ्का in the back of my mind.  My first
verse is a twist on the old verse अशनं मे वसनं मे जाया मे बन्धुवर्गो मे ।
इति मे मे कुर्वाणं कालवृको हन्ति पुरुषाजम् ।।.  With best wishes.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
