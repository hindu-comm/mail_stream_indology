The following edited volume was just published by Vienna University Press:



Karl Baier, Philipp A. Maas and Karin Preisendanz. *Yoga in Transformation*:
*Historical and Contemporary Perspectives.* Vienna: Vienna University
Press, 2018 (Vienna Forum for Theology and the Study of Religions 16). 630
p., ISBN Print: 9783847108627 – ISBN E-Lib: 9783737008624. 85,- €.
