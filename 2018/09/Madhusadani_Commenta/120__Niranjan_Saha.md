Dear Krishna Prasadji,

I can't say about the critical edition of MST, but the following editions
are available:


1*. Śivamahimnaḥstotra* (1996), *Śivamahimnaḥstotram of Puśpadantācārya
with the commentaries ‘Madhusūdanī’ and ‘Pa**ñ**camukhī’*, ed. Karuṇāpati
Tripāthī, Varanasi: Sampuranand Sanskrit University.

2.  Śarmā, Ralārāma (trans. into Hindi) (2001) *Mahimnastotram
Śrīmadhusūdanīṭīkā-tadāryabhāṣānuvādasahitam*, Vārāṇasī: Caukhambā
Vidyābhavana (reprint).
