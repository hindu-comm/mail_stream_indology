First, a small correction in the text : it is "yasyaiva" not "yasya"

*yasyaiva* sphuraṇaṁ sadātmakamasatkalpārthagam bhāsate

sākṣāttattvamasīti vedavacasā yo bodhayatyāśritān |

yatsākṣātkaraṇādbhavenna punarāvṛttirbhavāmbhonidhau

tasmai śrīgurumūrtaye nama idaṁ śrīdakṣiṇāmūrtaye || 3 ||


pratyagabhinnasya is pratyak + abhinnasya = of the one that is
non-different from the pratyak.

Pratyaktattva refers to Brahman.
