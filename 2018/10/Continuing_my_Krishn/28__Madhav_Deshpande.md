Continuing my Krishna verses:

पाथेयं तव सान्निध्यं किमन्यत्सविधे मम ।
सदा त्वयि समीपस्थे सर्वदे नान्यदीप्सितम् ।।५४५।।
Your company is the provision for my journey. What else do I have? With
you, the provider of all, always being with me, I don’t need anything else.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
