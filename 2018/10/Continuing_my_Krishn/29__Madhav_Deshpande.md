Continuing my Krishna verses:

कृष्ण त्वामनुगच्छामि करिष्ये वचनं तव ।
त्वां विनास्ति न मे कश्चिज्जगत्यां पथदर्शक: ।।५४६।।
O Krishna, I follow you, and I will carry out your words. I do not have
anyone else in the world to show me the path.

Madhav M. Deshpande
