Continuing my Krishna verses

शीतांशुरिव कृष्ण त्वमुदेषि मम मानसे ।
तव माधुर्यपूरेण निरुद्धाश्चित्तवृत्तय: ।।५५९।।
O Krishna, you rise in my mind like the moon with cool rays. With the flood
of your sweetness, the modulations of my mind have been arrested.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
