Dear list members,

On behalf of Prof. Taiken Kyuma at Mie University, I am pleased to 
announce the kick-off symposium of his new project "Comprehensive 
Studies of Indian Buddhist Monasteries from the Gupta Period Onward 
(Vihāra project)." Please see the pdf flyer attached to this email.

Dates: Nov . 2 (Fri.) – 4 (Sun.), 2018
Venue: Institute for Advanced Studies on Asia, The University of Tokyo, 
Main Conference Room (3rd Floor). URL: 
http://www.ioc.u-tokyo.ac.jp/access/index.html
*Nov. 2: semi-closed; Nov. 3: semi-closed (morning) and open 
(afternoon); Nov.4: semi-closed

If you are interested in the semi-closed sessions on Nov. 2 and 4, 
please contact Prof. Kyuma (kyuma at human.mie-u.ac.jp).

Best regards,

Ryugen Tanemura
