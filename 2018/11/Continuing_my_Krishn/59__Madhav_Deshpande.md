Continuing my Krishna verses:

बिभर्ति बालरूपं यो भूतानामीश्वरोऽपि सन् ।
बालानां सुखबोधाय तं वन्दे व्रजनन्दनम् ।।५६७।।
I offer my obeisance to Krishna, the Delight of Vraja, who while being the
lord of all beings assumes the form of a child for the easy comprehension
of the young.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
