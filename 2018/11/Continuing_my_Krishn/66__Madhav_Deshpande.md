Continuing my Krishna verses:

मयूरपिच्छमालोक्य तव शीर्षविभूषणम् ।
जानामि सत्वरं कृष्ण सर्ववर्णमयो हरि: ।।५७३।।
Looking at the peacock feather, your head ornament, O Krishna, I know right
away that all colors are contained in you.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
