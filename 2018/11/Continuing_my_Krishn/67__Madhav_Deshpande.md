Continuing my Krishna verses:

नास्ति वर्णो जगत्यत्र यो न ते शीर्षभूषणे ।
नाथस्त्वं सर्वलोकानामवर्णो वर्णकृद् हरे ।।५७४।।
There is no color in the world that is not in your head ornament [peacock
feather]. O Lord of the whole world, though you are truly colorless, you
are the creator of all colors.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
