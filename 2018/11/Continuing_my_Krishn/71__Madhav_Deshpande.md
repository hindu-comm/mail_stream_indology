Continuing my Krishna verses:

कथयामि कथं शब्दै: कीदृशो मे सखा हरि: ।
अगण्यगुणसम्पन्न: सुहृदां स हृदि स्थित: ।।५७८।।
How do I describe in words the nature of my friend, Krishna? Endowed with
countless qualities, he resides in the heart of his friends.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
