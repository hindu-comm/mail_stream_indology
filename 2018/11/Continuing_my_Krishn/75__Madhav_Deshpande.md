Continuing my Krishna verses

कृष्ण त्वामेव पश्यामि रूपे रूपे प्रतिष्ठितम् ।
अन्तर्बहिश्च सर्वाणि तव रूपाणि माधव ।।५८२।।
O Krishna, established in every form, I only see you. O Madhava, inside and
outside, all forms are your forms.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
