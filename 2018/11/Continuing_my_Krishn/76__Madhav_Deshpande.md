Continuing my Krishna verses:

कालव्ययो मुधा तेऽयं रूपारूपविचारणे ।
अरूपोऽयं सरूपोऽयं सुरूपश्च जनार्दन: ।।५८३।।
Wasted is your time in figuring out if He has a form or not. Krishna is
formless, has forms and is handsome too.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
