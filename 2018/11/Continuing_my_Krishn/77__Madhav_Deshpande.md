Continuing my Krishna verses

विरामे सर्वरूपाणां कृष्ण एवावशिष्यते ।
अरूपे सर्वरूपाणि प्रतीयन्ते जनार्दने ।।५८३।।
When all forms come to an end, only Krishna remains. All forms appear in
the formless Krishna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
