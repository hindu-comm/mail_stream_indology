Continuing my Krishna verses

यतो रूपाणि जायन्ते यस्मिन् जीवन्ति सर्वदा ।
यस्मिंश्च विलयं यान्ति सोऽयं रूपाधिपो हरि: ।।५८४।।
Krishna is the lord of all forms. Forms are born from him, they always live
in him and they merge into him.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
