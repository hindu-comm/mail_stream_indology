The first work is the Prākr̥tapiṅgala (or Prākr̥tapaiṅgala), which is
available in a very nice edition by Bhola Shankar Vyas from 2007. It is a
work of Chandaḥśāstra, although its language is generally more Apabhramsha
than Prakrit.
