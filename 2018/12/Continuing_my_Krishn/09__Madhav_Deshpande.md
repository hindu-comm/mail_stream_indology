पाण्डुरङ्ग इति ख्यात: पण्ढरीनगरे हरे ।
नाम्नैव पाण्डुरत्वं ते कृष्णत्वं तव शाश्वतम् ।।५८७।।
In the city of Pandharpur, you are known by the name Pāṇḍuraṅga "One with a
White Body."  However, your whiteness is only in name.  Your dark hue is
permanent.
