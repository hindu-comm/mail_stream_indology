Continuing my Krishna verses

अरूपस्तत्त्वत: कृष्णो रूपवान् यमुनातटे ।
प्रीत्यर्थं सर्वलोकस्य जनिं लेभे दयार्णव: ।।५९३।।
In reality, Krishna is formless, but the compassionate Lord assumed a form
on the bank of Yamuna, born to please the whole world.

Madhav M. Deshpande
