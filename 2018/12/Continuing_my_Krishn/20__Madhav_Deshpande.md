Continuing my Krishna verses

लोभयन् जनचित्तानि दूरं नयति पापकात् ।
हरिर्हरति सर्वेषां हरणीयं जनार्दन: ।।५९६।।
Attracting the minds of the people, he takes them away from evil. The Lord
takes away from everyone all that needs to be taken away.

Madhav M. Deshpande

University of Michigan
