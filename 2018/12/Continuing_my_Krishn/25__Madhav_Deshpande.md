Continuing my Krishna verses

मा गमो रसिकान्यत्र रसपानाय कुत्रचित् ।
रसपूरो वहत्यत्र सर्वदा कृष्णनिर्झरे ।।६००।।
O Seeker of Delight, do not go anywhere else to savor delight. In this
stream of Krishna, there is a continuous flow of delight.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
