Continuing my Krishna verses

रसाप्लुत: सखायं मे रसेनाप्लावयन् जगत् ।
सर्वमेकरसं कुर्वन् जयत्येष रसेश्वर: ।।६०१।।
This friend of mind is filled with delight and he fills the world with
delight. Uniting everything in his singular joy, this Lord of Delight is
victorious.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
