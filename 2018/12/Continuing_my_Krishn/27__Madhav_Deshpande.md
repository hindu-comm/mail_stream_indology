Continuing my Krishna verses

न केवलं राधिकाया गोपगोपीकुलस्य वा ।
सर्वेषामेव भक्तानां गोविन्दो रसपायक: ।।६०२।।
Krishna offers his delight not only to Radhika or to the cowherd family in
Gokula, but to all those who love him.
