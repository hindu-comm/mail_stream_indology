Continuing my Krishna verses

ग्रीष्मे त्वं शीतल: कृष्ण उष्णोऽसि शिशिरे हरे ।
पक्वाम्रफलवत् कृष्ण मधुरस्त्वमसि सर्वदा ।।६०५।।
O Krishna, you are cool in the heat of summer and warm in the cold winter
months. Like a ripe mango, you are always sweet.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
