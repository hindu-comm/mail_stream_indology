Continuing my Krishna verses

तस्यैवांशं जीवलोके जीवभूतं सनातनम् ।
माधवो माधवं प्रीत्या परिष्वजति सर्वदा ।।६१०।।
Madhava-Krishna always lovingly embraces Madhava who is an eternal living
part of Him in the world.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
