Continuing my Krishna verses

वर्षं वर्षं नवं वर्षं वेणुना मधुरेण ते ।
दधासि नवतां कृष्ण जगत्यां त्वं क्षणे क्षणे ।।६१३।।
O Krishna, by constantly showering new showers with your sweet flute you
instill newness in this world every moment.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
