Continuing my Krishna verses

अद्वैतं न विजानामि न वा द्वैतं जनार्दन ।
मनो मे त्वयि संलग्नं यथासि त्वं प्रियोऽसि मे ।।६१५।।
O Krishna, I don't understand duality or non-duality. My mind is attached
to you, and I love you just as you are.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
