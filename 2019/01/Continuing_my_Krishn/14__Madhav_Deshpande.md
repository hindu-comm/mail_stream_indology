Continuing my Krishna verses

स्वप्नेऽपि चेल्लभेयाहं नन्दनन्दनदर्शनम् ।
सुखदु:खानुविद्धस्य नास्ति मे जाग्रतो भयम् ।।६१८।।
If I get to see Krishna, the Son of Nanda, even in a dream, I will have no
fear of the waking permeated with happiness and sorrow.
Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
