Continuing my Krishna verses

माधवेन सहायाति मापि प्रीता गृहं मम ।
माधवो न बहिर्याति मापि याति न कुत्रचित् ।।६२२।।
Along with Madhava, [his consort] Mā [= Lakṣmī, prosperity] is also happy
to come to my home. [Once they arrive], Mādhava does not leave, nor does Mā
go anywhere.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
