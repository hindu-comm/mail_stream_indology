Continuing my Krishna verses

यथानेकै: स्वरूपैस्त्वं मङ्गलं कुरुषे सदा ।
तथानेकै: स्वरूपैर्मा जगन्माता सुखप्रदा ।।६२६।।
Just as you promote our well-being with your many forms, so does Lakshmi,
the mother of the world, offers us happiness with her many forms

Madhav M. Deshpande
