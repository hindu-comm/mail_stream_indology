Continuing my Krishna verses

उमा च मा च रूपे द्वे नानेका तत्र देवता ।
नामरूपविभेदेऽपि समानं हृदयं तयो: ।।६३१।।
Umā [Pārvatī] and Mā [Lakṣmī] are two forms, but there are no different
deities. Though they have different forms and names, they have the same
heart.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
