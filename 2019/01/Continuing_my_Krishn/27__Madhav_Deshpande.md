Continuing my Krishna verses

उमाधवो माधवो वा सदा न: सम्मतावुभौ ।
नामरूपविभेदेऽपि समानं हृदयं तयो: ।।६३२।।
Śiva, the Lord of Umā, and Viṣṇu, the Lord of Mā, are both always
acceptable to us. Though they have different forms and names, they have the
same heart.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
