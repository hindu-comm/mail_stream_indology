Continuing my Krishna verses

द्वैताद्वैतविवादानां नादिर्नान्त: कदाचन ।
नन्दतां परित: कृष्णं विवादो नास्ति कश्चन ।।६३४।।
The disputes over Identity and Difference have no beginning nor any end.
For those who delight around Krishna, there is no dispute whatsoever.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
