Continuing my Krishna verses

नृत्यन्तं कृष्णमालोक्य वादिनो भावनिर्भरा: ।
सर्ववादान् परित्यज्य शरणं यान्ति माधवम् ।।६३७।।
Seeing the dancing Krishna, the disputants overcome with emotion give up
all disputes and seek refuge in Krishna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
