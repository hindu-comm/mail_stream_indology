Continuing my Krishna verses

हृदये यदि गोविन्द: कुत्रान्यत्र व्रजाम्यहम् ।
सकलं गोकुलं तत्र यत्र नन्दति माधव: ।।६४२।।
If Krishna is in my heart, where else do I need to go? The entire Gokula is
where Krishna delights.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
