Continuing my Krishna verses

यत्राहं तत्र गोविन्दो यत्रासौ तत्र राधिका ।
यशोदा कथमन्यत्र गोकुलं परितो हि माम् ।।६४५।।
Krishna is where I am, and Radhika is where he is. How would the loving
mother Yashoda be elsewhere? The entire Gokula is just around me.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
