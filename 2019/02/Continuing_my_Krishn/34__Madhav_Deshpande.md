Continuing my Krishna verses

तव वेणुध्वनि: कृष्ण निपतेद् हृदये यदि ।
कथं स्यादन्यशब्देषु रतिर्मे मधुसूदन ।।६४७।।
O Krishna, if the sound of your flute will reach my heart, how would I
rejoice in other sounds?

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
