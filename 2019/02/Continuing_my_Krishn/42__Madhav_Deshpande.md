Continuing my Krishna verses

चलनं तव गोविन्द चालनं जगतो गते: ।
विश्रान्तिस्तव गोविन्द विश्राम: सुहृदां हृदि ।।६५३।।
O Krishna, your movement sets in motion the movement of the world, and when
you rest, there is rest in the hearts of your friends.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
