Continuing my Krishna verses

निष्कास्य कालियं येन यमुना निर्विषीकृता ।
तेन नृत्यन्ति सानन्दं कालिन्दीजलवीचय: ।।६५९।।
The waves of Yamuna dance with delight with Krishna who exiled [the
poisonous dragon] Kaliya and removed the poison from the waters of Yamuna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
