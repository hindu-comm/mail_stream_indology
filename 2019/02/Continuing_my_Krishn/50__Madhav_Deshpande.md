Continuing my Krishna verses

गोविन्दपादविन्यासैरमन्दानन्दतुन्दिला: ।
गायन्ति जयगीतानि कालिन्दीजलवीचय: ।।६६१।।
Excited with immense delight due to the footsteps of Krishna, the waves of
Yamuna sing songs of his victory [over the dragon Kaliya].

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
