Continuing my Krishna verses

उच्छलन्ति प्रमोदेन वीचयो यमुनाजले ।
वहन्ति माधवामोदं प्रसरन्त: समीरणा: ।।६६३।।
In the water of Yamuna, the waves surge in delight, and the blowing winds
carry the fragrance of Krishna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
