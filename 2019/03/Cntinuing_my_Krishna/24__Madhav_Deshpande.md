Continuing my Krishna verses

स्फुरन्ति सर्वरूपाणि कृष्णरूपाद् हि केवलात् ।
विस्फुलिङ्गा विभासन्ते वह्नेस्तं प्रतियन्ति च ।।६९६।।
All forms emerge only from the form of Krishna. Sparks appear from the fire
and return to it.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
