Continuing my Krishna verses

अपश्यं तस्य कृष्णस्य मुखं चन्द्रनिभं निशि ।
सूर्यप्रभं दिवा किं मे द्रष्टव्यमवशिष्यते ।।६७२।।
I saw the face of Krishna, like the moon at night and like the sun during
day. What else is worth seeing for me?

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
