Continuing my Krishna verses

पट्टेनावृत्य नयनं ग्रहीतुं मधुसूदनम् ।
यमुनातटलीलायां गोपा धावन्ति सर्वत: ।।६७९।।
During the games on the bank of Yamuna, covering their eyes with bands, the
cowherds run all around to catch Krishna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
