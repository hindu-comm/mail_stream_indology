My Krishna verse on my birthday

यद्यपि वयसा वृद्धो मनस: शिशुता न याति गोविन्द ।
असमर्थौ मे पादौ तथापि मां कृष्ण नर्तयसि ।।
O Krishna, though I have grown in age, the childishness in my mind does not
go away. Even though my legs are not able, you still make me dance.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
