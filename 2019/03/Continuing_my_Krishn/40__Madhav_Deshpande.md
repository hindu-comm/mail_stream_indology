Continuing my Krishna verses

अज्ञानेनावृतं चक्षुरस्माकं मधुसूदन ।
भ्रमन्तस्त्वां वयं कृष्ण ग्रहीष्याम: कथं वद ।।६८२।।
O Krishna, our eyes are covered with ignorance. Please tell us how can we
catch you while we are wandering.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
