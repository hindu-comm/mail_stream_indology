Continuing my Krishna verses

अगृहीतो मया कृष्ण सखे गृह्णासि मां यदि ।
गृहीते मयि कृष्णेन का मे चिन्तावशिष्यते ।।६८४।।
O Krishna, My Friend, if I don't catch you, but if you catch me, what worry
will remain for me after being caught by you?

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
