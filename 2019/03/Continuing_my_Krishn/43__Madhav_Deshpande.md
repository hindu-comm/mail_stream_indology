Continuing my Krishna verses

खेलन्तस्तव लीलायां गृहीता: स्मो वयं त्वया ।
क इच्छेद् वद गोविन्द मुक्तिं ते प्रेमबन्धनात् ।।६८५।।
Playing in your game, you have caught us all. Tell me, O Krishna, who would
want to be released from the bond of your love.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
