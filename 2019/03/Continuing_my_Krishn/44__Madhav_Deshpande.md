Continuing my Krishna verses

निर्माय चतुरो वर्णान् न्यषिञ्चज्जगतीतले ।
चतुर्भिर्वर्णितो वर्णैर्होलीं खेलति माधव: ।।६८६।।
After creating four colors, he sprinkled them on the face of the world.
Colored with four colors, Krishna celebrates Holi.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
