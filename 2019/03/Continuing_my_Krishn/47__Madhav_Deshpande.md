Continuing my Krishna verses

एकवर्णो न कोऽप्यत्र होलिकायां व्रजाङ्गणे ।
सर्वैर्वर्णैर्लिप्यमान: कृष्णोऽगाद् बहुवर्णताम् ।।६८९।।
During Holi in the courtyard of Vraja, there is no one with just a single
color. Being smeared with all colors, Krishna himself became multi-colored.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
