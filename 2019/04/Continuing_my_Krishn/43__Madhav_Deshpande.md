Continuing my Krishna verses

अद्वितीयेन हरिणा होलीं खेलितुमिच्छता ।
लीलया निर्मितं तेन राधया सह गोकुलम् ।।७००।।
Krishna who was all alone [at the beginning of creation], wishing to
celebrate Holi, playfully created Gokula along with Radha.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
