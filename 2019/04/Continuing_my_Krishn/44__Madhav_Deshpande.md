Continuing my Krishna verses

हरिर्हरिर्हरी राधा हरिरेव च गोकुलम् ।
होलीं खेलति गोविन्दो नानारूपाणि धारयन् ।।७०१।।
Krishna is Krishna. Krishna is Radha, and Krishna is Gokula. Krishna plays
Holi assuming many different forms.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
