Continuing my Krishna verses

कुत्रासि राधिके गूढा प्रतिब्रूहि क्षमस्व माम् ।
मां विहाय कुतो याता न सहे मेऽद्वितीयताम् ।।७०३।।
Krishna says: "O Radhika, where are you hiding? Please respond to me.
Forgive me. Why did you leave me? I cannot bear my loneliness."

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
