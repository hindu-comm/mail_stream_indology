Continuing my Krishna verses

मा धाव यमुनातीरे मामन्वेष्टुं यदूत्तम ।
निजं हृदयमुद्घाट्य पश्य मां तत्र संस्थिताम् ।।७०५।।
Radhika says: "O Best of Yadus, do not run along the bank of Yamuna to look
for me. Open your heart, and you will see me residing there."

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
