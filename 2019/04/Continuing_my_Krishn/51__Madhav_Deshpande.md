Continuing my Krishna verses

न राधिका विना कृष्णं न कृष्णो राधिकां विना ।
परस्परावेशवशादभिन्नावेकतां गतौ ।।७०८।।
Radhika is not without Krishna and Krishna is not without Radhika. Because
they are infused into each other, they are inseparable and have become one.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
