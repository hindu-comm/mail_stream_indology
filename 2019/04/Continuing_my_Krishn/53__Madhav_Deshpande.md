Continuing my Krishna verses

समानहृदयौ बद्धावच्छेद्यै: प्रेमरज्जुभि: ।
राजेते राधिकाकृष्णौ रोहिणीशशिनाविव ।।७१०।।
Sharing a common heart and bound with unbreakable bonds of love, Radhika
and Krishna shine like the Moon and the star of Rohini.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
