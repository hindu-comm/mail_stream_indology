Continuing my Krishna verses

राधेति मधुरं नाम माधवेति मनोहरम् ।
राधामाधवयो: प्रीतिर्जगदानन्दवर्धिनी ।।७११।।
Radhika's name is sweet and the name of Krishna is enchanting. The love of
Radhika and Krishna increases the joy of the world.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
