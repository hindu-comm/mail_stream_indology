Continuing my Krishna verses

नन्दन्ति निर्जरा नाके पश्यन्तो हरिराधिकम् ।
कुसुमानि किरन्तस्ते ददते करतालकम् ।।७१३।।
Gods in heaven rejoice watching [the dance of] Krishna and Radhika.
Showering flowers they clap with their hands.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
