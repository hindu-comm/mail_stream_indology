Continuing my Krishna verses

न सुवर्णं न रत्नानि मौक्तिकानि न नो धनम् ।
धन्या वयं लब्धवन्तो हरिरूपं महद्धनम् ।।७१७।।
Our wealth is not gold, jewels or pearls. Blessed are we that we have found
the great wealth in the form of Krishna.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
