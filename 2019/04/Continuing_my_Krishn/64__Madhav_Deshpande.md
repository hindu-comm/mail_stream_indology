Continuing my Krishna verses

कुतो गोविन्दरत्नाभां वयमुत्प्रेक्षितुं क्षमा: ।
उद्घाट्य मे मनोद्वारं माधवो दर्शनं ददौ ।।७२१।।
How would we be able to imagine the brilliance of the jewel of Krishna?
Opening the gates of my mind, Krishna himself offered me a vision.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
