Continuing my Krishna verses

यशोदालालितो बालो नन्दतातेन पालित: ।
प्रीणितो गोपवृन्देन कुरुते नर्तनं हृदि ।।७२२।।
The little boy cherished by his mother Yashoda, cared for by his father
Nanda, and loved by a host of cowherds is dancing in my heart.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
