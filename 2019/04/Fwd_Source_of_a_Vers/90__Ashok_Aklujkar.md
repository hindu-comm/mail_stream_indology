Probably a nāndī verse of Rājaśekhara’s so far undiscovered or lost play Hara-vilāsa (for this work, see M. Krishnamachariar’s _History of Classical Sansrit Literature_, section 655, p. 627f. A.K. Warder may have more information in the corresponding volume of his _Indian Kāvya Literarure_.)

"In Vidyākara’s Subhāṣitaratnakośa, the following verse is attributed to Rājaśekhara: avyād vo valikāṅghri-pāta-vicalad-bhūgola-helonmukha-bhrāmyad-dik-kari-kalpitānukaraṇo nṛtyan gaṇa-grāmaṇīḥ / yasyoddaṇḍita-śuṇḍa-puṣkara-marud-vyākṛṣṭa-sṛṣṭaṃ muhus tārā-cakram udakta-śīkara-pṛṣal-līlām ivābhyasyati // VidSrk_5.13 *(83)—taken from GRETIL. Does anyone know the source text of the verse?Æ
