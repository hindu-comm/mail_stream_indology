Continuing my Krishna verses

हृदयं करुणापूर्णं तस्य बुद्धस्वरूपिण: ।
अहिंसाप्रमुखं धर्मं दिदेश हितचिन्तक: ।।७३५।।
In the form of Buddha, with a compassionate heart, thinking of the benefit
of the people, he taught a doctrine with the principal notion of
non-violence.
