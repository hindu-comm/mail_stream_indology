Continuing my Krishna verses

सर्वेषां हृदयानन्दोऽभवस्त्वं नन्दनन्दन: ।
समुल्लसति लीलाभिर्यस्याद्यावधि गोकुलम् ।।७४१।।
As the delightful son of Nanda, you became a delight for all. The village
of Gokula shines with your plays till this day.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
