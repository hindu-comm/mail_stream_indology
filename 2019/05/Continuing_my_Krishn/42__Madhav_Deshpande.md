Continuing my Krishna verses

क्रीडारथेन य: खेलन् क्वासि पार्थेति पृच्छति ।
गीतोपदेशचिन्तायां रतं तं बालकं नुम: ।।७४६।।
We offer our obeisance to that child who, while playing with a toy chariot,
was engrossed in the teaching of the Bhagavadgītā asking: "O Arjuna, where
are you?"

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
