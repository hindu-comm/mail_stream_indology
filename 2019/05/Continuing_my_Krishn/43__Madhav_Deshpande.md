Continuing my Krishna verses

क्रीडाधनु: समादाय बलेन सह सञ्चरन् ।
स्मरन् सीतापहरणं शिशू रोदिति गोकुले ।।७४७।।
Holding a toy bow and wandering with brother Balarama, the child in Gokula
cries remembering the abduction of Sita.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
