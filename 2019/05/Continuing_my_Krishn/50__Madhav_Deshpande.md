Continuing my Krishna verses

धावन्तं बालकृष्णं मेऽनिशं चेतोऽनुधावति ।
कथं गृह्णामि चपलं मन्देन मनसा मम ।।७५३।।
My mind always rushes after the running baby Krishna. How can I catch that
fast child with my slow mind?

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
