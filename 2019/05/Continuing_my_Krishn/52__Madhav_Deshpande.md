Continuing my Krishna verses

धावन्तं यमुनातीरे गूहमानं स्थले स्थले ।
गाहमानं जले कृष्णं कथं गृह्णन्तु गोपिका: ।।७५५।।
How can the cowgirls catch Krishna running along the bank of Yamuna, hiding
from place to place and diving in the water?

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
