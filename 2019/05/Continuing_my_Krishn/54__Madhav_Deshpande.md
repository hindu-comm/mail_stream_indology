Continuing my Krishna verses

दिक्षु दिक्षु प्रधावन्त्यो गोपिका यमुनातटे ।
सर्वास्ता अभिमन्यन्ते "गृहीतोऽयं मया हरि:" ।।७५७।।
The cowgirls, running in different directions on the bank of Yamuna, all of
them assert: "I caught this Krishna."

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
