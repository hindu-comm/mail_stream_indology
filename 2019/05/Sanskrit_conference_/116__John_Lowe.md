Mahopādhyāyamahotsava: Celebration of a Sanskrit Teacher

20th June 2019 at Wolfson College, Oxford

Join us for a one day conference in celebration of Sanskrit studies, in honour of Dr Jim Benson; with eminent speakers presenting on topics including Sanskrit and Vedic literature, Pāṇinian grammar, philosophy, religion, history, poetics and culture.

Lunch and refreshments are provided. Attendance is free, but please register in advance so that we can order the right number of sandwiches. For the full programme, and to register, visit:

https://mahopadhyayamahotsava.eventbrite.co.uk
