Continuing my Krishna verses

यस्तं पश्यति सर्वत्र सर्वं तस्मिंश्च पश्यति ।
तं स कृष्ण: सदानन्द: कृपयावृणुते सदा ।।७६२।।
That ever-delightful Krishna always envelops with compassion those who see
him everywhere and see everything in him.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
