Continuing my Krishna verses

यो न पश्यति गोविन्दं सर्वदा निकटे स्थितम् ।
तमप्यसौ दयासिन्धु: कृपयावृणुते सदा ।।७६३।।
Krishna, the ocean of compassion, constantly envelops with his compassion
even those who do not see him always standing nearby.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
