Continuing my Krishna verses

अज्ञा: प्राज्ञाश्च हरिणा सर्व एव विनिर्मिता: ।
सर्वान् पश्यन् स गोविन्द: कृपयावृणुते सदा ।।७६४।।

Krishna created them all, the wise and the ignorant. Watching over them
all, he always covers them with his compassion.

Madhav M. Deshpande
Professor Emeritus
Sanskrit and Linguistics
University of Michigan
[Residence: Campbell, California]
