Dear Listmembers,
Although Vedic studies and Pāṇinian studies can be very well practiced
independently, we know from the work of Thieme, Renou, Palsule, Cardona,
Deshpande, Bronkhorst ... that there is a potential plus-value to combining
the two.
I am therefore happy to announce the
*    Première Journée d’études védiques et pāṇinéennes*
that will take place in Paris on 12 juin 2019.
Organizers: Silvia D'Intino and Jan Houben
For further details see:
https://www.iran-inde.cnrs.fr/evenements-scientifiques/colloques-et-conferences-2019/etudes-vediques-et-p%C4%81%E1%B9%87ineennes.html?lang=fr

For the program see:
https://www.iran-inde.cnrs.fr/IMG/pdf/Programme_JEVP-12-06-2019.pdf
Hopefully next time we will be able to announce this day longer in
advance...
With best regards,
Jan Houben
