Continuing my Krishna verses

लोकत्रयपरित्राणशङ्खचक्रगदाधर: ।
लोकेशो भगवान् विष्णुर्गोकुले शिशुतां गत: ।।८०४।।

Vishnu, the Lord of the World, holding the conch, the discus and the mace
for the protection of the three worlds, became a child in Gokula.

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
