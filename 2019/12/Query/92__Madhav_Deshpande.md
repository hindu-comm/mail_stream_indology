>      This quote in some medieval Dharmaśāstra works is interpreted to mean
> that in the Kali Yuga, only the ādya varṇa [= Brahmins] and the antya varṇa
> [= śūdras] survive, and that there are no true Kṣatriyas and Vaiśyas.  This
> quote was the basis for rejecting the Kṣatriyahood of some kings during the
> medieval times.
