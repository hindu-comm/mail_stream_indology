कोरोनशब्दसंस्कारे  णत्वं न वेति निर्णये ।

यद् आशुकविना प्रोक्तं  तद् अस्माकं प्रमाणकम् ॥



Not difficult to guess who is here the आशुकवि ...

Even then, कोरोण should be deemed possible as a somewhat pedantic parallel
form.

The attribution of grammatical gender is not taught by grammar, but in view
of the enormity of the phenomenon the feminine seems a good choice (cp.
himānī vs. hima).

Jan Houben
