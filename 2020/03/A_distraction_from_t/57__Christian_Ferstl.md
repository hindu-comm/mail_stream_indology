And he said to Sita:

मा मठस्त्यज्यतां सीते तिष्ठ तिष्ठेह सर्वथा ।
स कोरोनौषधं यावद्धनुमानानयिष्यति ॥

"Don't leave the hut, Sita! Stay here under all circumstances,
until Hanuman brings medicine against Corona."


Christian Ferstl
