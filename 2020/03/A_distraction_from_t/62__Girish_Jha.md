Dear Mr Christian
Pranamami.
Hear आनेष्यति should be used instead of आनयिष्यति  as per Panini's
Grammar.It is advisable to use here प्रापयिष्यति which well maintains the
same meaning and metre.
क्षन्तव्योsयं जनः।
Girish K.Jha
Retd.University Professor
Dept of Sanskrit
Patna University
Current Residence: Kolkata
