आरम्भे भघञ्च्वीत्यादि किमप्यन्ते भविष्यति ।
तद्वद् रूपान्तरे प्रख्यः मर्कटादधमो सदा ।।

bha, gañ, cvi (suffixes) will be called in the beginning and will be
changed to something, at the end.

Similarly, the grammarian, renowned as a chameleon always poor than a
monkey.
