Dear Mr Christian

I am indeed happy to read your sloka but it is needed to be cautious about
the gender too.It should be स्पर्शनीये तव पादपद्मे as पद्म is in neuter
gender.you might have better used वृथैव सीते instead of सीते वृथा वै and
the meaning be the same with the protection of metre.
Regards
Girish K.Jha
