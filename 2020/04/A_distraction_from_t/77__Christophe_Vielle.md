Do you mean Triśaṅku (instead of Nahuṣa):

triśaṅkor ivobhayalokabhraṣṭasya naktaṃdinam avākśirasas tiṣṭhataḥ, Harṣacarita 7 [249]

triśaṅkor upabhogāya na dyaur api na bhūr api, Murāri, Anargharāghava 1.59 (ed. Törzsök)

see also Kālidāsa, Abhijñānaśākuntala (Act. 2, just before stanza 17); Abhinavagupta, Paryantapañcāśikā, v. 37; Mokṣopaya 4,5.11

Acccording to the Malayalam Lexicon, the expression triśaṅku-svarggam means "a state of suspense or indefiniteness"
