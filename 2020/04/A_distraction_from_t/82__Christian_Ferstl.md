विश्वव्यापिन्यनावासा सा विहायसगामिनी ।
अणुकाया महामारी कोरोनाख्या प्रशाम्यताम् ॥

Pervading the whole world, having no abode, moving through the air,
having but a minute body -- may the great plague called Corona fade 
away!


Christian Ferstl
