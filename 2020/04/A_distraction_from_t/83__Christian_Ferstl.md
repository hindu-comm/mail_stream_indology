अयोध्यां सा यदागम्य धर्म्यानैक्षत नागरान् ।
तदा प्रणम्य कोरोना तुष्टा कमपि नास्पृशत् ॥

When Corona had come to Ayodhya and saw the righteous townsfolk,
it was pleased, bowed down, and did not touch anyone.


Christian Ferstl
