समाजाद्दूरमासीना बान्धवेभ्यश्च यद्यपि ।
पतिदेवस्य बाह्वोस्तु सीताप परमं सुखम् ॥

Although she was dwelling far from society and from her relatives,
in the arms of her lord and husband Sītā felt greatest happiness.


Christian Ferstl
