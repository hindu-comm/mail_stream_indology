Chidamabara Samhitaa, Bhuvaneshwaree Samhitaa, Yoga Ratnavali etc. are the
ritual manual manuals dealing with Devi Saptashati Paatha paddhati .

They mention one when to perform Kavacha, beeja, argalaa, keelaka.

They don't define / explain these terms.
