What does vituṇḍita mean in the following verse?

trunkless".

Bhagavatī-padya-puṣpāñjalī-stotram verse 4

ayi śata-khaṇḍa-vikhaṇḍita-ruṇḍa-vituṇḍita-śuṇḍa-gajādhipate
ripu-gaja-gaṇḍa-viḍāraṇa-caṇḍa-parākrama-śuṇḍa-mṛgādhipate |
nija-bhuja-daṇḍa-nipātita-khaṇḍa-vipātita-muṇḍa-bhaṭādhipate
