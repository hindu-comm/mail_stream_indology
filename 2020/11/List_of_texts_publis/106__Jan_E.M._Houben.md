Dear List Members,
I thank Ashok Aklujkar for having kindly provided me with a scan of his
1966 article on The Pandit.
The article is written in Sanskrit and was published in the
Pune-based Sanskrit journal Śāradā.
I will try to attach it: it is not a very heavy file but perhaps it will
not come through...
In any case, I note the following:

saṁskṛta-patrakāritā-śatābdī-viśeṣalekhaḥ ‘paṇḍita’-patra-paricayaḥ
Śāradā 1888-1966, pp. 7-13 (22 May 1966)
by Ashok Aklujkar

containing an
Index of The Pandit under the following headings :
prathitāḥ kṛtayaḥ
sāmānyataḥ aprathitāḥ kṛtayaḥ
āṅglyā anūditāḥ prathitāḥ kṛtayaḥ
āṅglyā anūditāḥ sāmānyataḥ aprathitāḥ kṛtayaḥ
saṁskṛtena anūditāḥ kṛtayaḥ
paṇḍitāṅkeṣu mudritāḥ mahattvapūrṇāḥ nibandhāḥ

With best regards,
Jan Houben
