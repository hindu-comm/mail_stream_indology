There is a three volume edition of Prakriyākaumudī with the commentary
Prakāśa by Kr̥ṣṇaśeṣa edited by Muralidhara Mishra published in 1980 in
Sarasvati Bhavana Granthamālā, no. 112.  This was published after Cardona's
bibliography.
