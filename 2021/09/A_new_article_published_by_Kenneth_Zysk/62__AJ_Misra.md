Dear all:

This is a publication notice. Kenneth Zysk has recently published an
article "From symposion to goṣṭhī: The Adaptation of a Greek Social Custom
in Ancient India" in *Studia Orientalia Electronica*. The article explores
the relationship between the social institutions of the Greek *symposion* and
Indian *goṣṭhī*. The article can be accessed at
https://journal.fi/store/article/view/102235
