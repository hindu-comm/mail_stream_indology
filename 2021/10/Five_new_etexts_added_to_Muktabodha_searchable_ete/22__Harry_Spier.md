Dear list members,
The following five new etexts, all transcribed from manuscripts of  Shri
Ragunatha MSS Library Jammu, have been added to the Muktabodha searchable
etext library.
Link: https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/digital_library.htm

 1) agastyasaṁhitā transcribed from Shri Ragunatha MSS Library Jammu no:
5294

2) bhāvacūḍāmaṇi commentary by Vidyakaṇṭha transcribed from Shri Ragunatha
MSS Library Jammu no: 5291. There are now two manuscripts of this text in the
library, the other being transcribed from NGMCP 6/1748 reel number A 989/23

3) sarasvatīstotram (k), mantrabhāsyam (kh), aṣṭādaśajātinirṇaya (g)
transcribed from Shri Ragunatha MSS Library Jammu manuscripts 2674 (k) ,
2675 (kh), 2676 (g).

4) saubhāgyaratnākara transcribed from Shri Ragunatha MSS Library Jammu no:
4892

5) siddhāntaleśasaṁgrahaṭīkā transcribed from Shri Ragunatha MSS Library
Jammu no: 5388 gh

On behalf of the Muktabodha Indological Research Institute
Harry Spier
