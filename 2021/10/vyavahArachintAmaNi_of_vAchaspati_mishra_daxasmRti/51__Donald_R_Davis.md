Dear Friends,

I’m pleased to announce the addition of two new texts to the Resource Library for Dharmaśāstra Studies<https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/dharmasastra/>: 1) Vyavahāracintāmaṇi of Vācaspati Miśra<https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/dharmasastra/vyavaharacintamani/>,  transcription by Patrick Olivelle, based on the edition of Ludo Rocher, and 2) Dakṣasmṛti<https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/dharmasastra/daksasmrti/>, transcription by myself, based on the edition by Irma Piovano.

Best, Don

Don Davis
Professor & Chair
Dept. of Asian Studies
University of Texas at Austin
