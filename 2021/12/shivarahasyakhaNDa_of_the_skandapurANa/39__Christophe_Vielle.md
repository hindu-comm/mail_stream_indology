Dear list,

I am searching for the vol. 1  of the

Śivarahasyakhaṇḍa of the (Śaṅkara-/Śāṅkarī- or Agastya-saṃhitā of the) Skanda-Mahāpurāṇa

Ed. with Tamil translation by Cēṅkalipuram Anantarāma Tikṣitar, 2 vols, Cēlam [1964 s.d.]

The vol. 2 seems to be here:

https://archive.org/details/SriSkandaPuranam-SankaraSamhitaPart1/

(with the 6th and 7th/last Dakṣa- and Upadeśa-kāṇḍas)

The vol. 1 should have the 1st to 5th Saṃbhava-, Asura-, Vīramahendra-, Yuddha- en Deva-kāṇḍas.

This is a Sanskrit work closely connected with (the model of?) both the (14th c.?) Tamil Kantapurāṇam by Civācāriyar (see https://books.openedition.org/ifp/2756<https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbooks.openedition.org%2Fifp%2F2756&data=04%7C01%7Cchristophe.vielle%40uclouvain.be%7C6826c0f12e6d4452771408d9b57d3125%7C7ab090d4fa2e4ecfbc7c4127b4d582ec%7C0%7C0%7C637740371562576952%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000&sdata=hqTeQjfcTnB9CuIjZQMP7xq6ZQfuBGld%2BowaY1zxEH8%3D&reserved=0> and http://www.skandagurunatha.org/works/kantha-puranam/ ) and the (16th c.) Malayalam Skandapurāṇaṃ Kiḷippāṭṭŭ (respectively limited to the first 6 or 5 kāṇṭam/ kāṇḍaṃs).

Best wishes,

Christophe
