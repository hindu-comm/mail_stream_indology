Dear Colleagues,

The Sanskrit Library would like to announce a course on the Sanskrit theatre taught by scholar-performer Prasad Bhide beginning on 12 March 2022.
We are happy to inform you that we started our Indian branch this year. Now our courses are available for Indian residents as well.

CG40. Understanding the Sanskrit theatre
Duration: 12 March – 14 May 2022.
This course introduces certain core ideas of Sanskrit theatre with reference to भरत’s नाट्यशास्त्र.
