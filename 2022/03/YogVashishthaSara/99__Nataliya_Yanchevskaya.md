As far as I know, all the Yogavāsiṣṭhas (including the Sāra and Laghu) are
considered to be 'Rāmāyaṇas' and as such are ascribed to Vālmīki (the same
way as the Mahābhārata is traditionally ascribed to Vyāsa).
