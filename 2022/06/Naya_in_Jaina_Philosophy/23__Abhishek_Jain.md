Dear Raja,

Primary sources in Prakrit and Sanskrit are dedicated to the concept of
*naya* in Jain Philosophy:


Shastri, Kailash Chandra (1971):* Davvasabhāvappakasako Ṇayacakko by **Māïlla
Dhavala*. Varanasi: Bhāratīya Jñānapīṭha Publication.


Jain, Ratancand (translator and commentator) (Vīranirvāṇa Samvat 2467)
(1940): *Śrīmad Devasenācāryaviracitā Ālāpapaddhati Apara Nāma Drvyānuyoga
Praveśikā*. Saharanpur (U.P.): Prācīn Ārṣ Granthāyatan.

I hope this helps.
