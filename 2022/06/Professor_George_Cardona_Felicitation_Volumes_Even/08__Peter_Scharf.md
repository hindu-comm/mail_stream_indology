Dear colleagues,

On the occasion of Professor Cardona’s 86th birthday, 3 June at 10:30am U.S. Eastern Time (9:30 Central Time), The Sanskrit Library will host a Zoom event dedicating the volumes to him and inviting five of the younger contributors to Volume 1, Vyākaraṇa and śābdabodha, who have seldom had the opportunity to share their work with Professor Cardona to discuss their contributions with him.  The presenters will be:
 Junichi Ozono, Correct speech (śabda): from the perspective of the Veda and Pāṇinian grammar
Tanuja Ajotikar, भाष्यसम्मताष्टाध्यायीपाठ: a work on variations in the a work on variations in the sūtras of the Aṣṭādhyāyī
Aleix Ruiz-Falqués, Two levels of optionality in the Kaccāyana Vyākaraṇa
Andrey Klebanov, On the formation of ekadeśisamāsa
Balram Shukla, व्यञ्जनायाः सर्ववाक्यसाधारणत्वम
To join the event, please register in the Google form at: https://forms.gle/oeNMpk6L6Mn2sYpD6 <https://forms.gle/oeNMpk6L6Mn2sYpD6>.  Upon registration, you will receive the Zoom link both as a response to the Web form as well as by email.

We look forward to your participation.
Yours,
