Dear all,



We would like to invite you to join us online for our Conference on 'Jain Practices of Literary Transcreation', held at the Department of Indian Languages and Cultures at Ghent University on the 8th and 9th of September 2022. This conference will be preceded on the 7th of September by the Acharya Mahapragya Lecture given by Prof. Peter Flügel (SOAS, University of London).

The program of this conference is scheduled as follows (all times are CET: GMT+2):
