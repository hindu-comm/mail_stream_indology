Dear list members,
The long-term Academy project “Hindu Temple Legends in South India” (see https://www.uni-heidelberg.de/en/newsroom/academy-project-hindu-temple-legends-in-south-india ) is inviting applications for a full-time position as Postdoctoral Scholar in Indology. The project is committed to producing digital editions and annotated translations of the corpus of Sanskrit and Tamil mythological texts (sthalamāhātmyas/ talapurāṇams) relating to the South Indian city of Kanchipuram. The project will commence in September 2022 and is scheduled to run until 2037.
For details, see the attached document, or see https://www.hadw-bw.de/aktuelles/ausschreibungen/stellenausschreibung .

Please share this message with suitable candidates.

With best regards
Ute Hüsken
