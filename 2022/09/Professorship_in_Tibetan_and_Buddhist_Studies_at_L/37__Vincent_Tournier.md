Dear Colleagues,

Please find in attachment the call for applications for a Professorship in Tibetan and Buddhist Studies at the Institut für Indologie und Tibetologie (Fakultät für Kulturwissenschaften) of LMU Munich. This position, previously held by Prof. Franz-Karl Ehrhard, is a tenured position (W2), and it is funded by the TARA-Stiftung and the Khyentse Foundation. As you may see from the call, the required profile of the new position-holder is historical/philological.

I would be grateful if you could circulate the call to suitable candidates. The deadline for applications is 13 October 2022.
