Dear colleagues, 

We are delighted to invite you to the upcoming fourth instalment of the Georg Bühler Lecture Series of the Austrian Academy of Sciences featuring Annette Schmiedchen (Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin) and Arlo Griffiths (Ecole française d'Extrême-Orient, Paris/Jakarta) presenting about:

Epigraphy and History in the 21st Century
Looking Back on 140 Years of Maitraka and Rāṣṭrakūṭa Epigraphy (Annette Schmiedchen)
Looking Forward to a Digital Database of South and Southeast Asian Epigraphy (Arlo Griffiths)

The lecture will be held both in person in Vienna and online this Thursday 10th November at 17:00 CET. For the program and abstracts, please see here <https://www.oeaw.ac.at/fileadmin/NEWS/2022/PDF/Programmfolder-Buehler-Lectures-Epigraphy-and-History-V21-10-2022.pdf>.

If you are in Vienna, you are very warmly invited; otherwise you may also follow the lecture online. Please kindly register here <https://www.oeaw.ac.at/veranstaltungen/anmeldung/1/2020/georg-buehler-lectures-10-november-2022-5-pm>. 

The lecture series dedicated to Georg Bühler and organised by the Austrian Academy of Sciences marks and celebrates the 140th anniversary of Bühler's taking up the Chair for Philology and Antiquities of Ancient India at the University of Vienna and his subsequent membership at the Austria Academy of Sciences. Six lectures address Bühler’s intellectual legacy and also highlight new research and latest developments in Bühler’s fields of study. Please see below for the other speakers and talks in the series in 2022. 

Best wishes, 

Nina Mirnig and Hannes A. Fellner (Organizers)
