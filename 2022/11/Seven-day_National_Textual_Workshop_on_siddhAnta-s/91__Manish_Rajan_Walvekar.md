Greetings from the Amrita Darshanam, ICSS!

Amrita Darshanam-ICSS Bengaluru campus-Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, in
association with the Indian Council for Philosophical Research (ICPR), is
organizing a Seven-day National Textual Workshop on Siddhānta-Sārāvali.

The Siddhāṇtasārāvali composed by Trilocanaśiva, a great Śaiva Ācārya and a
resident of Tamil Nadu, is a very handy and useful text to get a fairly
detailed introduction to the Śaivasiddhānta. Readers will be introduced to
the basic philosophical doctrines of the Śaivasiddhānta system through a
detailed reading of the section known as the vidyāpāda of the
Siddhāntasārāvali.

Following are the details of the workshop.
Dates: 23rd to 29th January 2023
Venue: Conference Hall, Ground Floor, A Block, Amrita School of
Engineering, Kasavanahalli, Bengaluru - 560035

Kindly Note: This workshop is sponsored by Indian Council for Philosophical
Research, New Delhi. Only limited seats are available for participants. All
