Dear Colleagues,

Happy New Year!

The IASS (International Association of Sanskrit Studies) initiated this Fellowship in 2019 and it will be offered again for 2023. Any member of the IASS is eligible for the HRF, if the scholar has received a PhD in Sanskrit or allied studies in the last five years.


The HRF is for research work to be conducted in South Asia for a period of at least two weeks in any field related to Sanskrit and allied studies. If travel is impossible, an alternative may be proposed, for example, an online conference with experts in India in the chosen field.  The Honorary Research Fellowship is valued at 1000 Euros.


The DEADLINE for the written project proposal has been extended to 28 February 2023.

Please send all proposals and/or queries to Associate Professor McComas Taylor of the IASS:

McComas.Taylor at anu.edu.au<mailto:McComas.Taylor at anu.edu.au>
