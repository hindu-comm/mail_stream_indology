Dear colleagues,

With great pleasure I would like to draw your attention to a new  
publication of our Ukrainian colleagues:
"The World of the Orient" (Східний світ), No. 4 (2022) "Commemorating  
the 150th Birthday of Pavlo Ritter" (open access).
https://oriental-world.org.ua/index.php/journal/issue/view/39
The articles in this issue dedicated to Ukrainian Indologist Pavlo  
Ritter (1872-1939) are written in English and Ukrainian, and some of  
them also deal with Indological topics. That this volume could appear  
at all borders on a miracle in view of the continuing inhuman and  
brutal attacks, and one can only admire the courage and determination  
of the Ukrainian Scholars.

With kind regards
Frank Köhler
