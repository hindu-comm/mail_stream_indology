Dear colleagues,

we are happy to announce the publication of the*sixth volume in the 
series */*Puṣpikā*/, edited by Vitus Angermeier, Christian Ferstl, 
Channa Li, and myself.

The peer-reviewed series publishes the proceedings of the *International 
**Indology Graduate Research Symposium* (IIGRS, 
https://iigrs.wordpress.com/); in this case, this was the 12th IIGRS, 
which took place in Vienna, Austria, 22nd–24th of July, 2021.

The current volume contains contributions from *13 authors*: Jürgen 
Hanneder, Ge Ge, Seongho Choi, Sibylle Koch, Shihong Zhao, Mercy Dutta, 
Devdutta Kakati, Rosina Pastore, Amandine Wattelier-Bricout, Dominique 
Marcel Baur, Anna Scarabel, Charlotte Gorant, and Xiaoqiang Meng.

The new publisher**of the /Puṣpikā /series is *Heidelberg Asian Studies 
Publishing*, a *Fair Open Access* publisher (see https://foasas.org/). 
You can download and/or order the current volume here:

https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1133**

Puṣpikā <https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1133>

Best regards,

D. Haas
