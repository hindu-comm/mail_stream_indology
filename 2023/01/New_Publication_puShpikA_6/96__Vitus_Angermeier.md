Dear colleagues,

on this occasion I would like to mention that a host and location for 
the next IIGRS has yet to be found. Until now this graduate symposium 
has always taken place in Europe, but as the name includes 
"international", other locations would of course qualify as well. For 
further information, please visit the IIGRS website 
https://iigrs.wordpress.com/ or write to iigrsuk at googlemail.com. Please 
feel free to forward this information especially to younger colleagues 
and graduate students.

We really enjoyed holding this symposium in Vienna and very much hope 
that the IIGRS will live on and prosper!

Best wishes,
Vitus Angermeier

> we are happy to announce the publication of the*sixth volume in the 
> series */*Puṣpikā*/, edited by Vitus Angermeier, Christian Ferstl, 
> Channa Li, and myself.
>
> The peer-reviewed series publishes the proceedings of the 
> *International **Indology Graduate Research Symposium* (IIGRS, 
> https://iigrs.wordpress.com/); in this case, this was the 12th IIGRS, 
> which took place in Vienna, Austria, 22nd–24th of July, 2021.
>
> The current volume contains contributions from *13 authors*: Jürgen 
> Hanneder, Ge Ge, Seongho Choi, Sibylle Koch, Shihong Zhao, Mercy 
> Dutta, Devdutta Kakati, Rosina Pastore, Amandine Wattelier-Bricout, 
> Dominique Marcel Baur, Anna Scarabel, Charlotte Gorant, and Xiaoqiang 
> Meng.
>
> The new publisher**of the /Puṣpikā /series is *Heidelberg Asian 
> Studies Publishing*, a *Fair Open Access* publisher (see
