Dear Colleague,



I am happy to inform you that the International Standard Terminology
documents of Ayurveda, Siddha, and Unani have been published by WHO.



The documents are essential tools that will provide a universally accepted
interface between modern science and the time tested health care practices
of traditional medicine. This is a mile stone in WHO’s work to support
Member States to appropriately integrate traditional medicine in health
system, for research, and effective communication between various medical
systems.



Kindly do the needful to disseminate the same widely, especially where
these systems are mostly regulated and has high community acceptance and
buy-in.



PF the links to the documents below:
