Dear Colleagues,

Let me draw your attention to the following opening of a PhD position in Indology at the University of Zurich. Please feel free to circulate it among possible interested candidates. 

All the best and happy new year,
Elisa Ganser


The Institute of Asian and Oriental Studies, Department of Indian Studies, at the University of Zurich, invites applications for a PhD position (50%)

The position is available from February 1, 2023.

Applications with letter of motivation, CV and an outline of your research interests (in electronic form) by January 15, 2023 to:

Prof. Dr. Angelika Malinar
Asia-Orient-Institute, Indology
Rämistr. 59, CH – 8001 Zurich (email : angelika.malinar at aoi.uzh.ch <mailto:angelika.malinar at aoi.uzh.ch>)
