Dear members of Indology,

We are delighted to inform you of the publication of Science and Society in the Sanskrit World, edited by Christopher T. Fleming, Toke L. Knudsen, Anuj Misra, and Vishal Sharma (Brill, 2023). The volume is published to celebrate the scholarly contributions of Professor Christopher Z. Minkowski, Boden Professor of Sanskrit (now emeritus) at the University of Oxford.

We have attached Brill’s flyer for the volume. More information can be found at:

https://brill.com/edcollbook/title/61887

With all best wishes,
Toke, Christopher, AJ, and Vishal
