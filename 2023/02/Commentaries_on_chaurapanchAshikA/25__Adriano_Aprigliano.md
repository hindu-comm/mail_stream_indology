Dear colleagues,

I know of three editions of this text, Bohlen's (1933), Solf (1886,
Kashmiri recension) and Tadpatrikar (critical ed., 1966). Bohlen's is
edited with a Gaṇapa(ti)-vr̥tti. Tadpatrikar quotes from two commentaries
he doen't specify (one is certainly Gaṇapa's, although with different
readings sometimes, as compared with Bohlen's text.). Does anyone
known/happen to have editions of this text with other commentaries?
