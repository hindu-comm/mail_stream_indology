Dear colleagues and friends,



It is with great pleasure that the newly created* Global Critical
Philosophy of Religion Unit* at the *American Academy of Religion* invites
papers on the following topics:

. *Session on Categories*: The Problem of Evil Pollution/Contamination in
Global-Critical Philosophies of Religion

. *Session on Methods*: Decentralizing the Philosophical Inquiry into
Religion.

…a great opportunity for all South Asianists!



A detailed description for each call can be found here:
https://papers.aarweb.org/pu/global-critical-philosophy-religion-unit

And the proposals are to be submitted there:
https://papers.aarweb.org/login-options by* March 1st at 5pm EST* (you do
not need to be an AAR member at the time of submitting your paper).



The conference will be held in-person in *San Antonio (Texas) on November
18-21, 2023. *



Please contact us if you have any question!



The very best,



Marie-Hélène and Gereon
