Online Certificate Course on Aṣṭādhyāyī

Amrita Darshanam, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is announcing an online
certificate course on the Aṣṭādhyāyī of Pāṇini.

Course Outcomes
1. Students will be able to easily identify the prakṛti-s, pratyaya-s,
ādeśa-s, āgama-s, etc., and their workings in śloka-s and other general
usages.
2. Students will be able to easily understand the important texts of
Vyākaraṇa, like Siddhāntakaumudī, Prauḍhamanoramā, etc.
3. Students will be able to easily enter into different śāstra-s like
Nyāya, Vedānta, Mīmāṃsā, etc.

Mode of Teaching: The course will be taught in English and Sanskrit. The
classes will be held online to facilitate a maximum number of people
benefitting from the course.

Eligibility: Sound knowledge of Sanskrit is essential (spoken Sanskrit).
The applications will be scrutinized.

Starting Date: March 15, 2023 (170 sessions. 5 sessions per week)

Lecture Timings: 07:30 PM to 08:30 PM IST

Registration Closes: March 8, 2023

Fee Structure
Indian Participants: INR 5900 (18% GST included)
Foreign Participants: USD 300

All registered participants will be awarded a certificate.

For more details, please visit the website-
https://www.amrita.edu/program/astadhyayi/
