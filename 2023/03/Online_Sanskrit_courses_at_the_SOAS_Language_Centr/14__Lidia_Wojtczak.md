Dear Colleagues,
I'm getting in touch just in case you know any students who are looking for Sanskrit courses online.

The SOAS Language Centre has just opened enrolment for a selection of Beginners and Pre-Intermediate courses next term, as well as a Reading Group course which is currently working through the Saundarnanda.

All courses are online and run once a week in the evenings UK time (here is a link to the timetables<https://www.soas.ac.uk/study/short-courses-overview/language-centre-short-courses/term-enrolment/all-other-languages-short>).

If you know any students who might be interested, please ask them to contact me directly at lw24 at soas.ac.uk or to write to the SOAS Language Centre (sa at soas.ac.uk for the South Asia section).

With warmest wishes,
Lidia
