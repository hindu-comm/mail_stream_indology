Dear colleagues,

I am writing to ask if you can share the poster available through this link with prospective students:  https://tinyurl.com/minkowskiuoft. We are excited to be offering an in-person intermediate summer Sanskrit reading course this year at the University of Toronto taught by Prof. Christopher Minkowski.

The course will take place at the Mississauga campus from June 26 to August 10, 2023. There is no tuition. Students staying on-campus will receive a subsidy to cover part of the costs for room and board.

Interested candidates should apply by sending an unofficial transcript and 200-word statement about research interests to Aqsa Ijaz (aqsa.ijaz at utoronto.ca<mailto:aqsa.ijaz at utoronto.ca>) by April 15, 2023.

For more information about the course please contact me at ajay.rao at utoronto.ca<mailto:ajay.rao at utoronto.ca>.

Thank you,
