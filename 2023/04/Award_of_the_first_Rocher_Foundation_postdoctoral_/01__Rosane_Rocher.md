The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is pleased to announce the award 
of our first postdoctoral fellowship to Jean Arzoumanov (Ph.D. in South 
Asian Studies, Université Paris 3 - Sorbonne Nouvelle, December 2021). 
Dr. Arzoumanov will pursue his work on Indian sects and ascetics in 
Indo-Persian literature of the 16th to the 19th centuries. He has opted 
to be in residence at the University of Chicago for the duration of his 
fellowship.
