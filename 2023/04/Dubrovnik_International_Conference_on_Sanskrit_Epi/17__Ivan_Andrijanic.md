Dear list members,

this is the second call for participation in the conference DICSEP 10 (Dubrovnik International Conference on Sanskrit Epics and Purāṇas 10).

Although the deadline for submitting titles for presentations, panels and workshops has passed, those who have not announced a title can still submit an abstract until the end of April (iandrij at ffzg.hr <mailto:iandrij at ffzg.hr> and jezic.mislav at gmail.com <mailto:jezic.mislav at gmail.com>). Presentations, panels and workshops will be held live at the conference, but it will be possible to watch presentations online and participate in the discussions via Zoom. For online participation, we will post a separate call with details on this list.

All information about the conference, participation and accommodation in Dubrovnik is available at the conference website <https://dicsep.org/>.
