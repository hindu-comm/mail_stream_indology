Dear Colleagues,

	The Sanskrit Library would like to announce its 2023 summer intensive Sanskrit courses.
US101S Introductory Sanskrit I (Summer intensive)
US102S Introductory Sanskrit II (Summer intensive)
These courses cover the equivalent of one academic year of Sanskrit.
For further information about these courses and to register, please visit our Website at this link: https://sanskritlibrary.org/coursesnow.html.

Please share the news and the link with anyone and everyone you think might be interested.

Thank you.

	The Sanskrit Library also plans to launch a two-year full-time on-line Master’s Degree program in Sanskrit beginning in September.  We’ll send out details in early June.
