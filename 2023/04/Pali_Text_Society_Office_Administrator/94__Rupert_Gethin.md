Dear Colleagues,

The Pali Text Society (PTS ) <palitextsociety.org<http://palitextsociety.org/>> is seeking to appoint a part-time (25 hours a week) office administrator to take over the management of the PTS office from 1 July 2023.

The main responsibilities of the position will be the day-to-day management of the PTS office, including the processing of book sales and management of PTS membership. The PTS currently has a small office in Bristol where the officer administrator would be able to work, but the position would also be suitable for someone wishing to work from home. Please note, however, that the position is only open to those who have the right to live and work in the UK. The salary will be in the range £32,411–£36,333 a year, pro-rata.

Letters of application, together with a CV and the names of two referees, should be emailed to the PTS Office info at palitextsociety.org<mailto:info at palitextsociety.org> by 3 May 2023. Further particulars of the position are available in the linked PDF document:

https://palitextsociety.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/PTS-office-administrator-further-particulars-1.pdf

Best wishes,

Rupert Gethin
Professor Emeritus of Buddhist Studies
University of Bristol
