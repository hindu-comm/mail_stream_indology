Yes, sesame oil was widely used. A good introductory entry is in the 
classic Dymock survey:
https://archive.org/details/b2129737x_0003/page/n41/mode/2up

Specific textual references in Ayurvedic works can be found through the 
index of Meulenbeld's A History of Indian Medical Literature under the 
entry "tila".
