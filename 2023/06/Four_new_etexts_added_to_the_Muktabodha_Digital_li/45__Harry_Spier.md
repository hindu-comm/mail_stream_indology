Dear list members,
The following 4 etexts from the Ragunatha temple Jammu have been added to
the Muktabodha digital library.
The direct link to the Muktabodha digital library is:
https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/digital_library_secure_entry.htm

The etexts are:

dakṣiṇāmūrtisaṁhitā manuscript 1633kānandeśvarapaddhatiḥ manuscript
5520 ghbagalāmukhīpañcāṅgam manuscript 6025ajñānadhvāntadīpikā by
somanātha manuscript 4966

The transcriptions of these manuscripts have not been normalized.
Word breaks (or lack of word breaks) is as
in the devanagari manuscripts.  The letter "v" has been left as "v"
whether it represents a "v" or a "b".

Note: If you only want to browse the manuscripts, the search screen
can be hidden by
clicking the "hide/view search panel" button in the upper right corner
of the screen.

These etexts were created by the Muktabodha Varanasi office from
manuscript images made
available to the public by the generosity of the eGangotri organisation.

The etexts were produced under the supervision of Dr. Anirban Dash.
The transcribers were Mr. Vinay Mishra, Mr. Dharmendra Kumar, and Mr.
Sandeep Yadav.

Thank you,

Harry Spier
