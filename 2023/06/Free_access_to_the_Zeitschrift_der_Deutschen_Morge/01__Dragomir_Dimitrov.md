Dear colleagues,

members of this list might be interested to know or be reminded that all 
volumes of the "Zeitschrift der Deutschen Morgenländischen Gesellschaft" 
from its beginning in 1847 until 2019 (vol. 169) are freely accessible in digital 
format (PDF) at the following web address: 

https://menadoc.bibliothek.uni-halle.de/dmg/periodical/structure/2327 

According to a positive decision taken recently by the Harrassowitz Verlag, the 
moving wall delay for the ZDMG was reduced from five to three years only. In 
other words, with the exception for the last three years, all other volumes of 
the journal of the German Oriental Society are made freely available. I hope 
that you will appreciate this news and will make good use of it.

Kind regards,
Dragomir Dimitrov
