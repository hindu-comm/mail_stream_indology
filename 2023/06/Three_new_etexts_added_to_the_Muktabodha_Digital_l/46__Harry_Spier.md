Dear list members,
The following 3 e-texts transcribed from manuscripts of the Ragunatha
temple Jammu have been added to the Muktabodha digital library.
The direct link to the Muktabodha digital library is:
https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/digital_library_secure_entry.htm

The etexts are:
navārṇavacaṇḍipañjāṅgam manuscript 4818 dh
mudrāprakāśaḥ  manuscript 1406
mātṛkārṇa-nighaṇṭu by bhānudīkṣitaḥ nārāyaṇadīkṣita-putraḥ manuscript 4858

With the addition of these new manuscripts there are now 500 (mostly
tantric) manuscripts in the searchable Etext library
and slightly more than 3000 texts in total in the three collections of
Muktabodha.

The transcriptions of these manuscripts have not been normalized.
Word breaks (or lack of word breaks) is asin the devanagari
manuscripts. The letters v and b are left as in the manuscript.

These etexts were created by the Muktabodha Varanasi office from
manuscript images made
available to the public by the generosity of the eGangotri organisation.

The etexts were produced under the supervision of Dr. Anirban Dash.
The transcribers were Mr. Vinay Mishra, Mr. Dharmendra Kumar, and Mr.
Sandeep Yadav.


Thank you,Harry Spier
