Dear colleagues,

Next week, Friday, June 23, there will be a hybrid one-day workshop here 
in Vienna, presenting three ongoing research projects on medical 
traditions in South Asia and Tibet. We will start at 9:30 am CEST.

Programme:

  * 9:30–11:00: *COVID-19 in Sowa Rigpa: Approaching Epidemics in the
    Texts and Practices of Tibet and the Himalayas*
    Barbara Gerke, Jan M. A. van der Valk, and William A. McGrath
  * 11:15–12:00: *The Threats of Adharma and Environmental Disturbances:
    Understanding Epidemics in Pre-modern South Asia*
    Vitus Angermeier
  * 12:15–13:15: *A Doctor, a Scholar, and His Works*
    Cristina Pecchia and Sudipta Munsi

For more details on the workshop, the projects and the presentations, 
please see the announcement at the ISTB website 
<https://stb.univie.ac.at/news-events/detail/news/pandemics-epidemics-academics-projects-on-the-medical-traditions-in-south-asia-and-tibet/>.
To join online, use univienna.zoom.us/j/68080455024 
<https://univienna.zoom.us/j/68080455024?pwd=N2UvS0t4K3JLRXF0YXFDdTgwUUhRQT09> 
(Meeting-ID: 680 8045 5024, access code: 302045). If you happen to be in 
Vienna that day, please consider to join in person.
