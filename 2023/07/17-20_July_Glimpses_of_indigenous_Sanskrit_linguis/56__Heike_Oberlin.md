Tiziana Pontillo is Professor for Indology and Tibetan Studies at the Literature, Languages and Cultural Heritage Department of Cagliari. She has published several books and articles on a variety of topics in the field of ancient Indian cultural history. The focus of her research is mainly on indigenous Sanskrit grammar and the role of heterodox groups within Vedic society.
