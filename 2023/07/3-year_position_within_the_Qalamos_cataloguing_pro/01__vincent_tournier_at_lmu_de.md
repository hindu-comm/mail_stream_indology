Dear Colleagues, 

Please find in attachment a call for applications to a 3-year position within the cataloguing project “Qalamos” at the Bayerische Staatsbibliothek in Munich, the next phase of which will focus on South Asian manuscripts. 

Note that this call is only in German, since very good knowledge of that language is required. Deadline for applications is 28 July 2023. For further queries, please contact directly the Staatsbibliothek's colleagues.
