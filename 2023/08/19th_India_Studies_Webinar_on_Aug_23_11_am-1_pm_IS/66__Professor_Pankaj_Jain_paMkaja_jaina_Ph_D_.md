The India Centre, FLAME University is delighted to share that we will be
hosting 2 webinars featuring eminent scholars from Ghent University,
Belgium.

*Prof. Eva De Clercq*, Associate Professor at Ghent University, teaches a
range of courses covering Sanskrit and Prakrit languages. Her primary
research focuses on the Jain adaptations of Indian epics. She will be
speaking on *Jain Mahabharatas*.

*Prof. Tine Vekemans* holds the prestigious *Ācārya Mahāprajña Chair for
Jain Studies* at Ghent University in Belgium. Her scholarly pursuits focus
on contemporary Jainism and its daily manifestations beyond the boundaries
of South Asia. Her talk is titled, *O Maharaj! Take these lemons – Jains
and Jainism in the Western Indian Ocean*.
