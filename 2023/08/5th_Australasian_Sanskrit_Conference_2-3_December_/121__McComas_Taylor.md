Dear friends and colleagues,

I'm pleased to announce the 5th Australasian Sanskrit Conference to be held in Canberra 2-3 December 2023 under the auspices of the International Association of Sanskrit Studies. It is primarily aimed at Sanskritists in Australia and New Zealand, but is of course open to any scholar from further afield. All are welcome to attend. I sincerely hope that you can join us face-to-face.

Please download the information sheet here:  https://drive.google.com/file/d/1fiB0n7ntr7sZat25_IeJJiznc8FbXYti/view?usp=drive_link


Yours sincerely,
McComas

PS. This is not to be confused with the 19th World Sanskrit Conference in Kathmandu, 17-21 December 2024  - see you there as well!
