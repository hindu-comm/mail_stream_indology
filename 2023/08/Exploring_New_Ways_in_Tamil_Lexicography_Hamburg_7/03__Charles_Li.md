Dear everyone,

The Tamilex project is pleased to announce its inaugural conference, 
"Exploring New Ways in Tamil Lexicography," to take place from 7–9 
August 2023, in Hamburg and online. For the full program and to register 
for online attendance, go to 
https://www.csmc.uni-hamburg.de/ends/events.html
