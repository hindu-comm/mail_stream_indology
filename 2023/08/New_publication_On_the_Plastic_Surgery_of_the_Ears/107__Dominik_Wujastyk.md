I am pleased to announce the publication of the book *On the Plastic
Surgery of the Ears and Nose*. It is now available online and in print. The
open access edition can be accessed via the following DOI:
https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1203

   - Dominik Wujastyk, Jason Birch, Andrey Klebanov, Madhu K. Parameswaran,
   Madhusudan Rimal, Deepro Chakraborty, Harshal Bhatt, Vandana Lele, and
   Paras Mehta. On the Plastic Surgery of the Ears and Nose. The Nepalese
   Version of the Suśrutasaṃhitā. Heidelberg: Heidelberg Asian Studies
   Publishing, 2023. https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1203.

Best wishes,
