on behalf of the project’s investigators, I am sharing here the .pdf of a call for applications for a position of research assistant (‘‘Assegno per lo svolgimento di attività di ricerca’’) in the framework of the PRIN2022 project « The Wheel of Time: An Inquiry into New Sources of Late Indian Buddhism (X-XIII cents.) ». I am told that, while the project is based at the University of Naples « L’Orientale » and the University of Florence, there is no obligation for the hired scholar to be based in Italy.
