Dear Colleagues,

this is perhaps a good occasion to point out that there are many Fair 
Open Access (FOA) alternatives to the big publisher(s) that do not 
charge extremely high fees and prices for their services and products:

https://foasas.org/#journals

The “Fair” in “Fair Open Access” is important because FOA allows people 
from diverse backgrounds to access as well as contribute to scholarly 
literature. Since we launched the FOA Initiative three years ago, we 
have found that scholars are increasingly interested in such 
alternatives, and many excellent papers have been published in FOA 
journals since then.

Best regards,
Dominik A. Haas and Vitus Angermeier
