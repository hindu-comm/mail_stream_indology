Dear Colleagues

After a delay that was much too long, we are pleased to announce that the International Association of Sanskrit Studies' first Directory of University Sanskrit Programs is now available through the IASS website:

https://sanskritassociation.org/directory.php

We hope that the directory will help prospective students to find a suitable home. It will enable and encourage collaboration between universities and will also enable the IASS monitor the overall health of our discipline.

If you would like to update your entry or add your institution, please follow the link at the bottom of the page.

Thanks to everyone who contributed data to this world first.

Yours sincerely,

McComas
IASS Secretary-General
