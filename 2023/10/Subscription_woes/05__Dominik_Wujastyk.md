Dear colleagues,

Our INDOLOGY subscription system has been badly spammed over the last week
or two. We are seeing many hundreds of false bot-generated subscription
requests.

After discussion, the committee has decided to change the method of
subscription, at least until we have some spam protection.

For now, at least, new members are asked simply to send their CV to the
INDOLOGY committee:

   - https://indology.info/subscribing-to-the-forum/

That's all.  The committee will handle the rest manually.

The online documentation has been altered accordingly.

If you know of anyone who has subscribed in the last couple of weeks and
heard nothing back from us, please ask them to visit the URL above and
start again. Their genuine request has probably been lost in the maelstrom
of spam.

Best wishes,

Dominik Wujastyk
INDOLOGY list <http://indology.info> committee member
