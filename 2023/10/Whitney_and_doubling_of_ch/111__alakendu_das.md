Mr. Spier,
What we have learnt from Indological studies, Vedas were never written. We call it "Apaurasheya"... i. e. not written.The Vedic Mantras came as revealations to the seers( we call them 'Drashta" i. e. one who sees) while in Meditation.The hymns or mantras were then recited orally and passed on across generations of disciples. who memorised them.Finally, sage Vyasa arranged a compilation and divided them among his 4 disciples namely, Poilo, Boishampayan, Jaimini and Sumanta.Thus we got the 4 Vedas
In the 19th Century, Max Mueller edited theVedas.
Regards
Alakendu Das.
