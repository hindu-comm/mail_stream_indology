Subject: [INDOLOGY] Why is this hymn an aṣṭakam?


Dear list members,
I've been asked why the title of this hymn issūryamaṇḍalāṣṭakam, when it has twelve verses and a phalam verse. Written after the collophon issūryamaṇḍaladvādaśastotram . I do not know where that came from, whether from the transcriber or from what he was transcribing.
Is it possible that the aṣṭakam in the title doesn't refer to the number of verses but is a qualifier ofsūryamaṇḍala in the title? I.e.sūryamaṇḍalāṣṭakam means "the eight circles of the sun" ?




The hymn is:
