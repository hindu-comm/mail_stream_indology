Dear list members,
It is our great pleasure to announce volume 16 of the series Ethno-Indology: Heidelberg Studies in South Asian Rituals, edited by Ute Hüsken and Axel Michaels.
The Play of the Feminine: Navarātri in Kanchipuram<https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1167>, by Ina Marie Lunde Ilkama. Heidelberg: Heidelberg Asian Studies Publishing, 2023.
(Ethno-Indology: Heidelberg Studies in South Asian Rituals ; 16 ISBN 978-3-948791-59-9 (Hardcover), ISBN 978-3-948791-60-5 (PDF), DOI: https://doi.org/10.11588/hasp.1167

We are pleased to announce that with this volume the series has found a new home at Heidelberg Asian Studies Publishing (HASP), Heidelberg University, following its tenure under the esteemed publisher, Harrassowitz.
On the HASP platform<https://hasp.ub.uni-heidelberg.de/>, the PDF version this book and all further volumes in this series are available for free download. A hardcover edition also has been published, which can be purchased via stationary bookshops and online booksellers.
Furthermore, we are delighted to acknowledge the commitment of the members of the newly established editorial board (Jörg Gengnagel, Universität Würzburg; Vera Lazzaretti (Centro em Rede de Investigação em Antropologia, Lisboa; Angelika Malinar, Universität Zürich; Alexander von Rospatt, UC Berkeley; Caleb Simmons; The University of Arizona, Tucson; Astrid Zotter, Universität Heidelberg), who have kindly agreed to contribute their expertise and guidance.

Ute Hüsken and Axel Michaels
