Dear all,

the Zurich Paippalada project has made important manuscripts of the
Odisha Paippalada tradition publicly available. The original photographs
were provided by Arlo Griffith. Thomas Zehnder has, in quite painstaking
work, compiled reference tables that are very convenient to quickly
locate passages in the manuscripts. The whole collection is available on
SWISSUBase:

https://www.swissubase.ch/en/catalogue/studies/20544/19570/overview

For an overview, please refer to the documentation found here (click on
the link "File" in the left part of the window):

https://www.swissubase.ch/en/catalogue/studies/20544/19570/datasets/2428/2870/files/document/31265/13817/overview


Best, Oliver
