Dear colleagues,

The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is pleased to announce the second 
round (2024) of its *postdoctoral fellowship for recent PhDs*. Details 
on the fellowship, information on requirements, and application form can 
be found on its website *www.rocherfoundation.org*. Note the application 
deadline of *15 January 2024.*

Please display the attached poster and/or forward this announcement to 
potential candidates.

With thanks and best wishes,

Rosane Rocher
Professor Emerita of South Asia Studies
University of Pennsylvania
President
The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation
