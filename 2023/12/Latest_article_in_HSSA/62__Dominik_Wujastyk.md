The journal HSSA <http://hssa-journal.org> is delighted to publish a new
research article by A. J. Misra and J. Arzoumanov entitled "Calendars,
Compliments and Computations"  https://doi.org/10.18732/hssa95  This paper
 presents, for the very first time, a comparative survey of the canon of
two seventeenth-century works on astronomy, one in Persian and one in
Sanskrit, to reveal the intimacy between the two works and to analyze the
process of linguistic and cultural translation between these two profound
cultures of India.

--
