*One-Day Online International Symposium on the Studies in the Aṣṭādhyāyī*




*Organised by: Amrita Darshanam ICSS, Amrita Vishwa VidyapeethamUnder the
Project- The Critical Edition of the Aṣṭādhyāyī(funded by the IKS division
of the AICTE under their scheme of IKS centre)*

We are currently working on a project to publish a critical edition of the
Aṣṭādhyāyī of Pāṇini, funded by the IKS division of the AICTE. To fulfil
the goals of our project and disseminate the knowledge of the studies in
the field of Sanskrit Grammar, we are organizing a “One-Day Online
International Symposium on the Studies in the Aṣṭādhyāyī.”

We are blessed to have these eminent resource persons for the
symposium: *(Please
read the attached brochure for more details)*
