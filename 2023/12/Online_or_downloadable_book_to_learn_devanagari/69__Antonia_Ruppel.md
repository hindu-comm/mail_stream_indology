Dear Harry,

The chapters going with the video Tim mentioned are available as pdf a
download online:

https://www.cambridge.org/de/universitypress/subjects/religion/buddhism-and-eastern-religions/cambridge-introduction-sanskrit?format=HB&isbn=9781107088283#resources

The code to freely use the flash cards going with them is
www.brainscape.com/p/16X72-LH-49PH8 . (You need to open a free account
first and then use this code.)

This isn't a book, but a website, but in my view it's the best resources
available for practicing devanagari online:
https://www.hindibhasha.com/hindiscripttutor.htm

Hope this helps!
      Antonia
