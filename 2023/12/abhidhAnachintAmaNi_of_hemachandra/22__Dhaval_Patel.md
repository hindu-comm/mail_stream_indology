Dear scholars,
I had earlier notified the group regarding availability of
Abhidhānachintāmaṇi of Hemachandrācārya.

This is in continuation with the previous notice.
Now this dictionary is provided with headwords.
The dictionary is now available in various formats at the following links.

1. Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries.
One can search the headwords at
https://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/scans/ABCHScan/2023/web/webtc2/index.php

2. Stardict files.
For offline view, one can download stardict files from
https://github.com/indic-dict/stardict-sanskrit/tree/gh-pages/sa-head/sa-entries/tars

Hope these two formats will be useful to the scholars.
