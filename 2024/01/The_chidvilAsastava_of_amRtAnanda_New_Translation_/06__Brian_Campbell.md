Dear friends,

I'm happy to share with you a new translation of a Śrīvidyā classic, the
"Cidvilāsastava" of Amṛtānanda by Dr. Ben Williams. This new translation
follows the critical edition of the Cidvilāsastava, as edited and published
by Professor Vrajvallabha Dwivedi in his 1985 edition of the
Nityāṣoḍaśikārṇavatantra, and is being published open-access by an emerging
group of Śrīvidyā practitioners. Although specifically oriented towards
practitioners of the vidyā, it will likely be of interest to scholars of
Śaiva tantra and Śrīvidyā as well.

https://tripuratallika.org/cidvilasastava

Best wishes,
Brian Campbell
