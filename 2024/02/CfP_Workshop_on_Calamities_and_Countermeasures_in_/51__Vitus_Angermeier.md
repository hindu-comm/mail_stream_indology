Dear colleagues,

As part of the project "Epidemics and Crisis Management in Pre-modern 
South Asia" a workshop on "Calamities and Countermeasures in Pre-modern 
South Asia" will take place in Vienna and online on November 8–9, 2024. 
Please find more details about the event in the attachment or on the 
project website: 
https://epidemics.univie.ac.at/the-project/calamities-workshop/.

If you want to participate in this workshop, please submit a title and 
an abstract of approximately 300 words by March 15, 2024. Kindly send 
your submission to vitus.angermeier at unvie.ac.at, with the subject phrase 
"calamities workshop submission".

If your proposal is accepted, we expect to receive a first draft of your 
paper until September 15, for pre-circulation among the other 
participants. Further details will be provided with the final programme 
in April.
The workshop will be held in hybrid format. This means that if you are 
unable to come to Vienna due to appointments or other difficulties, it 
will also be possible to participate online. If you have further 
questions about the event, please do not hesitate to contact me.

With best regards,
Vitus Angermeier
