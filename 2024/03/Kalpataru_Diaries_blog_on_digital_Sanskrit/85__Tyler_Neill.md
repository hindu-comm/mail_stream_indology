Dear List,

I've just started a new blog on topics in Sanskrit digital humanities,
called Kalpataru Diaries, on my personal website. The first few posts are
getting the project warmed up by introducing my own projects, but then the
main idea is to move from there into offering practical commentary on other
projects and on the field as a whole. It's meant to be instructional,
helping people stay informed and try things out if they're interested.

kind of thing!
