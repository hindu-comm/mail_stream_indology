Dear colleagues,

I would like to make you aware of a series of online talks and discussions
on the history of science in early South Asia, convened by Dr Lisa Brooks
and myself and hosted by the Consortium for History of Science, Technology,
and Medicine:

https://www.chstm.org/early-south-asia

Our next speaker is Anna Winterbottom (McGill university), who will give a

Please see the website cited above for the abstract for this talk.

All talks and discussions are online and joining is free. However, to
access the zoom link, you need to sign up to our group on CHSTM here:
https://www.chstm.org/user/register

We look forward to welcoming you to our working group!
