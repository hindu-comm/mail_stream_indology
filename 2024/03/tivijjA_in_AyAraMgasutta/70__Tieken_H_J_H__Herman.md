Dear list members,

in the first suyakkhaṃdha of the Āyāraṃgasutta the word tivijjā occurs three times. In Buddhism tevijja is a topic by itself (Tevijjasutta). I have, however, been unable to find more information about it in Jaina texts. In Āyāra 1. 3. 2, su. 112 it is preceded by the following three terms, namely jāti, vuḍḍhi and sāta (Skt svāda). Could these three words be the topics headed by tivijjā? Or else, where can I find more information about tivijjā in Jaina texts?
