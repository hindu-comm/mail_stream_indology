Dear colleagues,

The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is pleased to announce that the 
2024 Postdoctoral Fellowship has been awarded to Dr. Tamara Cohen (Ph.D. 
in the Study of Religion, University of Toronto, April 2023). Dr. Cohen 
will pursue her project to map the cosmological system of the early 
Pratyabhijñā philosophy of Utpaladeva with that contained in the 10^th 
century text the Mokṣopāya. She has opted to be in residence at the 
University of Virginia for the duration of her fellowship.

Please visit our website rocherfoundation.org

for information on our several activities and for parameters and 
guidance on applications for the next round of postdoctoral fellowships,
