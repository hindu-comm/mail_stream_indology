Dear Harry,

Sāyaṇa’s commentary on the Mahānārayaṇa-Upaniṣad is included in the 1898 
Ānandāśrama edition of the Taittirīya-Āraṇyaka (pp. 783–909), which you 
can find here: https://archive.org/details/dli.ernet.496133

Bhaṭṭabhāskara Miśra’s commentary is included in the Bibliotheca 
Sanskrita edition (vol. II, pp. 71–191): 
https://archive.org/details/Taittiriya-Aranyaka

Best regards,
D.
