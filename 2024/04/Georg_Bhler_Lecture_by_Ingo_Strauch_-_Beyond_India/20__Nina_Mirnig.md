Dear colleagues, 

We are delighted to invite you to this year’s Georg Bühler Lecture of the Austrian Academy of Sciences, which will be delivered by Ingo Strauch on the topic "Beyond India – About scratchings, scribblings and Buddhists in the west of the Indian ocean“. For more information, please see the flyer here <https://www.oeaw.ac.at/fileadmin/NEWS/2024/pdf/Einladungskarte-Buehler-Lecture-Ingo_Strauch-V26-03-2024.pdf>.

The lecture will be held on Tuesday, 16th April at 17:00 CET and can also be followed online. For those in and near Vienna, please do join us in person at the Theatersaal of the Austrian Academy of Sciences (Sonnenfelsgasse 19, 1010 Vienna)! 

For online and in person attendance, please register here <https://www.oeaw.ac.at/veranstaltungen/anmeldung/georg-buehler-lectures>.

With best wishes, 

Nina Mirnig and Hannes Fellner
