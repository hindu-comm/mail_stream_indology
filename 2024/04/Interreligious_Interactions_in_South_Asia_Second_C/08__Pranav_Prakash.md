Dear Colleagues and Friends,



As a gentle reminder, we would like to invite you to attend our second
virtual colloquium on “*Interreligious Interactions in South Asia.*” Our
colloquium will take place over Zoom from *April 3 to April 12, 2024*. Please
find the flyer and program of our colloquium as attachments here.

*Abdul Manan Bhat *| *Postures of Tradition: Corporeality and Islamic

April 5, 2024 |  16:00–17:30 BST / 11:00–12:30 EDT / 20:30–22:00 IST
