Dear All,

I just received information that the French Sanskrit and Vedic scholar
Michel Angot passed away in his home town on 8 April 2024 at the age of 75 (
https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michel_Angot). A number of his important
publications are listed on his own website (https://www.michelangot.com/),
which also mentions several other ones still under preparation.
Condolences to his family and to his numerous students in France and world
wide, who will always remember him for his enthusiastic and inspiring
teaching and lecturing, including in the domain of theoretical and
practical aspects of Vedic recitation.

Jan Houben
