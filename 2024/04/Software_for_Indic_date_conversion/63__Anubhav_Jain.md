Dear Peter,

There is a free tool that can be used to convert Indic calendar dates to
the Gregorian calendar and vice versa.

Link:
https://ancient-buddhist-texts.net/Reference/Christian-Buddhist-Conversion.htm

It provides conversion of dates among multiple calendars including the Common
Era, Sri Lankan and Myanmar Buddhist Era, Thai, Laos and Cambodian Buddhist
Era, Saka Varṣa, Cūḷasakarat, Buddhist Kāḷiyuga, Hindu Kāḷiyuga, Mahā Era,
Vikram Samvat.

[Note: Mahā Era is not Mahāvīra era.]

Regards,
Anubhav
