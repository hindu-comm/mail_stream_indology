Dear All:

Congratulations to Prof. Subhasini Kaligotla for receiving the 2024 Alice Davis Hitchcock Book Award from the Society of Architectural Historians for her book Shiva’s Waterfront Temples: Architects and their Audiences in Medieval India (New Haven and London: Yale University Press, 2022). The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is especially pleased at Subhashini’s award, because it supported the publication of this fabulous book under its “First Book Subvention” program. We encourage others with first books inching toward publication to contact us for possible support of your book.

With best wishes,

Patrick Olivelle
