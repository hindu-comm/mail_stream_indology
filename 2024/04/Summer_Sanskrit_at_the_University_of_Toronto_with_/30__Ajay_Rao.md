Dear colleagues,





I am writing to ask if you can share the poster below with prospective students. We are excited to be offering an in-person intermediate summer Sanskrit reading course again this year at the University of Toronto taught by Prof. Christopher Minkowski.



The course will take place at the Mississauga campus from June 26 to August 10, 2023. Interested candidates should apply by sending an unofficial transcript and a 200-word statement about research interests to utmla at utoronto.ca<mailto:utmla at utoronto.ca> by April 26, 2023.


For more information about the course please contact me at ajay.rao at utoronto.ca<mailto:ajay.rao at utoronto.ca>





Thank you,

Associate Professor, Historical Studies, University of Toronto
