Dear colleagues,

Is this stanza known to anyone from any Indian source?

dr̥ṣṭānubhūtam arthaṁ yaḥ pr̥ṣṭo na vinigūhate |
yathābhūtapravāditvād ity etat satyalakṣaṇam ||

It is stanza 139 in

Raghu Vira. 1962. Sāra-Samuccaya (a Classical Indonesian Compendium of High Ideals). Śata-Piṭaka Series (Dvīpāntara-Piṭaka), 24 (7). New Delhi: International Academy of Indian Culture.

Almost all stanzas in this compendium can be traced in the Mahābhārata. But not the above stanza.

Thank you.

Arlo Griffiths
