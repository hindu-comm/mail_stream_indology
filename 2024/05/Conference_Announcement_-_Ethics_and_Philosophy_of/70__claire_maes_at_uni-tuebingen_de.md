Dear Colleagues,

You are cordially invited to attend in person or online our upcoming
conference on Ethics and Philosophy of Extreme Fasting on Friday May 31 and
Saturday June 1 in Bern. This event is a collaboration between the
Department of Indology of the University of Tübingen and the Institute for
the Science of Religion of the University of Bern. 

This international conference is the first of its kind to explore a network
of pertinent questions surrounding various types of extreme fasting
practices from an interdisciplinary and transcultural perspective. The first
two panels will deal with extreme fasting practices in religious and
political contexts. The third panel connects the different case studies by
offering a systematic comparison.
