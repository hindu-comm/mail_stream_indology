Esteemed Scholars and Participants,

After thorough deliberation and in response to the expressed needs of the scholarly community, who found the December dates rather inconvenient to travel to Kathmandu, we have made the decision to postpone the 19th World Sanskrit Conference. The conference will now take place from June 26th to June 30th, 2025.


[https://58iocbengaluru.com/btn.png]<http://tracking.19thwsc2024.org/tracking/click?d=g4ksp9RdMQ4lYcME25xm6EOWaOmIpeWY64bfGWRALQ57HGNGKVn0D6TMbluksCEq3NVZQX75E7KB2ogs2s-vH4ee-rev66G2sFkFpARokVRXHp-FkZYrym06ZSWGuPdyhzxkQhKM8OtHoIJmx8P4Ni0jYtborYne4JcSVax1uj517Au1RPUGeQgDi3h2DgQ7pCEJuxVJqji8A6T8X8jQy_Y1>

In light of this change, abstract submissions will remain open until August 15th, 2024. We encourage all interested participants to take advantage of this extended deadline. Detailed information regarding abstract submission (along with newly added special panels) and other crucial deadlines can be found in the third circular.

Additionally, we are pleased to announce that registration for the conference (with updated, reduced prices) is open on our website.
