Dear Indologists,

We are delighted to inform you that an international conference on Tense,
Aspect, and Modality in South Asian languages, both *ancient and modern*,
will take place at the National Institute of Oriental Languages and
Civilizations (INALCO), Paris, France, from October 29th to October 31st,
2024.

Abstracts for individual presentations are welcome until June 30, 2024. We
accept submissions that discuss current research on Tense, Aspect, and
Modality in South Asian languages.

For further details, please visit: https://tamsa2024.sciencesconf.org/

Regards,

Ghanshyam Sharma

INALCO, Paris, France
