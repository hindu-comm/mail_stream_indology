Dear list members,

A bug has been found in the Muktabodha searchable etext library that causes
lines beginning with an asterisk (used to indicate unreadable letters)
aren't displayed in the unicode transliteration files or converted to
devanagari in the devanagari files.

This bug has now been fixed but unfortunately it means that the cumulative
download will have to be recreated from scratch.  This will probably take
from several weeks to a month.

Harry Spier
