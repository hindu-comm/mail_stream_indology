We are pleased to announce that enrollment is now open at Yogic Studies for our Fall term online Elementary and Intermediate language programs. The courses run for 12 or 9 weeks, respectively, and are part of a three-term series. Students can enroll for each term individually, or for the entire series. Classes are held live on Zoom twice per week with sessions recorded for later viewing. 

All of our language courses are taught by highly regarded instructors who approach their online courses with the same expectations and pedagogical rigor as they do their university courses.
