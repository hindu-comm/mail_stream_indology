Dear list members,

 Āgamavacanasaṅgraha, śrīcakranavāvaraṇapūjā, and Navagrahadhyānam from the
collaborative Muktabodha/IFP project have been added to the Muktabodha
searchable etext  library.  The description of these three texts by Dr.
Deviprasad Mishra is:

RE 20082 - The title of this bundle is Āgamavacanasaṅgraha. It starts with
some dhyānā ślokās but it mainly covers the Pratiṣṭhābhāga of Śaivāgama. It
also deals with the nyāsa technique which is used for the sake of nityapūja
and pratiṣṭha from different āgamās. The text mainly covers the
Adhvaṣaṭkanyāsam which means mantrādhvanyāsam, padādhvanyāsam,
varṇādhvanyāsam, bhuvanādhvanyāsam, tatvādhvanyāsam, kalādhvanyāsam, etc.
m. The condition of the folio is a little bit damaged but the letters are
very clean.

RE 31226   śrīcakranavāvaraṇapūjā  this text starts with Dīpapūja and then
continued to the dhyānās to the devatas which are there in the circles of
the nine (śrīcakranavāvaraṇa) also it recited the mūlamantrās and gurupūja
in between, all that covers the Devī (Goddess) (śrīcakra). That’s what it
is known as navāvaraṇapūjā.

RE 32425 Navagrahadhyānam. The text starts with the dhyāna ślokās of
Vināyaka and it continues providing prayer and praises to the nine planets.
It offers salutations to each planet in turn, describing their appearance
or association. The planets mentioned are the Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury,
Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu and Ketu. It also provides the ashtakam (eight
verses) of all the planets. There is also the description of offering of
Dīpa, dhupa and flowers to the planets as it has been described in the
Śaivāgama texts and also some vachana-s that describe the  way of pūja done
through Śaivāgamas.
