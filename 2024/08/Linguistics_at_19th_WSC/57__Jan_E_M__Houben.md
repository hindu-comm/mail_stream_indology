RE: Section of Linguistics, 19th World Sanskrit Conference in Kathmandu,
Nepal, 26-30 June 2025

Dear members of the Indology List and of BVParishat,

The conveners of the Linguistics Section at the 19th World Sanskrit
Conference in Kathmandu, Nepal, 26-30 June 2025, kindly invite scholars
working in the domain of Linguistics of Sanskrit (including Vedic Sanskrit)
to submit their abstract at the website:

https://www.nepalworldsanskrit.org/Home/Perabstractsubmision

Within the limit of 300 words please indicate clearly what the original
scientific contribution of your paper is to the chosen subject in the
domain of Linguistics.

Please note that the extended deadline is now set at 31 August 2024.
For the names of the conveners of all Sections, including Linguistics, see
now:

https://www.nepalworldsanskrit.org/Home/Sectionsandconvenors?Name=Sections%20and%20Conveners

With best regards, the conveners of the Section of Linguistics, 19th WSC,
Kathmandu, 26-30 June 2025.

Prof. John Lowe
Prof. Madhav Pokharel
Prof. Malhar Kulkarni
