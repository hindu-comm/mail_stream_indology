Dear list members,

11 new tantric etexts have been added to the Muktabodha searchable etext
library.  The cumulative zipfile download has also been updated to include
these.
The link to the library is:
https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/digital_library.htm

The etexts are:
1. *kaumāra-saṁhitā* from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library manuscript 4078

2. *mātṛkābhedatantram* from Calcutta Sanskrit Series vol. 7

 3. *mṛgendra-vṛtti* by bhaṭṭanārāyaṇakaṇṭha from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS
Library manuscript 4387

4. *paramahaṁsa-paddhati * from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library
manuscript 1065gh

5. *prāsādamaṇḍana* by sūtradhāramaṇḍana from The Kashmir Series of texts
and studies vol. 72

6 *ramārcana-candrikā* by ānandavanaḥ mukundavana-śiṣyaḥ from Shri
Raghunath Temple MSS Library manuscript 4740

7. *śivarahasya *part 1 from the 1913 edition by Ganesh Shastri and
Sundaresvara Shastri.

8.*śivasūtravṛtti *from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library manuscript 1672 kh

9. *svātantrya-dīpikā* from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library manuscript
1127 k

10.*tripurāsārasamuccaya* written by nāgabhaṭṭa, commentator Govindācāryya,
editor and publisher Jīvanānda Vidyāsāgara Bhaṭṭācāryya  1897.

11. *navārṇacaṁḍīpaṁcāṁgam* from Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library
manuscript 4818 gh

All Shri Raghunath Temple MSS Library manuscripts were copied from
archive.org from photographic copies generously provided by eGangotri.

Harry Spier
