Dear colleagues and friends,

I would like to draw your attention to the following conference at Bonn, which might be of interest to you if you would like to present material in the form of a research paper.
With best wishes,

Julia (A.B. Hegewald).




Dear BCDSS Members and Friends, 

I am happy to share with you the Call for Papers for the International Conference “Strong Asymmetrical Dependencies: Perspectives from Asia, Past & Present” which will take place in Bonn 06 – 08 May, 2025. 

We are looking for contributions on all of Asia and on all times and periods you can think of. We kindly ask you to spread the word and make everybody aware for whom this could be relevant. The conference aims at advancing scholarly dialogue and interdisciplinarity when thinking of strong asymmetrical dependency in Asia. 

Please find all the details here: https://www.dependency.uni-bonn.de/en/outreach/news-bcdss/call-for-papers-2 <https://www.dependency.uni-bonn.de/en/outreach/news-bcdss/call-for-papers-2>

Kind regards
Jeannine
