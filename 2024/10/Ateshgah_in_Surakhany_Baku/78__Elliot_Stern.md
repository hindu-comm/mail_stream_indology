Dear Andrea,

Justin E. Abbott, Indian Inscriptions on the Fire Temple at Bāku, JAOS 29 (available at https://archive.org/details/jstor-592636/mode/1up?view=theater) contains a photographic image of one inscription, with a translation.

Karam Tej Sarao <https://du-in.academia.edu/KaramTejSarao?swp=tc-au-107076573>, BAKU'S TEMPLE OF ETERNAL FIRE, 2021, includes a chapter on the inscriptions (available at https://www.academia.edu/107076573/BAKUS_TEMPLE_OF_ETERNAL_FIRE).

Best wishes,

Elliot
