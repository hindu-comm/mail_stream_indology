CALL FOR PAPER IS NO OPEN!!!!



Dear Colleague,



Greetings from Heidelberg. We are happy to announce that the academic committee has accepted a record 125 panels to form the basis of the conference program at ECSAS Heidelberg 2025. The call for papers will open on October 30, 2024. ECSAS invites scholars to submit paper abstracts that align with the panels that have been accepted by the academic committee, which you can see https://ecsas2025.com/panels/<https://tr.ccicongress.com/e/tr?q=7%3dRUHbW%26B%3dB%26H%3dTXJb%26C%3dQBVQTB%26O%3d6J5JG_Hbwn_SL_Losa_Vd_Hbwn_RQ91IlMPZCY.1Ex_Losa_VdFlH3B4_Losa_Vd%264%3d%26tM%3dOXGYS%26J4%3dVXREUXR5y1lJUUZCWU%268%3dEWQ1JWy4C6zWIbTSHd31EYT5J8yVIaQQJ022mU4QFbRSIX3SK7OQD9Q5C9SZHWWQ&mupckp=mupAtu4m8OiX0wt>.



The submission deadline for paper proposals is January 30, 2025 (midnight CET). All paper applications will be reviewed by the panel convenors. All proposals must be made via the online form on the ECSAS website https://registration.ccicongress.com/cmsweb/ABS_Login.asp?IDcommessa=C006/25&Lang=EN<https://tr.ccicongress.com/e/tr?q=A%3dJbHfO%26I%3dB%26L%3dLeJf%265%3dXBZIaB%26S%3dxQ5N9_Ob1f_ZL_Pgza_ZV_Ob1f_YQPuDtQ0OlRyLy.AsFnM4D3C9P.nM3_Ob1f_YQA3P8Cr_Ob1f_YQiRz_WMwFy.99M_3yiw_DnYknM3JpQ98_3yiw_DlSgKd_8xdx_HmCc_8xdx_HcW94D_3yiw_DlUu%26o%3d%26G9%3dXIdKb%265Q%3dHgCbGgCgGdKZId%26s%3dCs9q8lglcvaBZqYp0PcpaPgnbI8Bhq8IYIdEAL8CcN8Ibu8FhK9EDMAnDKbmhNcEeMBH&mupckp=mupAtu4m8OiX0wt>

This includes any papers by panel convenors.



Please keep in mind that all paper submitters, including co-presenters, must be members of the EASAS by the time of submission. Prospective panelists can join here: https://www.easas.eu/become-a-member<https://tr.ccicongress.com/e/tr?q=7%3dDW8bI%26D%3d2%26H%3dFZ0b%26y%3dS2VCV2%26O%3drLuJ3_JRwZ_UB_LauQ_VP_JRwZ_TGQ7O.f533t.95_JRwZ_TG6o5pGo-3-n9w4fL%26n%3d%26At%3dUHX6Y%264K%3d3dBV2dB5k3ba2aJT4a%26r%3d74VETA9G44ZkZg0p82ak74WIVgZAUeZoSdbEW78BSeVCW66B660H72bEb5UmU56l&mupckp=mupAtu4m8OiX0wt>.

It is technically possible for one person to submit up to three paper proposals to different panels, but due to overwhelming demand, we have a strict limit of one panel presentation per person at ECSAS Heidelberg. Any scholars accepted in more than one panel will be required to choose only one.

All papers must be submitted in English. The title should not exceed 150 characters (incl. spaces). Each abstract should not exceed 1500 characters (incl. spaces). The content must be original and not presented previously. Regretfully, we do not have the resources for online presentations.



Do not hesitate to contact the Organizing Secretariat at info at ecsas2025.com<mailto:info at ecsas2025.com> if you have any further questions.

We are looking forward to seeing you in Heidelberg,

the ECSAS Heidelberg 2025 Conference Team
