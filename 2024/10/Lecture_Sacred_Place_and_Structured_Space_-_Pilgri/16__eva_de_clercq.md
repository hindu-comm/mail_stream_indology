Dear colleagues,


We are glad to announce that the third Ācārya Mahāprajña Annual Lecture in Jain Studies will take place on Tuesday October 22nd, from 4:30 pm CET.

After an introduction delivered by myself, Prof. Dr. Julia Hegewald (University of Bonn) will give a talk titled ‘Sacred Place and Structured Space – Pilgrimage in Jainism’.

The event will be held on-campus at Blandijnberg 2 – 9000 Ghent, but will also be accessible online (Zoom).

Detailed information will be circulated after registration.



Registration for on-campus attendance: https://forms.gle/dDBN8EeVSWjYJ7tj6
