Dear Oliver and all,

Yes, in 2020 it was also from Anna Esposito that I received the scan of
Gonda's *Stylistic Repetition*... (thanks again!), where Gonda investigates
phenomena that could perhaps be better explored now with the electronic
texts and research tools currently available. Or perhaps Gonda's research
of 1959 cannot be improved upon with these tools because of the semantic
dimension taken into account by Gonda. Thanks to Lubomir Ondracka for
uploading the book on Archive.org, let's hope it remains functional as it
is now.

In the meantime a new request to the list:
I have not been able to find a scan of Jan Gonda's *Reflections on the
Numerals "One" and "Two" in Ancient Indo-European Languages*, published in
his home town and town of the University where he was teaching since 1932,
Utrecht (publication date: 1953, 80 pages).
