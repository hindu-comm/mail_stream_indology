Dear All,

We are delighted to invite you to a webinar with *Prof. Mrinal Kaul*,
Assistant Professor of Indian Philosophy at IIT-Bombay. In this session,
Prof. Kaul will explore the theme of *Abhinavagupta and Non-Duality.* The
discussion will highlight how Abhinavagupta’s unified meta-category of
non-dual consciousness bridges binaries while emphasizing inclusivism and
pluralistic forms of singular consciousness. It will also delve into
connections to modern ideas such as pluralistic non-dualism and subjective
pluralism, as well as the relevance of his philosophy in contemporary
contexts.

*Date:* 29 November
*Time:* 2-3 pm
*Zoom:*  https://flameuniversity.zoom.us/j/96316786125
