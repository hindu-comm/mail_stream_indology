Dear list members,
In addition to the cumulative download feature there is now an individual
file download feature available for the Muktabodha digital library.

Clicking on these  links (also on the searchable digital library entry
page)
For IAST transliteration:
https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/MUKTABODHA-LIBRARY-IAST/UTF8-TITLE-LINK-LIST.html

For devanagari:

https://muktalib7.com/DL_CATALOG_ROOT/MUKTABODHA-LIBRARY-DEVANAGARI/DEV-TITLE-LINK-LIST.html

Clicking on these links bring you to a page of direct download links to the
individual files . This is for individuals who just want to download a text
and don't need the searchable interface.
