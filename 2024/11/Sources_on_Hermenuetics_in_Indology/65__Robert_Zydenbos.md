The great classic of hermeneutics in Indology is the famous book by 
Wilhelm Halbfass (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilhelm_Halbfass): 
_Indien und Europa, Perspektiven ihrer geistigen Begegnung_ ("India and 
Europe. Perspectives of their inner encounter", 1981), translated and 
slightly extended as _India and Europe: An Essay in Understanding_ 
(1988; a different edition has the subtitle "An Essay in Philosophical 
Understanding"). I have always recommended this book to all my students.

RZ
