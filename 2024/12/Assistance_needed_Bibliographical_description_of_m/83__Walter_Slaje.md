A quick look suggests that

Ms 01 is a fragment of the Saundaryalaharī,
Ms 02 is a fragment of Annambhaṭṭa's Tarkasaṅgraha,
Ms 04 belongs to the genre of parācikarma ritual texts (last rites and
śrāddha ceremonies), and Ms 03 seems to belong to it.
