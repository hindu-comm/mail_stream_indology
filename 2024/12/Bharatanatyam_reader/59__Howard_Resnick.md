Apropos of this topic, I am providing here a link to the “Jagannātha-priya-nāṭakam”, written by a very dear friend who died tragically in a car crash in West Bengala in 2001, just weeks before he was to receive his PhD in Indology at Cambridge, working under Professor Julius Lipner.

In this book, Tamāla Kṛṣṇa Gosvāmī wrote an original play in which he sought to strictly follow traditonal rules of Indian dramaturgy.

https://www.amazon.com/Jagannatha-Jagannatha-Natakam-English-Krishna/dp/B09BLFD1YT￼
The Drama of Lord Jagannatha (Jagannatha- Priya Natakam) English Tamal Krishna Goswami
amazon.com

Best wishes,
Howard
