Dear colleagues,

We cordially invite you to participate in an evaluation study of the Qalamos portal<https://www.qalamos.net/content/index.xed?lang=en> for access to metadata and digital copies of manuscripts from Asian and African writing traditions. As part of a DFG-funded project, the portal is currently being further developed and your feedback is crucial for adapting the platform even better to the needs of the community.

As the current phase of the project focuses on South Asian languages, we are looking for researchers who specialize in this area and who are willing to take part in an online interview lasting approximately 60 minutes. We would like to talk to you about your experiences with the portal as well as your suggestions and ideas for improvement. In return for your participation in the interview, we offer you the opportunity to have a manuscript of your choice from the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin's collection digitized.

If we have been able to spark your interest, please register to take part in the interview study by completing the survey below. Completing the survey will take a maximum of 5 minutes and will help us to gain an initial impression of your use of the portal. We will also ask for your e-mail address so that we can arrange an appointment with you for the online interview.

Survey link: https://umfrage.hu-berlin.de/index.php/556617?lang=en

Thank you in advance for your valuable support!

With kind regards,

Your Qalamos team


--------------------------------------
Note: The evaluation study is being conducted by Prof. Dr. Elke Greifeneder and Dr. Kirsten Schlebbe (Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin) in close cooperation with the Qalamos team. The overall responsibility for the Qalamos project lies with the Oriental Department of the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin - Stiftung Preußischer Kulturbesitz.

If you have any questions about the study or technical problems relating to the survey, please do not hesitate to contact us via e-mail: schlebbe at ibi.hu-berlin.de<mailto:schlebbe at ibi.hu-berlin.de>
