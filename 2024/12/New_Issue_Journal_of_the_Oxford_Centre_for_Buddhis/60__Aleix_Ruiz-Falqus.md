Dear friends and colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the publication of Volume 24 of the _Journal 
of the Oxford Centre for Buddhist Studies_(ISSN: 2047-1076), available 
online: http://jocbs.org/index.php/jocbs/issue/view/27

This issue includes the following articles:

* Alexander Wynne, "Proto-Madhyamaka in the Pāli Canon Revisited: Early 
Buddhism, Gandhāra and the Origin of the Prajñāpāramitā"

* Nir Feinberg, "Turmoil and Tranquility in the _Attadaṇḍa Sutta_: A 
Study of Emotions in Early Buddhist Scripture"

* Bryan G. Levman, "Pāli Variants: A Typology (Part I)"

* Bernat Font, "Is joy (_pīti_) a feeling (_vedanā_)? Perspectives from 
early Buddhism"

* Brian Victoria, "Can Karma Cause Suffering?"

And one book review:

* Aleix Ruiz-Falqués, "Review: Javier Schnake (ed.), _Saddhammakitti's 
_Ekakkharakosa_ and its _Ṭīkā (Pali Text Society, 2024)"

The JOCBS accepts articles, notes, review articles and book reviews in 
any area of Buddhist studies. The editorial board especially encourages 
submissions pertaining to Pali and Southeast Asian Buddhism on a broad 
range of disciplinary interests. For more information, please visit: 
http://jocbs.org/index.php/jocbs/about
-- 
Aleix Ruiz-Falqués
