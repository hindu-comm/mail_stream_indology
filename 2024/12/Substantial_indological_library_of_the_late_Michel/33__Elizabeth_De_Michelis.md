Dear All,Former students of the late Michel Angot are taking care of what remains available of his prodigious Indological library, about 2,000 choice volumes, which is now looking for a home. The best thing would be to keep the whole collection together. An Excel list of the titles has been compiled, which can be forwarded to those who may be interested. Alternatively, the students may be able to help with actually carrying out or sponsoring its transport if the collection were to be moved relatively locally (say France, Italy, Switzerland....).If you would like to receive the list and/or discuss the matter, please get in touch directly (in English, French or Italian) with the following person:Antonio email address:  sanscrito1010 at gmail.comCordially,Elizabeth De Michelis
