Dear colleagues,

I am writing to ask if you can share the poster below with interested students. We are excited to offer in-person intermediate summer Sanskrit again this year at the University of Toronto taught by Prof. Christopher Minkowski.

There is no tuition for this course, which will take place at the Mississauga campus from June 23 to July 31, 2025. Applicants should send an unofficial transcript and a 200-word statement about research interests to utmla at utoronto.ca<mailto:utmla at utoronto.ca> before April 30, 2025.

For more information about the course please contact me at ajay.rao at utoronto.ca<mailto:ajay.rao at utoronto.ca>

Thank you,
Ajay Rao
