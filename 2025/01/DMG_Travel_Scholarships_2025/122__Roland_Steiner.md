Dear List Members,

On behalf of Professor Weninger, President of the Deutsche  
Morgenländische Gesellschaft (DMG, German Oriental Society), I would  
like to pass on the following information:

* * *

Each year, the Deutsche Morgenländische Gesellschaft offers up to  
three travel scholarships to junior researchers who specialize in a  
humanities branch of Oriental Studies. Applicants need to hold a  
Master’s or equivalent degree.

Scholarships include an allowance of up to 2000 Euros for travels that  
aim at an improvement of language skills and knowledge of the country,  
and which are designed specifically for the purpose of furthering an  
envisaged scientific qualification or a particular research project  
(e.g., archives, libraries, field research) related to the respective  
country of specialization.

Applications (CV, outline of the aims of the project, and  
justification of the need to undertake travel) are to be submitted, no  
later than the 1st of March of each year, to the First Chairman of the  
DMG:

Prof. Dr. Stefan Weninger
Centrum für Nah- und Mittelost-Studien
Philipps-Universität Marburg
Deutschhausstr. 12, 35032 Marburg, Germany.

Submissions received after this deadline will be considered for the  
following year.

Further Information:
https://dmg-web.de/en/sponsorship/travel-scholarships/

* * *

This scholarship is open to applicants of any nationality and from any  
country.
