Dear list members,

After a record 125 panels were accepted for the European Conference of South Asian Studies (ECSAS Heidelberg 2025) which will take place between October 1-4, 2025, we are excited to receive your paper proposals. The last date to make your submissions is January 30, 2025 (midnight CET). If you are yet to send in your proposals, please make sure to do so before the deadline via the online submission portal:Submit Your Paper Here<https://registration.ccicongress.com/cmsweb/ABS_Login.asp?IDcommessa=257006&Lang=EN>.

Please note that a membership to the EASAS (European Association for South Asian Studies) is mandatory for panel and paper submissions. More details on the submission process can be foundhere<https://ecsas2025.com/call-for-panels/>.

For more information on the conference panels, please refer to the list of accepted panels on the ECSAS 2025 website: Conference Panels<https://ecsas2025.com/panels/>

If you have any questions or require further assistance, feel free to reach out to the Organizing Secretariat at info at ecsas2025.com<mailto:info at ecsas2025.com>.

We look forward to your contributions and to welcoming you in Heidelberg.

Warm wishes,
ECSAS Heidelberg 2025 Conference Team
