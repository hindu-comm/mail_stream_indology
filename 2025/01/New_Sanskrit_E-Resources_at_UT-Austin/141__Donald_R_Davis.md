Dear Colleagues,

The searchable Sanskrit e-texts available through UT-Austin’s Sanskrit program continue to grow. Patrick Olivelle has just made his edition of the Early Upaniṣads available and the list of available Dharmaśāstra now includes many more commentaries and digests. Please remember that these texts are always works-in-progress and likely contain typographical errors. Therefore, please consult an original print source to ensure correct citation.


  *   Early Upaniṣads (Olivelle edition)<https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/early-upanisads-olivelle-edition/>
  *   Resource Library for Dharmaśāstra Studies<https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/dharmasastra/>

Happy searching!

Best, Don
