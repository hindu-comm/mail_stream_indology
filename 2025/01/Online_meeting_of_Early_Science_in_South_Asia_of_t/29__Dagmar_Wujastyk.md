Dear colleagues,

The new season of our CHSTM Science in Early South Asia group is starting
up again. Join us on Monday for The Suśruta Project Group Presentation!
Members of this research group, led by Dominik Wujastyk, will talk about
their research on the oldest known manuscript of The Compendium of Suśruta,
highlighting their most exciting discoveries, and reflecting on their most
important insights on collaborative editing and emerging Digital Humanities
methods. Please join us for what promises to be an enlightening
conversation on *Monday, January 13, 2025, at 10:30 EST.*

To join, you need to sign up to CHSTM and then join the group at
