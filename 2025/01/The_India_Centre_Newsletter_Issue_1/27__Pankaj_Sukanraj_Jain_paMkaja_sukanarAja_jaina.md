Esteemed colleagues,

We are thrilled to launch The India Centre's inaugural newsletter! This
initiative aims to connect you with the diverse world of Indian Knowledge
Systems (IKS). The newsletter reflects our ongoing efforts to study,
preserve, and reinterpret IKS for contemporary relevance. From India’s
culture, heritage, and archaeology to its contributions in science, art,
and architecture, this newsletter will explore the different paradigms of
India’s intellectual traditions. This has been designed and
thoughtfully curated by our team of research associates.

We hope it sparks curiosity and inspires meaningful conversations.

Thanks and regards
