Dear colleagues,

Our annual online lecture series, this year titled “Ascetiscapes: Studies at the Intersection of Asceticism and Space” starts this Thursday, Feb. 27th at 4pm CET. These six lectures invite reflection on the various ways in which ascetic practitioners in South Asia have engaged with abstract, physical, social and embodied space. We will explore different traditions from various disciplinary angles, collectively interrogating the relationship between ascetic paths and the transformation of conceptual and material space. Do join us in our explorations if you can! For more info and registration, have a look on https://www.india.ugent.be/ascetiscapes/<https://www.india.ugent.be/ascetiscapes/?fbclid=IwZXh0bgNhZW0CMTAAAR0U_TW09nu2MAfD6XN6c6bbG755MGe-ABq0fy6wh1z73DHakofpO35OdG4_aem_TdLZQNYZ-FUewKd4s6VcNA>.
