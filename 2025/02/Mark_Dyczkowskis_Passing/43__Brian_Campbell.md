Dear friends and colleagues,

Sharing the news that Dr. Mark Dyczkowski, who needs no introduction,
passed away in Varanasi on Sunday, February 2nd. Last year, he was able to
complete his Tantraloka translation project, a full translation and study
of the Tantraloka with Jayaratha’s commentary, which he published on
Amazon. Mark had three other publications in the works, including a
translation of the Malinivijayottara tantra, all of which will likely be
published in the days to come.
