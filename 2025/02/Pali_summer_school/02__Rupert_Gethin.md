Please see below notice of the Bristol Buddhist Studies Pali summer school:

Pāḷi & Aṭṭhakathā: Advanced Summer School in reading canonical Pali texts with their commentaries.
2–6 June 2025, Department of Religion and Theology, 36 Tyndalls Park Road, Bristol, BS8 1PL, UK

This five-day summer school will be led by:

Rupert Gethin (Professor Emeritus and President of the Pali Text Society): Sutta/Abhidhamma and commentary.

Petra Kieffer-Pülz (Secretary of the Pali Text Society): Vinaya and commentary.

Martin Straube (Senior Research Fellow, Pali Text Society): Vessantara Jātaka.

The school is intended for those with a prior knowledge of Pali or Sanskrit who wish to develop their skills in reading Pali canonical texts and their commentaries.

Daily Timetable:
Morning sessions: 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM (with coffee break)
Afternoon sessions: 2:00 PM – 5:30 PM (with coffee break)
Complimentary lunches and refreshments provided
Course materials included

Note: This is a non-accredited course with a maximum of 20 participants.
