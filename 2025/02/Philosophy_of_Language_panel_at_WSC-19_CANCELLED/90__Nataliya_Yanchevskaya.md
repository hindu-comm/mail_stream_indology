Dear Colleagues,

It gives me no pleasure to inform you that we have decided to cancel the
'Philosophy of Language in Classical India' panel at WSC in Nepal due to
significant communication breakdowns with the Conference Organizing
Committee.

As a panel organizer, I have already notified the conference organizers and
those who sent their abstracts directly to me about the cancellation. We
are posting this announcement publicly, because we don't have any
information about other submissions that were sent to the conference
committee, and we hope that prospective participants will see this message.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. We hope to revive this
panel at the next World Sanskrit Conference or another South Asian studies
conference in the future.

Best wishes,
For the panel organizers,
Nataliya
