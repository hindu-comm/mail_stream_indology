Dear Colleagues,

The Dharma Academy of North America (DĀNAM) invites you to consider
submitting a proposal for the annual meeting to be held concurrently with
the 2025 Annual Meeting of the American Academy of Religion in Boston,
Massachusetts.

Conference Theme

Freedom: Philosophy, Practice, and the Future.  The DĀNAM Steering
Committee invites proposals for panels consisting of a maximum of four
papers. Papers are invited to reflect on philosophy, method, and ontology
in dharma resources that align with the Presidential Theme for the 2025 AAR
Annual Meeting of “Freedom.” The content can be from philosophy, theology,
psychology, anthropology, mythology, and the arts using forms of textual
analysis and the arts, and to engage with the theme of freedom at various
levels of being such as material, mental, bodily, social-political,
cosmological, and spiritual.  We welcome complete panel proposals, as well
as individual paper proposals for peer review.

Deadline: April 10, 2025 at 11:59 PM (pst)
Format: All in person, face to face
Conference will begin on Friday morning before the AAR

Please send your paper or panel proposals and any queries to:

Jonathan Edelmann, Steering Committee Chair,
jbe at ivs.edu

Veena Howard, Steering Committee Vice Chair, vehoward at csufresno.edu

Stephanie Corigliano, Steering Committee
stephanie.corigliano at humboldt.edu

following topics, please contact the organizer(s) listed below and submit
