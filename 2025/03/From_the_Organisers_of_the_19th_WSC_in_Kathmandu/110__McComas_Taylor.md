Dear Participants & Fellow Members,

We are writing to address recent rumours circulating regarding the status of the 19th World Sanskrit Conference (WSC).

We want to unequivocally assure you that the 19th WSC, Nepal is proceeding as scheduled. There is no change to the event, and it will take place as originally planned.

We understand that such rumours can cause concern, and we want to reaffirm our unwavering commitment to hosting a successful and enriching conference. We are diligently working to ensure a seamless experience for all attendees.

We urge you to disregard any misleading information circulating online or elsewhere. For official updates and accurate information, please refer to our official website at https://nepalworldsanskrit.org/

Should you require any further clarification or have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at registration at nepalworldsanskrit.org<mailto:registration at nepalworldsanskrit.org>

Thank you for your continued support and understanding. We look forward to welcoming you to the 19th WSC, Nepal.

Thanks & Regards,

Prof Kashinath Nyaupane
Chair
19th World Sanskrit Conference, Kathmandu, Nepal
