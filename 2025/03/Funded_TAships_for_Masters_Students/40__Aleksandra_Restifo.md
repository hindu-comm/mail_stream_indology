Dear Colleagues,



The Religious Studies Department at Florida International University
(Miami) is offering several funded teaching assistantships for master’s
students, including those in Jain Studies, Hindu Studies, Sephardic and
Mizrachi Studies, and Native American Studies.



TAships cover the full cost of tuition for full-time graduate study at FIU.
They also provide a stipend of $9,000, of which half is given in the fall
and half in the spring semesters. TAships have a limit of two years of
study.



Please follow the link for more details:
https://religion.fiu.edu/academics/funded-teaching-assistantships/index.html



Best wishes,

--

Aleksandra (Sasha) Restifo

Assistant Professor

Bhagwan Mahavir Professorship in Jain Studies

Department of Religious Studies

Florida International University
