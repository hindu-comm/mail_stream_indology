Hi McComas,

Dr. Spiers is certainly correct. These formants in -v-i- are part of a
bigger family of PIE verbal adjectives that are ultimately related to forms
like pak-va and the vas/uS suffix of the perfect active participant. Think
of what the perfect active participle to dī- would be: dīdivaMs-

Best,
Caley
