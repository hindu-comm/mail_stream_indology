Dear Colleagues,

This is a friendly reminder regarding the Postdoctoral Scholar position
within the Research Group "Astral Sciences in Trans-Regional Asia" (ASTRA:
https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/research/departments/max-planck-research-group-astra)
at the Max Planck Institute for the History of Science (MPIWG) in Berlin.

As previously announced, we are seeking a motivated *Postdoctoral Scholar
for a two-year position*, with a particular interest in historians of
astral sciences specializing in South or Central Asia. Researchers working
on other geographical regions are also strongly encouraged to apply.

*Please note that the application deadline is one month away: April 13,
2025, at 23:59 CEST.*

For detailed information and application instructions, please visit:

https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/job-position/postdoctoral-scholar-astra-research-group

We encourage you to share this opportunity with any potentially interested
colleagues.
