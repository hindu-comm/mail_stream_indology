This is from Department of Sanskrit Sahitya, SSUS Kalady.
We are organising an international conference on the thrust area "New horizons of Indological Research" (6th Pandit Subbarama Pattar Endowment) on 19-21 March 2025.
Topics of the conference include:
1.Vedic Literature
2.Epics and Puranas
3.Classical Literature & Contemporary writings
4.Grammar and Linguistics
5.Indian Philosophy
6.Scientific Literature
7.Manuscriptology and Translation studies
8.Dramatic Traditions
9.Aesthetic Studies
10.Cultural and Interdisciplinary studies
