Dear Colleagues,

We are delighted to announce the second edition of the Conversational Tamil
Summer School, which will be held over three weeks from August 7 to 28,
2025 at Bharathiar University in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

This program is a collaborative initiative between the Center for South
Asian and Himalayan Studies (CESAH), Paris, the French Institute of
Pondicherry (IFP), and Bharathiar University.

You can find full details in the attached poster. The summer school offers
two course levels: intermediate and advanced. These courses are especially
tailored for students and researchers in the social sciences, though anyone
with a few months of prior exposure to the Tamil language is welcome to
apply.

The fee for the intensive three-week program is approximately €750. This
includes accommodation and two meals per day (breakfast and lunch). Fee
reductions may be available in certain cases.

The application deadline is in the third week of May.

For any inquiries, feel free to contact us at: *spokentamilschool at gmail.com
<spokentamilschool at gmail.com>*
