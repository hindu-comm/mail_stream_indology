Zitat von Christophe Vielle via INDOLOGY <indology at list.indology.info>:

> Dear Eli,
>
> this happy news does not prevent me to add here a slight correction  
> (if I am myself not wrong): Louis Renou never held a Chair in  
> Indology at the Collège de France. The "Chaire de langue et de  
> littérature sanskrite" was successively held by Antoine-Léonard de  
> Chézy (1815-1832), Eugène Burnouf (1832-1852), Philippe-Édouard  
> Foucaux (1862-1894), Sylvain Lévi (1894-1935), Jules Bloch  
> (1937-1951) and Jean Filliozat (1952-1978) with whom its title  
> became "Chaire de langues et littératures de l'Inde". The original  
> Chair was not really continued after him. A new Chair of "Histoire  
> du bouddhisme" was held by André Bareau (1971-1993), and an other of  
> "Histoire du monde indien" by Gérard Fussman (1984-2011). Something  
> Indological at the Collège de France has been eagerly awaited ever  
> since. This new Chair of "Histoire des systèmes de pensée de l'Inde"  
> is a very welcome new departure.
>
> Bw
> Christophe
