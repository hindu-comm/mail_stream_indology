Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of the LINGUINDIC research team, it is our great pleasure to
invite you to the LINGUINDIC Conference – Modern Linguistics and Ancient
India, to be held in person from 12 to 14 June 2025 at Wolfson College,
University of Oxford.

We have a rich programme covering topics across Indian grammar, modern
linguistics, and their interface*. *Our keynote speakers include Paul
Kiparsky (Stanford), Madhav Deshpande (Michigan), Amba Kulkarni (Hyderabad)
and Malhar Kulkarni (IIT Bombay).

For full session schedules and paper abstracts, please consult the
conference website:
https://www.linguindic.com/conference/. The programme is also attached to
this email.

To secure your place, kindly complete the online registration form by
visiting:
https://forms.gle/rnwY7ZrLSeNQH9ZJ7

Should you have any questions or require further information, please do not
hesitate to contact us at linguindic at ames.ox.ac.uk.

We very much look forward to welcoming you to Oxford.

Yours sincerely,

Dr. Adriana Molina-Muñoz
Research Fellow, Wolfson College
Faculty of Asian and Middle Eastern Studies
University of Oxford
Co-Investigator, LINGUINDIC: New Ideas for Modern Linguistics from Ancient
India
