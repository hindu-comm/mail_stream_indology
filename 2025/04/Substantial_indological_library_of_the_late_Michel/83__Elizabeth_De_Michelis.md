 Dear Colleagues and Friends,
An update on this matter: 

Following a remarkable and well coordinated group effort, the whole library of the late Michel Angot has found an excellent new home (in fact two, some volumes also went to the Collège de France). The majority of the holdings have been carefully and attentively received and catalogued in a newly created university library "Angot section" under the care of Prof. Fernando Wulff at the University of Malaga, Spain, where, I understand, a substantial Indological collection is being established.
Indeed, Prof. Wulff made it known that he would be delighted if similar donations were to be offered to his institution. As offers of this kind have indeed been made on this list in the past, this information may well be of interest to someone. Any communications and enquiries in this sense should be forwarded to Prof. Wullf's attention at the following email address:bblcoord at uma.es .
With all good wishes,
Elizabeth De Michelis
