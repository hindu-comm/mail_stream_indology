There is a 587 page Marathi book: निर्णयसागरची अक्षरसाधना by P.B. Kulkarni
[1967] which gives a detailed historical narrative on the Nirnayasagara
Press.

Another useful Marathi book [342+Photographs] by Bapurao Naik is a general
account of the evolution of Devanagari from Brahmi upto modern Devanagari
printing: देवनागरी मुद्राक्षरलेखनकला, Vol 1, 1982, Mumbai.
