Due to the success of our postdoctoral fellowships forClassical 
Indology, the Board of The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundationhas 
resolved to expand the term of our fellowships from one year (renewable) 
to two years subject to satisfactory performance. The Board hopes that 
this change will give fellows a greater senseof security.

For information on our postdoctoral fellowship program and
