Dear colleagues and friends,


I would like to draw your attention to a new publication edited by myself on Jaina history and art in medieval Karnataka.


Julia A.B. Hegewald (ed.)
Jaina Culture in Medieval Karnataka: Dominance, Dependency and Endurance.
Dependency and Slavery Studies (DSS 18),
De Gruyter, Berlin & Boston, 2025

Besides contributions by myself, it has chapters by Tiziana Lorenzetti, Pius F. Pinto, Sabine Scholz and Julia Lauer.

The book is available on open access and can be downloaded free of charge: https://www.degruyterbrill.com/document/doi/10.1515/9783111695266/html
