Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of Penn’s Department of South Asia Studies, I am pleased to share the following announcement:

The Department of South Asia Studies at the University of Pennsylvania invites applicants for a Postdoctoral Fellowship in Premodern South Asia. The position will run for one year beginning July 1, 2025 (start date is flexible up to Jan. 1, 2026).

Please see the interfolio link for more details and application instructions: https://apply.interfolio.com/167376

Consideration of applications will begin on May 30, 2025 and continue until the position is filled. Inquiries may be addressed to Prof. Daud Ali (daudali at sas.upenn.edu<mailto:daudali at sas.upenn.edu>) or Courtney Brennan (cbrenna at sas.upenn.edu<mailto:cbrenna at sas.upenn.edu>).

We encourage you circulate this announcement to interested colleagues and advanced PhD candidates in your departments.
