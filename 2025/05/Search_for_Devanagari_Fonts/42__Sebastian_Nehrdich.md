Dear list members,

With excuses in case this was discussed not too long ago, but we (= the
Dharmamitra project team under the guidance of Prof. Kurt Keutzer at UC
Berkeley) are currently looking for Devanagari fonts. In case you worked on
this before and have resources to share, we would be very happy to hear
from you!
The background is that in collaboration with Rohan Pandey, the Dharmamitra
project is preparing the training of OCR models for typeset Devanagi
editions this summer, which will be openly released and shared with the
community in case our attempts are successful.
With many thanks,

Sebastian Nehrdich
