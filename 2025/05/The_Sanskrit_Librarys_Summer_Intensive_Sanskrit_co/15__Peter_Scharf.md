Dear Colleagues,

	The Sanskrit Library would like to announce its intensive summer Sanskrit courses.  The series US101S–US201S:

Covers the equivalent of two semesters of introductory Sanskrit in two months 2 June–25 August 2025,
Conducts live meetings as well as providing access to pre-recorded video presentations,
Utilizes the Sanskrit Library's sophisticated but easy-to-use interactive exercise platform that provides just-in-time help and immediate evaluation at every step of each exercise.
Utilizes the professor’s textbook शब्दब्रह्मन्: A linguistic introduction to Sanskrit. By Peter M. Scharf. 2022 <https://www.lulu.com/spotlight/scharf/>,
US101S. Introductory Sanskrit I (Summer intensive) https://sanskritlibrary.org/courses/us101S.html
US102S. Introductory Sanskrit II (Summer intensive) https://sanskritlibrary.org/courses/us102S.html
Please share the news and the link with anyone and everyone you think might be interested.

	Further information about The Sanskrit Library courses is available at the following link: https://sanskritlibrary.org/coursesnow.html
