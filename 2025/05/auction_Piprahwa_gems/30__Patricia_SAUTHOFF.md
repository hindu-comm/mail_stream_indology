Dear all,

I have attached the complete legal notice from the Ministry of Culture, if anyone is interested in it now or in the future. As far as I saw they only posted it on social media, so I figured I'd better grab it while it was easy to find.

Thank you all for the articles you sent when this initially came for auction and for allowing me to "report from the ground" to this email list. It isn't often that I get to dust off my old journalism hat for breaking ancient Buddhist news!

Sadly, despite myself and many others in Hong Kong registering to see the gems one last time today, no one was permitted entry to the exhibition. I am so glad that I got to see them in March. Truly a unique experience.

Best to all,
Patricia
