Hi Greg,

In addition to the classics already mentioned, Anway Mukhopadhyay's "Devī
As Corpse" has some interesting work and interpretations of the Śaktipīṭhas.

For some useful maps of various Śākta pīṭhas, Mark Dyczkowski's chapter
"The Inner Pilgrimage of the Tantras" in his "A Journey in the World of
Tantras" book, has a number of maps outlining sacred sites from the
Kubjikāmatatantra, JRY, Manthānabhairavatantra, etc., plus many charts and
info on their inner equivalence within/on the body.

Best wishes,
Brian Campbell
