Dear colleagues

We are delighted to announce the second edition of the Conversational Tamil
for Social Sciences  - Summer School, which will be held over three weeks
from August 7 to 28, 2025 at Bharathiar University in Coimbatore, Tamil
Nadu.

This program is a collaborative initiative between the Center for South
Asian and Himalayan Studies (CESAH), Paris, the French Institute of
Pondicherry (IFP), and Bharathiar University.

There are several places left so do not hesitate to contact the organizers
here: spokentamilschool at gmail.com
