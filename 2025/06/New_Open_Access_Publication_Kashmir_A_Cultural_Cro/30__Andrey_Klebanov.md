Dear members of the list,

On behalf of Satoshi Ogura, Akane Saito, and Yuko Yokochi, I would like to inform you about the publication of a new collection of scholarly essays titledKashmir: A Cultural Crossroads of the Sanskrit and Persian Cosmopolises, Vol. 2. The volume brings together papers presented at two International Workshops on Pre-Modern Kashmir held at Kyoto University in 2015 and 2018, along with several newly commissioned contributions.

A companion volume, currently in preparation, will be devoted entirely to the work of Prof. Alexis Sanderson.

The present volume is available in Open Access and can be downloaded here:
https://tufs.repo.nii.ac.jp/record/2001253/files/B588_Kashimir.pdf

Attached to this message are the front matter pages of the volume, including the title pages, table of contents, and a short introduction by the editors.

If you wish to order a physical copy of the book, please contact Prof. Satoshi Ogura at:ogura at aa.tufs.ac.jp.

With best regards,
Andrey Klebanov
