Stefan Baums via INDOLOGY <indology at list.indology.info> writes:

> Dear colleagues,
>
> the academy project “Buddhist Manuscripts from Gandhāra”, hosted
> by the Institute for Indology and Tibetology at LMU Munich, is
> inviting applications for a 3-year Ph.D. scholarship (with the
> possibility of extending it for a fourth year). The Gandhāra
> project team would be grateful if you could attract the attention
> of this call to suitable candidates. The Ph.D. student will be
> required to take an active role in the project’s next phase
> (running from 2026 to 2032): during that phase, work will not
> primarily be focused on the edition of Gāndhārī manuscripts, but
> the project team will investigate more broadly the religious and
> literary history of Gandhāran Buddhism. As a result, applicants
> from diverse disciplinary backgrounds and linguistic skills may be
> considered.
>
> The scholarship, set at 75% of an E13 position, is quite generous
> for a doctoral position in the German landscape. The deadline for
> application is 20 June, and the contract will start on 1 October,
> with some flexibility on the latter date.
>
> For further details, please see the attachments or refer to the
> LMU job-portal links, in either English or German.
>
>    English page:
>
>    https://www.b-ite.com/recruitingmanager/job-postings/share/diMGrJQoEGsEAzEsBtWyAKaOk1xgXkzSAUjSh0yKLAhNe2lJq6JK2RUwvWrGBntc
>
>    German page:
>
>    https://www.b-ite.com/recruitingmanager/job-postings/share/Yd72cMAwUQp5PbeRQsg4KUB3qusjZJZjHRWndlqHS0qJwVSq2eyNDFmpgILqvPOY
>
> We would be grateful if you could circulate this call to potential
> applicants, and stand ready to answer further questions.
