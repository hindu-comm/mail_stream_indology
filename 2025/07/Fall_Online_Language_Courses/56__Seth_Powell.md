We are pleased to announce that enrollment is now open at Yogic Studies for our Fall term online Elementary and Intermediate language programs <https://www.yogicstudies.com/language-courses> in Classical Sanskrit, Pali, Classical Tibetan, Hindi, and our newest offering, Greek for Sanskritists. 

The courses run for 12 or 9 weeks, respectively, and are part of a three-term series. Students can enroll for each term individually, or for the entire series. Classes are held live on Zoom twice per week with sessions recorded for later viewing.
