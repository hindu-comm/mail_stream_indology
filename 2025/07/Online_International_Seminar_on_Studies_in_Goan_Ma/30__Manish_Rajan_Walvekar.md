Greetings from the Department of English and Amrita Darshanam, Amrita
Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru!

We are pleased to announce a Two-Day Online International Seminar on
Studies in Goan Manuscripts, jointly organized by the Department of English
and Amrita Darshanam, scheduled for 21st and 22nd August 2025.

We are privileged to have an esteemed panel of invited scholars:

1. Dr. Balaji Shenoy – Archivist (Publication), Directorate of Archives and
Archaeology, Government of Goa
2. Dr. Vidya Kamat – Member Secretary, Centre for Study of Mythology and
Culture, Panaji, Goa
3. Dr. Carlos Fernandes – Former Professor and Head, Department of Library
Science, Goa University
4. Dr. Rohit Phalgaonkar – Assistant Professor, Department of History, Sant
Sohirobanath Ambiye Govt. College of Arts and Commerce, Goa
5. Adv. Uday Bhembre – Independent Scholar and noted lawyer, Goa
6. Ms. Sawani Shetye – Independent Archaeologist, Goa
7. Mr. Payyanur Ramesh Pai – Director, Mahatma Gandhi Museum, Mangalore
8. Dr. Nirmala Kulkarni – Former Research Scientist, Centre of Advanced
Studies in Sanskrit, University of Pune
9. Dr. Sweta Prajapati – Director, Baroda Oriental Research Institute,
Baroda, Gujarat
10. Mr. Kaustubh Somnath Naik – University of Pennsylvania, USA
