As a general note on questions of the type: "Is the digital searchable text
of X's Y available?", Pāṇḍitya is always worth a try, as it can also turn
up lesser-known options when they exist and are registered with SETI. (In
this case, just GRETIL seems to have it finished; this ambitious project at
IIT Bombay <https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/~ocr/> also claims to have
re-digitized it—from what source is not clear—and proofread it, in case
anyone is interested.)
-Tyler
