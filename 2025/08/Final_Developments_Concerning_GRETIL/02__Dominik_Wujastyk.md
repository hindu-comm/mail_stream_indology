I see what Christophe is saying, and he's quite right.  The Textgrid system
is not displaying the "header" information (= etext metadata = TEI header)
in an appropriate manner.  It makes it appear that Gruenendahl personally
typed all the texts in GRETIL.

As to using services like Gitlab and Github, it can be a bit daunting at
first.  The whole Git ecosystem is fascinating and powerful, but not
everybody needs it.  And using it casually if you're not really into it is
indeed daunting.   In the case of Teodore's copy of GRETIL, you can get all
of it by clicking this link:

   -
   https://gitlab.com/claudius-teodorescu/gretil-corpus-data/-/tree/main/contents/text?ref_type=heads

and then pressing the blue [image: image.png] button at the top right of
the screen and choosing your preferred download format (probably Zip).
