If the bird is blue coloured it may be a crow, the emblem of the planet 
Saturn (śāni). In Sri Lanka at each monastery you will find flags with 
this crow and a short prayer for śāni to counteract his influence, which 
is most feared by the people.

May be you search the text for śāni or its Hindi equivalent.
