Dear Colleagues,

The second volume of Alfred Ludwig's English translation (1886–1893) of the
Rigveda, which remained unpublished during the author's lifetime, has just
been released. Comprising Maṇḍalas VI–X, it has been edited by Raik Strunz,
just like the first volume.

You can find more information at:

https://www.harrassowitz-verlag.de/Alfred_Ludwigs_englische_%C3%9Cbersetzung_des_Rigveda_%281886%E2%80%931893%29/titel_8453.ahtml
.


This completes the edition of Ludwig's English translation, the first
volume of which was published in 2019:

https://www.harrassowitz-verlag.de/alfred-ludwigs-englische-uebersetzung-des-rigveda-1886-1893/titel_6441.ahtml
.
