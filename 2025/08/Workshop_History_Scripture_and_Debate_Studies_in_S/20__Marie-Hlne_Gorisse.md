Dear colleagues, dear friends,



I am happy to announce that the workshop *History, Scripture and Debate:
Studies in South Asia in Memory of Paul Dundas* will be held on *12-14
September* 2025 at the *University of Birmingham*, where Paul’s library was
donated.



This workshop will gather scholars, mainly but not necessarily in Jain
Studies, who interacted with Paul in a meaningful way.

All are wellcome!

In case you wish to attend, thanks for sending me (m.gorisse at bham.ac.uk) a
line in advance for (vegetarian and vegan) catering purpose.



Note that on September 15, this event will be followed  by a one-day
symposium on “(Non-)One-sidedness”, organised by Jinesh Sheth (more details
soon).



Looking forwards to seing some of you at this occasion!



With all best regards,



Marie-Hélène
