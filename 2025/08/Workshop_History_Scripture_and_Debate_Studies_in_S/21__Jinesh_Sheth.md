Dear all,

Following the workshop in memory of Paul Dundas, as mentioned earlier, I
would like to announce and invite you to join, in-person or online, for a
symposium on "(Non-)One-sidedness" on Monday, 15th September, at the
University of Birmingham, UK. This symposium aims to explore themes
connected to one-sidedness, *nayas, anekāntavāda*, and related topics.
Kindly find attached herewith the flyer and schedule for the event. All are
welcome!

For those who might be able to join in-person, kindly write to me at
j.sheth at bham.ac.uk. For online participation, kindly register here:
https://bham-ac-uk.zoom.us/meeting/register/MIqwvqteS-6MCFBb_shYoQ.

I look forward to seeing some of you in-person or online!

Thanking you,
Jinesh

Jinesh R. Sheth

Dharmanath Postdoctoral Fellow in Jain Studies
Department of Philosophy
School of Philosophy, Theology and Religion
University of Birmingham
