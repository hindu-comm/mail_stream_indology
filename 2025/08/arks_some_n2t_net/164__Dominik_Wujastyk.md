I routinely use n2t.net to resolve ARK references to items in archive.org
and elsewhere.  But today I'm finding many of these calls don't work, and
instead get sent to ezid.cdlib.org, where they fail.

E.g.,

   - https://n2t.net/ark:/13960/t75t8vp2v

gives an error message.
[image: Screenshot from 2025-08-22 09-46-56.png]

fine.  So, for the above ark, it would be
http://ark.archive.org/ark:/13960/t75t8vp2v.
