Malayalam: Course for Beginners

Winter Term 2025/26
Prof. Dr. Heike Oberlin & Vishnupriyaa P.S. (M.A.)

Place:
Hybrid: either in-class at Tübingen (students of Tübingen University)
or online via Zoom (external guest students).

Time:
20 October 2025 – 5 February 2026
Mondays (10:15 – 11:45 am CET) & Thursdays (10:15 – 11:45 am CET)

Fees:
Free for students of Tübingen University (registration via ALMA).
External guest students have to register centrally and pay a fee of 50€: https://uni-tuebingen.de/en/951.

Contact & Application: indologie at uni-tuebingen.de

The number of participants is limited. Tübingen students are given preferential admission.
All other interested are requested to enclose a short letter of motivation (max. half page).
Application deadline for external participants (indologie at uni-tuebingen.de): 15.9.2025.
