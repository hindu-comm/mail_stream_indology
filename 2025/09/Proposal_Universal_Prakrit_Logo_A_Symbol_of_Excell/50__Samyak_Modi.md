Dear Indologists,

We propose, as passionate students and enthusiasts of Prakrit, a new
identity celebrating Prakrit as a classical indian language and promoting
it academically.


*You can enhance your Prakrit-related endeavours with a wonderful logo that
embodies the richness and depth of Prakrit heritage.*
Adopt it universally:

   - Adorn the covers of Prakrit texts and publications with this emblem
   - Showcase it prominently on banners for Prakrit study programmes and
   events
   - Feature it at conferences, workshops, and gatherings celebrating
   Prakrit
