The India Centre, FLAME University, is delighted to invite you to a webinar
with *Prof. Pritha Chakraborty:*

Drawing from mythology, fiction, and lived narratives, Prof. Chakraborty
will explore *“Tripathagamini Ganga: The Intersectional Flows of the River
in Indian Mythology, Fiction, and Experiential Narratives.”* From the
Puranic goddess to folk traditions of Bengal, from oral narratives of
riverine communities to Amitav Ghosh’s *The Hungry Tide*, the lecture
traces the Ganga’s many currents—religious, ecological, and cultural. This
engaging session invites reflection on human–river entanglements and the
ways Indian traditions shape our understanding of nature, devotion, and
community life.
