Dear colleagues,

Recently I enquired about K. K. Dutta's edition of Rāyamukuṭa's Amarapadacandrikā:

Dutta, Kali Kumar. 1966, 1973, 1978. Padacandrika: A Commentary on the Namalinganusasanam of Amara (Amarakosa). Calcutta Sanskrit College Research Series 48, 86, 126. Calcutta: Sanskrit College.

I find that the edition, to which I now have access thanks to pdfs shared by several colleagues, repeatedly cites a certain Raghunātha. The work in question is identified by Dutta as Raghunātha Cakravartin's Trikāṇḍacintāmaṇi. Dutta mentions an edition by Chandra Mohun Tarkaratna (Calcutta, 1886).

I am unable to find this edition online. Can anyone help?

For the record, NCC does not seem to mention the edition: https://vmlt.in/ncc/search?page=1&q=trikandacintamani.

I don't find the work mentioned in Vogel's Indian Lexicography (1979, 2015).

Thanks.

Best wishes,

Arlo Griffiths
