Dear Colleagues,

The Graduate Program in the Study of Religion is now accepting applications
for its standalone, direct admit MA. Our graduate program has a strong
record of placement, excellent, individual mentoring of students, attention
to professional development, and faculty who are exceptional teachers. As
our graduate program has been intentionally kept small, students have the
opportunity to develop an individualized course of study while also
getting a thorough grounding in the field. Applications are now open.
