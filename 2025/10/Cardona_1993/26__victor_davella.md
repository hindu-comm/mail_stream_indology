Dear all,

May I again, in such quick succession, call upon the generosity of the
members of the list with a follow-up request? I've noted that scholars have
given the unaccented text of the Br̥hadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad according to, or
at least with reference to, two unpublished dissertations (as far as I
know) in which the authors make an attempt at a critical edition with
accentuation: D. Maue 1976 Br̥hadāraṇyak Upaniṣad I: Versuch etc., and C.
A. Pérez-Coffie 1994 Br̥hadāraṇyakopaniṣad II: Critical Edition of the
Second Chapter of the Kāṇva Recension etc. I have noted a review of the
former by M. Witzel, but I believe that was still of the unpublished
dissertation. Thank you!

All the Best,
Victor
