Dear Colleagues,

The Max Planck Institute for the History of Science (MPIWG) in Berlin
invites applications for its fellowship program, offering exceptional
predoctoral and postdoctoral scholars the opportunity to conduct research
in a vibrant and interdisciplinary environment.

We particularly wish to draw your attention to the possibility of joining
the Max Planck Research Group "Astral Sciences in Trans-Regional Asia"
(ASTRA). ASTRA explores the history of astral sciences across premodern
Eurasia, focusing on the transmission, translocation, and transcreation of
astronomical knowledge. We encourage scholars whose research aligns with
our interests (detailed here:
https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/research/departments/max-planck-research-group-astra)
to consider applying.

*For the next round, we are especially looking for scholars working
on South and South-East Asian astral sciences.*

Key Details:

   - *Application Deadline*: January 15, 2026 (for fellowships starting May
   15, 2026, at the earliest)
   - *Fellowship Duration*: ASTRA can host fellows for visits up to twelve
   months.
   - *Eligibility*: Predoctoral (PhD students) and postdoctoral
   (early-career) scholars.
   - *Note*: The MPIWG has two application deadlines per year; the second
   deadline is September 15, 2026. Fellows can begin four months after the
   deadline, at the earliest, meaning the earliest start date for those
   applying by Sept 15th would be January 15, 2027.

*We especially encourage promising PhD students and early-career scholars
to apply.* This fellowship provides an excellent opportunity to gain
valuable experience as a visiting fellow at the MPIWG, fostering
intellectual growth and expanding your research network.

Interested candidates are invited to:

   - Carefully review the fellowship program details and eligibility
   criteria on the MPIWG website:
   https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/news-events/career/fellowship-program
   - Contact Professor Anuj Misra (amisra at mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de) to discuss
   your proposed research project and its alignment with ASTRA's research
   interests.

*Please ensure you have thoroughly read the fellowship guidelines before
contacting Professor Misra.*

We look forward to receiving your applications!

Sincerely,
