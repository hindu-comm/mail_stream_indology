On behalf of Professor Ruzana Vladimirovna Pskhu (RUDN University):

Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to invite scholars to the international conference “Philosophical Sanskrit Texts: Problems of Reading and Interpretation”, to be held 10–20 November 2025 in a hybrid format (on-site and via Zoom). Working languages: English, Russian, Sanskrit.

Programme overview:

12–13 November: Academic sessions — Savitribai Phule Pune University (Pune)

14 November: Excursion — Ajanta Caves

16–17 November: Seminars — Unity Pavilion (Auroville), including “Reading the Yoga Sūtras of Patañjali with Commentary.”

Participation is free of charge. Coffee breaks and meals will be provided for on-site participants. The Organising Committee will offer assistance with logistics, visa support, and accommodation.

Modes of participation:

Participants may attend the entire programme (Pune–Ajanta–Auroville), join one of the on-site segments, or take part fully online as presenters or attendees.

If you wish to apply for participation or submit a paper proposal, please contact the organiser listed below.

On behalf of the Organising Committee,

Ruzana Vladimirovna Pskhu
Head, Russian Group of the Purushottama Centre for the Study of Indian Philosophy and Culture
Peoples’ Friendship University of Russia (RUDN University)
Associate Professor; Doctor of Philosophical Sciences
Department of Oriental and African Studies
