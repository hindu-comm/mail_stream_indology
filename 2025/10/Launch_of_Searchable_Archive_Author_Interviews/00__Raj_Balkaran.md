Dear Colleagues,



I’m delighted to announce the launch of a new, user-friendly, searchable
archive of the scholarship featured on the *Indian Religions* podcast (New
Books Network), now with 400+ episodes:



www.indianreligions.com



*Indian Religions* explores the texts, philosophies, and practices that
shape South Asia. Beyond books, the podcast also covers developments in the
field—ranging from research tools to conferences—and features both
first-time authors and senior scholars across religious studies, history,
philology, anthropology, and philosophy.



The archive is fully searchable by author, title, and topic (with or
without diacritics), and new episodes will continue to be added weekly.



This is the first iteration, so I’d be glad to hear any feedback on
possible improvements. Please also feel free to reach out if the platform
can be of service in publicizing new Indological work.



Warm regards,

Raj
