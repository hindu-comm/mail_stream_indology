BUDDHICA TOLETANA

1st WORKSHOP ON READING BUDDHIST TEXTS
_(Sanskrit, Pali, and Khotanese)_

Organized by the Shan State Buddhist University [1], European Campus

📅 Dates: April 20-25, 2026
📍 Venue: Classrooms of the Castle of San Servando, Toledo, Spain
-------------------------

WORKSHOP DESCRIPTION

The workshop consists of guided reading sessions of Buddhist texts in 
different languages.
It is recommended for intermediate or advanced students, that is, those 
who already have some prior training.

Each day will include three sessions (two in the morning and one in the 
afternoon):

	*

Sanskrit: _The Tigress Jātaka_ in the version of Āryaśūra
	*

Pali: The section on wrong views in the _Brahmajāla Sutta_, with 
Buddhaghosa's commentary
	*

Khotanese: Overview of the grammar and practical work on a classical 
Buddhist text

👉 Students will receive materials for each of these daily sessions, but 
they are free to choose which ones to attend.

Language of instruction: English (with Spanish support).
