Dear Patrick et al, 

On a Mac using the Devanagari QWERTY layout, one trick to get the long vocalic r glyphs is to do the following: 

1. switch to the Devanagari QWERTY keyboard
2. press and HOLD Option+r (which would ordinarily give you the regular short vocalic r
3. a popup menu should appear, with two options, either ऋ or ॄ. 
4. If you then type “1”, you can use the full long vocalic  ṝ, or if you press “2", you will be able to use the vowel marker. 

Hope that helps! I am using macos 11.7.10, so presumably newer versions also have this feature!
