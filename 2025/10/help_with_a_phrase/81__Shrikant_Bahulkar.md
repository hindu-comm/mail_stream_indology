Here is the verse Dominik referred to:

rogas tu doṣa-vaiṣamyaṃ doṣa-sāmyam a-roga-tā |
[nijāgantu-vibhāgena tatra rogā dvi-dhā smṛtāḥ || 20 ||]
Vāgbhata, Aṣṭāṅgahṛdaya, Sūtrasthāna, I.20

Shrikant
