Dear colleagues,

We cordially invite you to participate in an International Conference on
the Works of Svāmi Saccidānandendra Sarasvatī, to be held on May 19-20,
2026 at the Mattoo Center for India Studies, State University of New York,
Stony Brook.

Svāmi Saccidānandendra Sarasvatī (1880–1975) (SSS) was one of modern
Advaita Vedānta's most prolific yet underrecognized scholars. His
extraordinary corpus encompasses over 250 works in Sanskrit, English, and
Kannada, including critically edited translations of Śaṅkarācārya's major
commentaries, original Sanskrit commentaries engaging with post-Śaṅkara
literature, and independent monographs. His works remain largely unknown
and deserve serious scholarly attention.

We welcome abstracts from faculty, graduate students, and independent
scholars on any aspect of SSS's work—from textual analysis and comparative
studies to methodological innovations and broader contextual studies within
the Advaita tradition. Please see the attachment for more details.

For any queries, please write to: manjushree42 at gmail.com

Best regards,

Dr. Manjushree Hegde.
