Dear Colleagues,

The Pali Text Society is pleased to announce its first stock clearance event taking place in December 2025.

To further its aim of promoting the study of Pali texts, a significant number of books  are being made available for free (excluding shipping). To take advantage of this offer, you must be a member of the Pali Text Society.

You can sign up via our website (https://palitextsociety.org <https://palitextsociety.org/>) to benefit from this member-only offer.

The sale will begin on the 1st December and will run for the whole
of the month or whilst stocks last. Further information will be announced shortly.

For those who are interested, please, turn to the administrator of the Pali Text Society, 
Andrew Bishop (info at palitextsociety.org <mailto:info at palitextsociety.org>)

With kind regards,
Petra Kieffer-Pülz
