Dear Andrew,

   Some grammatical works use the sign "ᳲ" for Upadhmānīya and Jihvāmūlīya,
and these are described as ardhavisargasadr̥śau. If I remember correctly,
the Vājasaneyi-Prātiśākhya says that these two do not occur in the
Mādhyandina-Saṃhitā, probably indicating that they do occur in the Kāṇva
Saṃhitā. So it is possible to find them in the manuscripts of the Kāṇva
saṃhitā.

Madhav
