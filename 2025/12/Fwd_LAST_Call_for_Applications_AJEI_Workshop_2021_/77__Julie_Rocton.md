> Dear colleagues,
>
> The *Association Jeunes Études Indiennes (AJEI)*, in collaboration with
> the *Centre for South Asian and Himalayan Studies (CESAH)*, is pleased to
> announce its upcoming workshop in South Asian Studies on the theme: “*States,
> Religion, and Bodies: Current Issues in South Asian Medical Social
> Sciences.*”
>
> The workshop will be held *20–21 January 2026* at Campus Condorcet
> (Paris–Aubervilliers).This event is open to *Master’s and PhD students* from
> all social science disciplines working on South Asia. It aims to provide
> participants with a space for constructive academic exchange in a collegial
> environment, as well as opportunities to interact with senior scholars in
> the field.
>
> *Application Process*
> Applicants are invited to submit a single document including:
> • Institutional affiliation
> • Thesis title and supervisor’s name
> • Title of the proposed paper
> • A 250-word abstract
>
> Applications should be sent to ajei.office at gmail.com by *19 December 2025
> MIDNIGHT CET*
>
> *Please note that participants must be available to attend the workshop in
> person.*
>
> Organizers will notify all applicants of the results by* 24 December 2025*.
> Successful candidates will be expected to become members of the AJEI.
>
> For more details, please look at the PDF enclosed, visit:
> https://ajei.hypotheses.org/
>
> We look forward to receiving your applications and to welcoming you in
> Paris.
>
