Dear Colleagues,

I am writing to provide a brief reminder regarding the upcoming fellowship
application deadline for the *Max Planck Research Group "Astral Sciences in
Trans-Regional Asia" (ASTRA
<https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/research/departments/max-planck-research-group-astra>)*
at the MPIWG in Berlin.

As a reminder, we welcome application from predoctoral and postdoctoral
scholars focusing on *South and South-East Asian astral sciences* for the
current round.

   -

   *Application Deadline:* January 15, 2026
   -

   *Earliest Start Date:* May 15, 2026
   -

   *Duration:* up to 12 months

Interested candidates should review the fellowship program details
<https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/news-events/career/fellowship-program> and
are encouraged to contact me at amisra at mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de to discuss
project alignment before submitting.

We would be grateful if you could share this again with any promising PhD
students or early-career researchers in your networks.

Best regards,

Professor Anuj Misra
Max Planck Research Group "Astral Sciences in Trans-Regional Asia" (ASTRA)
Max Planck Institute for the History of Science (MPIWG) Berlin, Germany
