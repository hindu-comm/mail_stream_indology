Dear colleagues,

This is a reminder of the upcoming deadline for applications for the 
two-year 2026 Rocher Foundation Postdoctoral Fellowship.









The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is pleased to announce the 
competition for its 2026 two-year Postdoctoral Fellowship for Classical 
Indology. The deadline for applications, including two letters of 
support, is January 15, 2026, with the result to be announced on or 
about April 1, 2026. All information about the features and requirements 
of the fellowship and the application form can be found on the 
Foundation's website rocherfoundation.org.

Kindly spread this announcement to potential applicants and to 
institutions of higher learning that might find it of interest.

Rosane Rocher
Chair of the Board
The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation
rocherfoundation.org
