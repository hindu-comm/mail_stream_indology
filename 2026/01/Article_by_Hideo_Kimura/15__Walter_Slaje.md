> This journal publishes primarily Japanese articles from leading scholars
> as well as grad students, and usually includes several articles at the end
> of the volume in English. The reason for the two types of pagination is
> that Japanese books read from right to left, i.e., what in most Western
> languages would be the back of the book is the front, so the numbers start
> at that end. When it gets to the English articles, since they appear in
> left to right format, the pagination is reverse of the Japanese order going
> in the other direction, and the western pagination is given in parentheses.
> This article following the Japanese page order at the bottom of the page is
> on pp. 752-749 (seems backwards), and the English pagination order, in
> parenthesis is (26-29).
