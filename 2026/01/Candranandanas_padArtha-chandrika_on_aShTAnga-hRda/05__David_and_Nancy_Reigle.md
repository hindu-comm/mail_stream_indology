Is it still the case that only the Sūtra-sthāna section of Candranandana's
*Padārtha-candrikā* commentary on the *Aṣṭāṅga-hṛdaya* has so far been
published? The bibliographic data for this edition is given by G. Jan
Meulenbeld in his *History of Indian Medical Literature*, Volume 1B, p.
731, note 126, as first published in 1928, and then reprinted in 1956/57. I
was not able to find a more recent reprint for sale. A scan of what is
apparently the 1928 edition is available at Archive.org, although the
opening pages are missing:



https://archive.org/details/ashtangahridayavagbhattacommentaryofarunadattapadarthachandrikachandranandatikah_167_i/mode/2up



Despite spending hours searching all the various spellings of
*Aṣṭāṅga-hṛdaya* at Archive.org, I was not able to find another copy of
this there. So my questions are:



1. Has any more of Candranandana's *Padārtha-candrikā* commentary been
published?



2. Is there a recent reprint of the 1928 edition of the Sūtra-sthāna with
his commentary that can be purchased?



3. Does anyone have access to the original book who can scan the opening
pages missing in the Archive.org download?



Thank you,



David Reigle

Colorado, U.S.A.
