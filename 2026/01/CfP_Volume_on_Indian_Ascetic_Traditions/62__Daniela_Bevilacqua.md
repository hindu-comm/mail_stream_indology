Dear colleagues,

Greetings!
It is with great pleasure that I share with you the call for papers for a
volume on *Indian Ascetic Traditions,* which I am co-editing with Dr Anjali
Duhan.
The volume, tentatively titled *Indian Ascetic Traditions: Organisations,
Practices, Representations and Social Manifestations, *aims to critically
examine the nature of asceticism in South Asia. We welcome theoretically
informed and critical contributions from a wide range of disciplinary
approaches, time periods, and areas (from religious studies, textual
analysis, ethnography, visual studies, and the broader Indic studies).
We invite the submission of *abstracts of 250–300 words* along with a *short
biography (100 words)* including affiliation, research interests, and two
to three key publications.
Submissions should be sent to *indianasceticvolume at gmail.com
<indianasceticvolume at gmail.com>* by *31 March 2026.   *
Please refer to the attached pdf for further information.

With best wishes,
