>> In 1998, I published *The Roots of Ayurveda* with Penguin India.  They

>> In protest at Bertelsmann's and Penguin India's craven submission to a
>> right-wing challenge to my friend Wendy Doniger's book *The Hindus: An
>> Alternative History* and their subsequent withdrawal of her book from

>> market.
