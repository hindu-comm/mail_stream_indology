Dear Colleagues,

The Department of Indian Studies<https://ind.elte.hu/> of Eötvös Loránd University (Budapest, Hungary) is holding an online (as well as in-person) info-session on 28th January 2026 at 13:00 CET about its on-campus English-language Indology MA program. Those who are interested are kindly requested to send an email to Máté Ittzés (ittzes.mate at btk.elte.hu) in order to receive the Teams-link to the online meeting.

Further information about the program can be obtained here<https://www.elte.hu/en/indology-ma> and here<https://www.btk.elte.hu/en/master-programmes-1>. Details of the application process for the academic year 2026/2027 are described here<https://apply.elte.hu/courses/course/321-ma-indology?search=718865>.

I also kindly ask you to draw the attention of your students to this possibility.

Thank you so much.
Best regards,
Máté Ittzés
