Dear Richard and others,

Archiv Orientální, published since 1929 by the Oriental Institute in 
Prague, is available in subscription databases (ProQuest, EBSCO), but 
all back issues (1929-2010) are freely available in the digital library 
of the Czech Academy of Sciences here:

https://kramerius.lib.cas.cz/periodical/uuid:c5ff669c-3e3e-11e1-8f8f-005056a60003

Unfortunately, the interface is somewhat unfriendly. It is not possible 
to download individual articles, but you need to select the range of 
pages in the relevant issue and print them as a PDF. However, the limit 
for selection is twenty pages, so if the article is longer, this means 
generating two PDFs and then merging them. It is therefore impractical 
and time-consuming, but you can get the desired article.

Please note that due to this arrangement, neither Google nor any other 
search engine can find the article you are looking for here.

Best,

Lubomir
