Dear members of the list,

It is my pleasure to announce the publication of the Felicitation volume for S. L. P. Anjaneya Sarma, which has appeared as volume 166 in Pondicherry’sCollection Indologie:

David, Hugo, Andrey Klebanov, and Victor D’Avella, eds. 2026.Vidvanmānasaprasādinī. Essays in Honor of S.L.P. Anjaneya Sarma. Institut Français de Pondichéry, École française d’Extrême-Orient.

In principle, it should be possible to order the volume via the EFEO publication website (https://publications.efeo.fr/fr/collections/collection-indologie), although it does not yet seem to have been added to the catalogue; it may therefore take a few days before orders can be placed.

Attached to this message you will find the initial pages of the book, including the bibliographical information and the table of contents.

best,
Andrey
