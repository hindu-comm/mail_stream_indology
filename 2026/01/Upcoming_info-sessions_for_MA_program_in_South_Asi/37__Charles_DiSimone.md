Dear friends,

I would like to draw your attention to our new MA program in South Asian
Languages and Cultures Our two year program offers specializations in
Buddhist Studies, Jaina Studies, and Material Culture in South Asia. This
is a hybrid program that can be taken in-person here in Ghent or online.
The program is taught in English and is designed to be friendly and
accommodating toward international students. Info about the program may be
found here
<https://www.india.ugent.be/english/program/masters-degree-in-south-asian-languages-and-cultures-hybrid/#anchor6>
.

We are holding two online info-sessions next week:

Session 1: January 15th, 2026 at 18:00 CET, 22:30 IST, 12 noon EST, 9:00 PST
Session 2: January 16th, 2026 at 10:00 CET, 14:30 IST, 4:00 EST, 1:00 PST

Registration for these info-sessions may be done here
<https://www.india.ugent.be/infosessions/>.

Please do share this with any students you might be interested in pursuing
an MA in these topics.

Friendly greetings,
Charles

Prof. Dr. Charles DiSimone
