> I checked Slaje's book on the śāradā script but didn't see anything (of

You will find the numeral "2", which is the sign you are looking for, on p.
46 (sub. "1.8. Zahlzeichen"). It is not a special character, but simply
means 'two times' or 'repetition' (of the preceding akṣaras).

Best, WS
