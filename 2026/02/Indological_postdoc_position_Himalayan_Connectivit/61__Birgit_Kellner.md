(apologies for cross-posting)

the Institute for the Cultural and Intellectual History of Asia (IKGA) 
of the Austrian Academy of Sciences offers a full-time postdoc position 
on "Himalayan Connectivities" with an Indological background. The 
position is to be filled as soon as possible, with the contract 
extending up until 31 March 2031. The application deadline is 31 March 
2026.

Historically, the Himalayan region has been a contact zone for cultural 
interaction between the Indian subcontinent, the Tibetan plateau, and 
beyond. This research focus addresses processes of circulation, 
mediation, and transformation of knowledge and practices in the domains 
of philosophy and religion, as well as in other knowledge fields; 
technologies in particular knowledge fields are also a possible topic of 
investigation. The position is based at the IKGA and is affiliated with 
the Cluster of Excellence “EurAsian Transformations.”

Further details about the position and the application process can be 
found on the Academy's application platform: 
https://oeawnr.onlyfy.jobs/job/cym9oc9b

Please disseminate this announcement to suitable candiates,

with best regards,

Birgit Kellner
