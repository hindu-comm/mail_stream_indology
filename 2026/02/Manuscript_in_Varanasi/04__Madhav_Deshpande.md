I had a similar frustrating experience with VSBL many years ago while
trying to get copies of the Śaunakīya-Caturādhyāyikā, but by great luck,
Professor Cardona was in Varanasi, and he was able to get those copies for
me.

> I am not surprised to know the experience with VSBL. About twenty years

> collection of the said MSS has ever been touched by any scholar to date.
