Dear All:

Congratulations to Manasicha Akepiyapornchai on the publication of her book: Surrender to God Across Languages: Multilingual Intellectual History of Premodern India. The book is a comprehensive study of a key multilingual moment in the intellectual history of premodern India. It offers a systematic account of the interplay of linguistic and intellectual history within the Śrīvaiṣṇava community. It is part of the Rocher Foundation book series with Oxford University Press. All books in this series are open access.
