Dear Colleagues,

The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation is pleased to announce that *Dr. 
Radha Blinderman*is the 2026 Rocher Foundation Postdoctoral Fellow.
Dr. Blinderman will produce a critical edition, translation, and 
contextual study of a section of the 18th century Sanskrit grammar of 
Bālarāma, the/Prabodhaprakāśa/. Her work will contribute not only to 
grammatical studies, but also to religious studies and the history of 
Bengal. She will be in residence at Brown University for the duration of 
her Fellowship.

For detailed information and the application form for our two-year 
Fellowship, next due by January 15, 2027, and for news and information 
on our other, publishing, activities, please see our website 
rocherfoundation.org.

With warm good wishes,

Rosane Rocher,
Professor Emerita of South Asia Studies
University of Pennsylvania
Chair of the Board
The Ludo and Rosane Rocher Foundation
