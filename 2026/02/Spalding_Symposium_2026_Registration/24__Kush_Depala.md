Dear Colleagues,

I am pleased to announce that ticket sales are now open on Eventbrite for
the Spalding Symposium 2026, titled “Questioning Boundaries.”

The symposium will take place on 27–28 March 2026 at the Faculty of
Divinity, University of Cambridge.

This year’s theme, Questioning Boundaries, invites reflection on
intellectual, religious, artistic, geographic, and methodological
boundaries within and across South Asian traditions. We look forward to two
days of stimulating discussions and interdisciplinary exchange.

Registration is now available via Eventbrite at the following link:
https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/51st-annual-spalding-symposium-on-indian-religions-tickets-1982632212318
.

We warmly encourage colleagues and students who may be interested to
register early.
Best wishes,

and
