Dear list members,

Twenty six etexts from the SARIT collection
https://github.com/sarit/SARIT-corpus were converted from devanagari to
IAST transliteration and added to  SEARCHABLE AGGREGATE LIBRARY of SANSKRIT
ETEXTS <https://searchable-sanskrit-library.org/>

Those etexts are:
kalyāṇakāraka by ugrāditya
kāvyādarśa by daṇḍin with commentary ratnaśrī by ratnaśrījñāna
cakrapāṇidatta by cakradatta
jñānaśrīmitranibandhāvali
tattvasaṅgraha by śāntarakṣita with commentary by kamalaśīla
tantravārttika by kumārila bhaṭṭa
tarkabhāṣa by mokṣākaragupta and jitāripāda by vādasthāna part 1
tarkabhāṣa by mokṣākaragupta and jitāripāda by vādasthāna part 2
daśarūpaka by dhanaṃjaya with commentaries
dvādaśāraṃ nayacakraṃ with commentary nyāyāgamānusāriṇī
dharmottarapradīpa
nyāya - six buddhist tracts in sanskrit
nyāyakumudacandra by prabhācandrācārya
nyāyamañjarī with ṭippaṇī both by jayantabhaṭṭa
nyāyavārttikatātparyaṭīkā by vācaspatimiśra
pramāṇamīmaṁsa with bhāṣā tippaṇa
pramāṇavārttika pariśiṣṭa 1
pramāṇavārttikālaṅkāra
pramāṇavārttikasvavṛttiṭīkā
pramāṇāntarbhāva
vādanyāya by dharmakīrti with commentary by śāntarakṣita
vidhiviveka by maṇḍanamiśra with nyāyakaṇikā by vācaspatimiśra
sarasvatīkaṇṭhābharaṇa
siddhayoga AKA vṛndamādhavā
siddhiviniścaya with vṛtti and ṭīkā
skanda-purāṇa  first 69 adhyāyas

Harry Spier
