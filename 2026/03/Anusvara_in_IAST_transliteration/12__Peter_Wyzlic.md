Dear all,

it may not be of interest internationally, but the same has been done in 
the German DIN 33904 for the transliteration of Devanagari (published in 
2018):

अं = aṃ

*माँसा = *māṁsā

Source: DIN 33904: Information und Dokumentation - Transliteration 
Devanagari = Information and documentation - romanization Devanagari 
(Berlin, 2018), p. 11

All the best

Peter Wyzlic
