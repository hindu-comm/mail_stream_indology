Dear Colleagues,

I am writing to share an opportunity for an upcoming hands-on
workshop, *"Premodern
Knowledge Systems from Primary Sources,"* taking place in September 2026 at
the *Department of Cross-Cultural and Regional Studies, University of
Copenhagen*.

This training week offers a rare opportunity for researchers to work
directly with the *Kenneth G. Zysk Indological Manuscript Collection*.
Given the technical and intensive nature of the archival work,
participation is strictly limited to *six (6) participants*.

We would be very grateful if you could circulate this call among your
networks. We kindly ask that interested candidates *read the requirements
on the event page carefully* before applying; proficiency in reading
primary sources is essential for participation.

*Full details and programme:*
https://www.mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de/event/premodern-knowledge-systems-primary-sources
Key Details at a Glance:

   -

   *Application Deadline:* April 30, 2026
   -

   *Target Audience:* Advanced students of Sanskrit (Doctoral students and
   ECRs).
   -

   *Technical Requirement:* Advanced Sanskrit reading proficiency.
   -

   *Format:* Letter of Motivation and CV combined into a *single PDF*.
   -

   *Grants:* Limited travel and accommodation support available for
   eligible candidates.
   -

   *How to Apply:* Send applications directly to *amisra at mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de
   <amisra at mpiwg-berlin.mpg.de>*.

Thank you for your assistance in bringing this to the attention of
qualified candidates.

Best regards,
Anuj Misra
