Hare Krishna Namaste
As part of our efforts to review different indological aspects Poornaprajna
Samshodhana Mandiram, Bengaluru welcomes you to this very important seminar
on the "Review of contemporary Indology texts translations and
Interpretations" being organised by Poornaprajna Samshodhana Mandiram,
Bengaluru with the financial support of Central Sanskrit University New
Delhi on 24.3.2026 at Poornaprajna Samshodhana Mandiram.

First session will be chaired by well-known scholar Prof. Nagaraj Paturi
and second session will be chaired by Prof. Madhusudan Penna, Director of
Sanskrit Academy, Hyderabad.

Young scholars will take on Indology head-on on different aspects indian
philosophies. Invitation letter is attached herewith.

I request you to attend this seminar.


Acharya Veeranarayana Pandurangi,
 Director Poornaprajna Samshodhana Mandiram Bengaluru.
