Dear Colleagues,

	The Sanskrit Library would like to announce its spring Sanskrit courses.

US101S. Introductory Sanskrit I will begin Monday, 1 June.
US120. Intermediate Sanskrit prose reading continues and can be joined by intermediate students.
US201. Selected readings in the Siddhāntakaumudī continues and can be joined by advanced students.
UT102. Character and higher-level encoding is begins 21 March to train Sanskrit students and advanced scholars in preparing navigable searchable digital editions of printed Sanskrit texts.
UT110. TEI-conformant XML markup workshop guides those who have completed UT102 or otherwise have basic competency in TEI in preparing a TEI edition of a Sanskrit text.

	For information about the these and other planned courses follow the link: https://sanskritlibrary.org/coursesnow.html
	To inquire about consulting on individual research projects, send an email to 
info at sanskritlibrary dot org <mailto:info at sanskritlibrary.org>
	For information about our publications, please visit https://sanskritlibrary.org/publications.html.
	Submit an article for publication in our journal विमर्शिनी here: https://sites.google.com/sanskritlibrary.org/courses/research-journal
	Please donate to The Sanskrit Library here: https://sanskritlibrary.org/donate.html
