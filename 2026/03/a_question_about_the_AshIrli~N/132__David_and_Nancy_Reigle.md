Referring to the question raised by Harry Spier a few days ago: I am now
back at my books, and can confirm that *vīkṣiṣiraṃs* is a typographical
error in the digital input of the 1977 book referred to, as Matthew
Kapstein assumed. It is correctly *vīkṣiṣīraṃs* in this book. Digital
editions are very helpful, but it is still necessary to have the books or
image scans of the books to check possible typos such as this one.
