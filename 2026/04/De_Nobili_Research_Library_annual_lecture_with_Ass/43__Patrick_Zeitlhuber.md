Dear list members,

the De Nobili Research Library (“Sammlung De Nobili”) cordially invites 
you to its 17th annual lecture on Friday, 8 May 2026, 3:00–5:00 pm 
(UTC+2), which will take place in-person and on Zoom. Associate Prof. 
Pär Eliasson (University of Stavanger) will speak about “Early Jesuit 
Use of Hindu Scripture: The Case of Marathi.” Please find further 
details including the Zoom link and abstract in the attached document.

At this occasion, we would also like to draw your attention to our 
series of quite affordable books and occasional papers: 
https://sdn-istb.univie.ac.at/order-form/

On behalf of the De Nobili Research Library,
Patrick Zeitlhuber
(Secretary of the De Nobili Research Library)
