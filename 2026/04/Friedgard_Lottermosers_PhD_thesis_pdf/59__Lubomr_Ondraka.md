Dear Aleix,

it is great that you have made the file available on archive.org. Thank 
you so much for that.

However, it would be very helpful if you could add metadata. As we all 
know, searching on archive.org is very difficult, and it is often nearly 
impossible to find a file that is actually there because it lacks 
quality metadata.

So I strongly urge everyone who invests time and energy in making 
academic works available to other colleagues on archive.org to always 
provide the most complete metadata possible. That is the only way your 
files will be easy to find.

You can find inspiration, for example, in the files uploaded by Dominik 
Wujastyk, whose metadata is usually exemplary.
https://archive.org/details/kanjilal-1978

Or take a look at the file I recently uploaded:
https://archive.org/details/meghadutam-jagaddhariya-rasadipini

Best,

Lubomir
