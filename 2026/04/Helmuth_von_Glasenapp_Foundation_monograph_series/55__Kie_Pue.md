Dear Colleagues,


The Helmuth von Glasenapp-Stiftung (Helmuth von Glasenapp Foundation) would like to draw your attention to its  monograph series.

This series publishes monographic studies on the history of Indian thought, culture, literature, and religion, without strict limitations on subject matter or length. The series aims to highlight the breadth and diversity of Indological research and to promote its various perspectives.

Manuscripts submitted for publication ought to be written in one of the standard academic languages of Indological research: German, English, or French.

Submissions should be made informally via email by mid-February of each fiscal year, including the manuscript in a modern format (.docx, .rtf, .odt, .tex) and simultaneously as a PDF.
