Dear All,

The last time I wrote in to the list about my projects, on Dec. 19th, 2025,
it was to announce HANSEL <https://hansel-library.info> (blog post
<https://tylerneill.info/blog/hansel> December 19, 2025). Before that, two
other project launch announcements occurred during the same year: Pāṇḍitya
<https://panditya.info/>, blog post
<https://tylerneill.info/blog/panditya> February
7, 2025; SETI <https://panditya.info/seti>, blog post
<https://tylerneill.info/blog/seti> April 23, 2025.

This year, I launched another small website to concisely articulate the
vision of my interconnected Sanskrit projects under a new brand,
called Kalpataru
Grove <https://kalpataru-grove.info>.
Blog post: *Kalpataru Grove* <https://tylerneill.info/blog/kalpataru-grove>,*
February 6, 2026.*

And just the other day, I wrote a quarterly update covering all my
Kalpataru Grove activities from January to March of this year. It is
essentially the story of how I used Claude Code to revamp existing
projects, which I think may be of special interest to some of you.
Blog post: *Quarterly Update: Jan–Mar 2026*
<https://tylerneill.info/blog/2026-q1-quarterly>, *April 5, 2026*

Moving forward, I plan to publish these quarterly digests at the conclusion
of each quarter, and I'll also do occasional one-off pieces when
appropriate. My updates to this list should also happen on the same
quarterly cadence.

As always, I welcome any comments or questions!

With warm spring regards,
Tyler
