Dear Ron,

This is a local publication from Northern Orissa, only distributed by the author himself. I deposited a copy in Leiden University library when it came into mu hands, and still happen to have a few others copies on my bookshelf here in Jakarta.

I can try to get the book scanned next week. I also have an e-text that I seems for some reason never to have been deposited on GRETIL, and I am looking into a way to make it public. I also have digital photos of the ms. from Asimila village on which the book is essentially based. I attach one of them here, and would be happy to share all should anyone be interested in working on this material. (I would welcome practical suggestions from the list on how to make all my old photos of Orissa mss. public.)

Best wishes,

Arlo
