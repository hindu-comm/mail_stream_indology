Dear list members,
20 dictionaries and reference works from the Cologne Digital Sanskrit
Lexicon have been added to the Searchable Aggregate Library of Sanskrit
https://searchable-sanskrit-library.org

These were mostly downloaded from the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon
(CDSL). They are all copyrighted by The Sanskrit Library and Thomas Malten
and licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial Share
Alike license.

In the Searchable Aggregate Library of Sanskrit these dictionaries and
reference works appear first in the index before the other texts.  The
search results from these various dictionaries/reference works display
first and afterward the results for the sanskrit literature in the library.
The effect of this is to make the searchable library dual functioned, as a
multi-dictionary/reference work lookup (whose results appear first) and a
sanskrit word/phrase locator (whose results in the literature appear after
the dictionary/reference work references).

The dictionaries/reference works added are:

 -Bohtlingks Roth Grosses Petersburger Worterbuch

-Bohtlingks Sanskrit-Worterbuch in Kurzerer Fassung

-Dictionnaire Sanskrit-Français by Stchoupak Nitti and Renou

-Grassmanns Worterbuch zum Rig-Veda

-Index to the Names in the Mahabhrata by Sorensen

-Indian Epigraphic Glossary by Sircar

-Mahabharata Cultural Index by Mehendale

-Monier-Williams English Sanskrit Dictionary

-Personal and Geographical Names in the Gupta Inscriptions by Sharma

-Practical Sanskrit Dictionary 1890 by Apte

-Purana Index by Dishitar

-Puranic Encyclopaedia by Mani

-Sabdakalpadruma by Rādhākāntadeva

-Sanskrit English Dictionary by Cappeller

-Sanskrit Names of Plants by Meulenbeld

-Schmidts Nachtrage zum Sanskrit-Worterbuch

-Sircars Indian Epigraphical Glossary

-The Students English-Sanskrit Dictionary by Apte

-Vacaspatyam by Bhattacarya

-Vedic Index of Names and Subjects by Macdonell and Keith

These join the two dictionaries/reference works already in the searchable
library.

-Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit Dictionary by Edgerton

-Sanskrit-English Dictionary by Monier-Williams 1899
