Join Dr. Jonathan Edelmann, Fellow of the International Society for Science
and Religion (ISSR) and the Owner and Manager of Scholar Path Consulting,
LLC, for an engaging session.

The session “Hindu Theology and Biology: The Bhāgavata Purāṇa and
Contemporary Theory,” organized by FLAME University’s India Centre, will
explore the relationship between Hindu thought and modern scientific
inquiry through the lenses of metaphysics, epistemology, and philosophical
anthropology. Drawing on the Bhāgavata Purāṇa and its interpretive
traditions, the talk will examine how theological knowledge can engage
constructively with contemporary scientific discourse while preserving its
intellectual integrity.
