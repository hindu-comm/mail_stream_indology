Dear all,

We are pleased to announce that the full program for the *4th International
Conference Yoga Darśana – Yoga Sādhana, *to be held at the Campus
Condorcet, Paris from 27–29 May 2026, is now available here
<https://ydys2026.sciencesconf.org/data/pages/YDYS_Full_Program.pdf>.

The event brings together over 120 international scholars working on topics
ranging from ancient to contemporary perspectives across the social
sciences and humanities. Attendance is *free of charge* for all members of
the academic community.

Those wishing to attend in person are invited to complete the non-speaker
registration form
<https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfVDE_FCsV8gaZ30gdgvpMKWQ_i9sCYNWKqYk829C0t-8VeHA/viewform>.
 All other details, including practical information and directions to the
venue (Campus Condorcet – Centre des colloques) can be found at the conference
website <https://ydys2026.sciencesconf.org>.

For those unable to join us in Paris, *two keynote lectures and a book
launch will be live-streamed* in partnership with SOAS, see the program
<https://ydys2026.sciencesconf.org/data/pages/YDYS_Full_Program.pdf> for
links.

For any questions, please write to us at: ydys2026 at sciencesconf.org

We look forward to welcoming you to Paris.
Raphaël Voix, with Suzanne Newcombe, Amandine Wattelier-Bricout, Pamela
Warner, Ruth Westoby and Theo Wildcroft.
