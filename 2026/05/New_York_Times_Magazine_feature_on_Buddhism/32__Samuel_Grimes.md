The editors at the New York Times should perhaps do even minor fact
checking before publishing so much misinformation on Newar Buddhism.
Acknowledging its existence is perhaps a step in the right direction, even
while saying things like it is “inextricably linked with Hinduism” and a
“prison of secrecy.” All while mentioning people like Birat Raj Bajracharya
and Swostu Kayastha who would be appalled having their names appear beside
such nonsense.
