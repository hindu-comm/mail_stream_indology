Dear list members,

1. A searchable plain text version of Monier-Williams 1899 Sanskrit-English
dictionary has been added to the Searchable Aggregate Library of Sanskrit
Etexts
https://searchable-sanskrit-library.org/ .
It was created from the XML tagged version copyrighted to "The Sanskrit
Library and Thomas Malden (but CC licensed).
https://sanskritlibrary.org/index.html .
The xml version was downloaded from the Cologne Digital Sanskrit
Dictionaries page https://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/ .
Like the other reference material in the library (Bloomfield's Vedic
Concordance), it appears at the beginning of the etext list, so that in
searchs of the library, results from the dictionary will appear first.

2. caturvargacintāmaṇi pariśeṣakhaṇḍa kālanirṇaya by hemādri from the
University of Texas at Austin's
Resource Library for Dharmaśāstra Studies
https://sites.utexas.edu/sanskrit/resources/dharmasastra/ has been added to
the library and
caturvargacintāmaṇi vrata-khaṇḍa by hemādri part 2   has been updated with
revisions made in April 2026.

Thank you,

Harry Spier
