Dear member,
We are pleased to share an exciting opportunity for students interested in
learning Hindi and Urdu.


[image: 7d8e269e-5682-4743-9bee-f09861eecf1b.JPG]
AJEI is pleased to support the *Hindi–Urdu Intensive Summer School
organised by Ca’ Foscari University of Venice (ITALY) by offering one
scholarship covering the full course fee (€400) for the upcoming programme
(31 August – 18 September 2026).*

This three-week programme is open to both beginners and advanced learners
and provides immersive training in spoken and written Hindi and Urdu, along
with a rich cultural programme.

*Scholarship details:*
*.* One €400 grant (full tuition coverage)
. Open to students applying to the summer school

*How to apply:*
1. Apply to the summer school programme > submitted online
<https://forms.gle/baJDpZRn4u8hMkeo8>. (*https://www.unive.it/pag/50411/
<https://www.unive.it/pag/50411/> *)
2. Include a short statement outlining your motivation and purpose
3. Download the application and submit it to: ajei.office at gmail.com

*Deadline: 30 June 2026*

*Important notes:*
*1. AJEI will select one eligible student for this scholarship together
with the board of Ca Foscari professors.*
*2. AJEI membership is mandatory*
*(https://www.helloasso.com/associations/association-jeunes-etudes-indiennes/adhesions/ajei-365-days-membership
<https://www.helloasso.com/associations/association-jeunes-etudes-indiennes/adhesions/ajei-365-days-membership>)
*
[image: qr.png]
3. The selected candidate must attend the programme *in person*
4. Accommodation* is not covered and must be arranged independently* by the
student (*Affordable accommodation is available for students (in twin
rooms) during the Summer School period. We invite participants to visit the
following link for the San Francesco Student Residence for further
information and bookings:*
*https://www.fondazionecarpinetum.org/san-francesco-student-residence/*
<https://www.fondazionecarpinetum.org/san-francesco-student-residence/>)

We strongly encourage interested students *to apply and take advantage of
this unique opportunity to study in Venice  (ITALY) while deepening their
engagement with South Asian languages and cultures.*

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at
ajei.office at gmail.com.
