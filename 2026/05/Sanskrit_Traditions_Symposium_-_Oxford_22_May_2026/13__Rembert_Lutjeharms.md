Dear colleagues,

*The 42nd Annual Sanskrit Traditions Symposium*
*Friday 22 May 2026*

The 41st annual The Sanskrit Traditions Symposium will take place in
Oxford, at Trinity College, on Friday 22 May 2026 (11am - 5pm), hosted by
the Oxford Centre for Hindu Studies.

This year we have the following papers:

   -

   *Simon Brodbeck *(Cardiff), *The plan to capture Yudhiṣṭhira in the
   Droṇaparvan*
   -

   *Ruth Westoby *(OCHS)*, **An argument from silence: female practitioners
   in premodern haṭha yoga*
   -

   *Poojan Patel* (Cambridge), *Sri Aurobindo’s Psychological-Spiritual
   Hermeneutic: A Systematic Response to the Problem of Ṛgvedic
   Intelligibility*
   -

   *Maximillian Hoth *(Hamburg), *The ‘gajavṛṣabhanyāya’ – Śivānanda’s
   Interpretation of a Visual Analogy from South: Indian Temple Architecture
   in his commentary on the Mātṛkācakraviveka*

To register, please visit: https://sts-ochs-26.netlify.app/

Best wishes,
