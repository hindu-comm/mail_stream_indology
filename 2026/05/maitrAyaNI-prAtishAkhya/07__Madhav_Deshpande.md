Evidently a manuscript of the Maitrāyaṇī-Prātiśākhya was in the private
possession of Shridhar Shastri Ware of Nasik, but after he passed away, the
whereabouts of the manuscripts in his possession is not clear. I think
Yudhishthir Mimamsak tried to find it but was not able to find it. I was
told that some of those manuscripts were given to the Nasik Nagar
Vachanalaya. Several years ago, I visited this place. I looked through the
rough list of their manuscript holdings, but did not find this particular
text. Vidhata Mishra probably had access to a different source.
