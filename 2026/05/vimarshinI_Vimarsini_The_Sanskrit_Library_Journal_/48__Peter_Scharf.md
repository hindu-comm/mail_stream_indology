 विमर्शिनी Vimarśinī: The Sanskrit Library Journal
In honor and celebration of Professor George Cardona’s 90th birthday, we are pleased to announce the publication of the first volume of विमर्शिनी Vimarśinī: The Sanskrit Library Journal.  This volume includes five in-depth research articles devoted to Sanskrit vyākaraṇa by Dr. Tanuja Ajotikar, Dr. Sharon Ben-Dor and myself. The journal is available in a hard-cover printed volume for a modest cost, and by on-line access free of cost. For details about ordering, access, and submission guidelines, please see the Journal’s webpage at:

https://sanskritlibrary.org/vimarsini.html


On-line intensive summer Sanskrit courses
Last call for The Sanskrit Library's on-line intensive summer Sanskrit courses. The series US101S–US201S:
Covers the equivalent of two semesters of introductory Sanskrit in two months 1 June–24 July 2026,
Conducts live meetings as well as providing access to pre-recorded video presentations,
Utilizes the Sanskrit Library's sophisticated but easy-to-use interactive exercise platform that provides just-in-time help and immediate evaluation at every step of each exercise.
Taught by Professor Peter Scharf who taught Sanskrit in the Classics Department at Brown University for 19 years.
Utilizes the professor’s textbook शब्दब्रह्मन्: A linguistic introduction to Sanskrit. 2022.
