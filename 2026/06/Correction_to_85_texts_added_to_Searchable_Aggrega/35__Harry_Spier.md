Dear list members,

Thanks to the keen eyes of Viliam Gostishchev, the following errors have
now been corrected to the latest update to the Searchable Aggregate Library
of Sanskrit.

1) Each of the 12 texts added from the Digital Corpus of Sanskrit were
inadvertantly added twice. That has now been corrected.

2) sakalāgamasārasaṅgraha from IFP manuscript bundle RE22716 was
inadvertantly not added because sakalāgamasārasaṅgraha from IFP transcript
T0351 was already in the library.  That has now been corrected, both are
now in the library.

3) The announcement said a text called āgamagrantha had been added. That
was a mistake. No āgamagrantha has been added to the library.

Thanks,
Harry Spier

https://searchable-sanskrit-library.org/

60 śaiva-siddhānta texts from the joint project between the Institut
Francais de Pondichery and The Muktabodha Indological Research Institute,
India. This was made possible by their generously adding Creative Commons
licensing to this collection.

the online displays of these texts.

11 texts from the Muktabodha Indological Research Institute. These are the
latest texts added recently.
