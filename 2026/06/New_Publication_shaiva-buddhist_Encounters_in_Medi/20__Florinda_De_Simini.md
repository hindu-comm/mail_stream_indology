Dear Colleagues
I am delighted to announce that a new publication in the peer-reviewed, open-access series Studies on the History of Śaivism is finally available online:
Den Boer, Lucas, and Florinda De Simini (eds). 2025. Śaiva-Buddhist Encounters in Medieval India. Studies on the History of Śaivism 5. Napoli: Unior Press.
A PDF of the book can be downloaded from the following link:
www.fedoabooks.unina.it/index.php/fedoapress/catalog/book/800<http://www.fedoabooks.unina.it/index.php/fedoapress/catalog/book/800>
(try to paste the link in a browser if not immediately re-directed)
Hardbound copies are currently being printed. While a proper distribution system is not yet in place (though I am working on it and expect it to be operational by the time the next volume appears), you are welcome to order a copy by writing to livia.mariano at gianninispa.it<mailto:livia.mariano at gianninispa.it> and copying me on the email, starting next month.
Alternatively, if you happen to be in Naples, please let me know and I would be happy to hand you a copy in person.
Yours sincerely,
Florinda
