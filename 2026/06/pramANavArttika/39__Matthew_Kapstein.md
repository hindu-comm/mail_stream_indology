Dear friends,

Can anyone direct me to a verse index of the Tibetan translation of the PV, or else to a searchable Tibetan e-text that includes verse numbers? I am aware of several Sanskrit editions that include verse indices, as well as Miyasaka's useful Tibetan-Sanskrit and Sanskrit-Tibetan word indices, published, like his edition of the PV in Acta Indologica. So there is no need to call these materials to my attention here. I am looking for an easy means to locate, in the text of the PV, the many citations from it that one finds in Tibetan authors.

with thanks in advance,
