So happy to finally have in my hands both editions of my Krishna poetry.
The two volume edition contains 2700 of my Sanskrit verses with my Marathi
verse rendering, and it is published by the Bhandarkar Institute in Pune.
The three volume edition contains the same 2700 Sanskrit verses with my
English and Hindi translations. This edition is published from New Delhi by
the Central Sanskrit University and Motilal Banarsidass Publishers. Now
hoping that this poetry will reach some readers. As Bhavabhuti says: कालो
ह्ययं निरवधिर्विपुला च पृथ्वी. [image: 😀][image: 😀][image: 😀][image:
image.png]
